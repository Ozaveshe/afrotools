Calculation methodology

The calculator adds or extracts 15% standard VAT and rounds to SCR 0.01. A zero-rated result requires an exact current Second Schedule item and evidence; an exempt result separately requires an exact current First Schedule item and evidence. Registration screening compares past and expected taxable supplies with the SCR 2,000,000 compulsory and SCR 100,000 voluntary thresholds without asserting automatic approval.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Only 15% standard VAT and exact-evidence First or Second Schedule treatments are modeled.
  • The registration screen does not decide application timing, approval, cancellation, filing frequency or Commissioner discretion.
  • Input-tax deduction, refunds, invoice compliance, imports and other special regimes are not calculated.
  • Confirm filing, registration, and remittance duties with the official authority or a qualified adviser before submission.

Test-case examples

Input: Net amount: 10,000 SCR at the standard rate.
Expected: VAT is 1,500 SCR and gross total is 11,500 SCR.
Why: Confirms the current standard-rate path.

Input: A zero-rated or exempt treatment without the exact current Schedule evidence.
Expected: Calculation is blocked and no special-treatment result is shown.
Why: Prevents blanket zero-rating or exemption claims.

Input: Past or expected twelve-month taxable supplies of 2,000,000 SCR.
Expected: The compulsory threshold is marked as met, with an instruction to confirm timing and apply to SRC.
Why: Checks the Fourth Schedule compulsory threshold without asserting automatic registration.
