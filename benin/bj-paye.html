How the estimate is built

  1. Apply the monthly marginal ITS bands to taxable gross remuneration.
  2. Add the ORTB levy only for the selected month.
  3. Calculate employee CNSS at 3.6% separately.
  4. Subtract ITS, the selected levy and employee CNSS from gross pay.
  5. Show employer family, pension and selected occupational-risk contributions separately.

The annual summary assumes the same taxable gross every month and includes both scheduled ORTB levies.

Calculation methodology

The calculator applies the five monthly marginal ITS bands directly to taxable gross remuneration, adds the month-specific ORTB levy, calculates employee CNSS at 3.6% separately, and shows employer family, pension and occupational-risk contributions separately.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Benefits in kind, exceptional remuneration, exemptions, variable monthly income and contract deductions are not modeled.
  • The annual view assumes the same taxable gross salary in every month.
  • Confirm filing, registration, and remittance duties with the official authority or a qualified adviser before submission.
