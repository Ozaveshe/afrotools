            1. A written agreement signed BEFORE the incident

            2. Deduction must not reduce salary below minimum wage


            What you can do:

            → Cite Labour Act s.7 in writing

            → File at State Ministry of Labour

What we do NOT store

          

            We do not store your phone number. Phone numbers are immediately hashed (one-way SHA-256) — meaning we can count unique users but can never recover or share any phone number. We store only: intent type (e.g. "paye"), country mentioned, response time. No names, no contacts, no message content beyond the current conversation window.
