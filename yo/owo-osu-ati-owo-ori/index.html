Akopọ

Akopọ, formula ati ìgbesẹ tó tẹ̀le yóò hàn níbí.

Ewu ati ìgbesẹ tó tẹ̀le

  • Ṣayẹwo gross pay, taxable allowance ati deduction ṣáájú payroll run.
  • Ṣayẹwo oṣuwọn PAYE, pension ati NHF pẹlu orísun osise ṣáájú remittance.
  • Pa payslip, payroll register ati ẹ̀rí remittance mọ́ fun audit.
Ìkìlọ̀: iṣiro yìí jẹ́ àṣàro fun ìṣètò payroll. Kì í ṣe ìmòràn owó-orí, ìmòràn òfin, filing, remittance tàbí ìjẹ́risi compliance.

Ọ̀nà tó jọmọ

Naijiria ni YorùbáỌ̀nà iṣẹ́ fun Naira, PAYE, VAT, NIN, BVN ati PDF. VAT àti owó-orí iṣẹ́TIN, FIRS/NRS, invoice ati ìkọ̀wé ìṣòwò. Ìwé àti PDFṢeto payslip, invoice, receipt ati PDF. Àwọn irinṣẹ YorùbáWa gbogbo oju Yorùbá tó yẹ fún iṣẹ́ rẹ.

Formula, àṣàro ati orísun

Bí a ṣe ń ṣe iṣiro

  • Annual gross = gross pay loṣooṣu × 12, bí akoko rẹ bá jẹ́ oṣù.
  • Taxable income = annual gross - pension osise - NHF - allowance tí kò jẹ́ taxable - relief.
  • PAYE = bands legacy tàbí oṣuwọn ọwọ́ × taxable income.
  • Net pay = gross - pension osise - NHF - PAYE - ìyọkúrò míì.
  • Employer cost = gross + pension agbanisiṣẹ.
