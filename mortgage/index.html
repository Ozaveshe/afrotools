African property markets operate differently from Western ones. Stamp duty varies from 1% in Rwanda to 15% in Lagos State. Transfer costs, legal fees, and mortgage interest rates are country-specific. Our tools use real local data.

Buying property in Africa involves navigating unique challenges: varying land title systems, country-specific stamp duty rates, mortgage interest rates that differ dramatically between nations, and transfer fee structures that change with each budget. AfroTools provides free mortgage repayment calculators, stamp duty estimators, property transfer cost breakdowns, and affordability indices tailored to each of the 54 African countries. Whether you are purchasing your first home in Lagos, investing in Nairobi, or comparing property costs across the continent, our tools give you accurate, up-to-date numbers sourced from each country's land registry and revenue authority.
