ERS: Eswatini VAT overview

ERS: VAT Act 2011 and current amendment schedule

ERS says VAT applies to most goods and services at 15%. Section 24 of the Act ties 0% to the Second Schedule; ERS currently lists the 2022 amendment and Legal Notice 130 of 2025 as schedule updates.

Planning estimate only. Confirm classification, evidence, registration, invoice, return and payment requirements with ERS or a qualified adviser.

Calculation methodology

The pure engine adds 15% to a net amount or extracts the 15% tax fraction from a gross amount. The 0% path fails closed unless the user confirms an exact current Second Schedule match and supplies the matching evidence type. Exemptions, custom rates, registration, withholding and filing are not inferred.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Zero-rating requires an exact current Second Schedule match and retained transaction evidence.
  • Exempt supplies are not treated as zero-rated and are not calculated.
  • Confirm classification, evidence, registration, invoicing, filing and payment with ERS or a qualified adviser.
