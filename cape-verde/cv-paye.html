How this Cape Verde payroll estimate works

The calculator applies the official monthly Category A withholding formula directly to gross monthly employment income. It does not substitute the annual assessment bands or deduct INPS before applying the formula.

  • Standard employee INPS: 8.5% employee and 16% employer.
  • Domestic service INPS: 8% employee and 15% employer.
  • Special payments: holiday, Christmas and productivity payments can require autonomous withholding and are outside this simple monthly scenario.

The 2026 State Budget was checked for an employee-withholding amendment; none was identified. The 2018 formula preserved in the official 2021 code remains the basis of this estimate as checked on 21 July 2026.

Calculation methodology

The calculator applies the official Category A employee-withholding formula directly to gross monthly employment income. Income below CVE 36,607 has no withholding; the three formulas above the threshold use 14%, 21%, and 25% rates with their published subtraction constants and a CVE 100 minimum positive withholding. INPS is calculated separately and does not reduce the IRPS base. Standard employment uses 8.5% employee and 16% employer INPS; domestic service uses 8% and 15%.
