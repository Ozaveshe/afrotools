Buyer questions

Fast answers for teams deciding whether this should be a widget, sponsored placement, custom build, or API pilot.

What school workflows work well as calculators?

Admissions scores, tuition planning, scholarship readiness, school fees, student budgets, grade tracking, and study planning are strong starting points.

Can a calculator promise admission or scholarships?

No. It should support planning and readiness while avoiding admission approval, scholarship guarantee, grade certification, or endorsement claims unless formally reviewed.

What is the smallest school pilot?

One audience, one workflow, one market, and one honest next step such as apply, enquire, download a checklist, join a webinar, or request counselling.

Can AfroTools support sponsored education placements?

Yes. Education sponsorships can route through the media kit or sponsored tools path with clear labels and claim-safe copy.
