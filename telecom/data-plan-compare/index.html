How is price per GB calculated?
Price per GB is calculated by dividing the bundle price by the data volume in gigabytes. For example, a 10 GB bundle costing 3,000 NGN gives a price of 300 NGN per GB. Lower price per GB means better value for your money.
Are these prices up to date?
Prices are sourced from official operator websites and updated regularly. However, operators may run flash promotions not yet reflected here. Always confirm on the operator's app or USSD menu before purchasing.
Should I always buy the cheapest per-GB plan?
Not necessarily. Consider your actual usage pattern. If you only need data for one day, a daily bundle may be better even at a higher per-GB cost than committing to a monthly plan you will not fully use. Match the validity period to your needs.
What about night and social media bundles?
Many operators offer cheaper night-only or social-media-only bundles. These are not listed here because they are restricted. This comparator focuses on general-purpose data bundles usable at any time for any service.
