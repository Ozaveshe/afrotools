AfroBooks Finance OS

A Pro finance workflow shell for African SMEs, freelancers, accountants, schools, clinics, NGOs, and small importers. It tracks local demo books, invoices, expenses, payroll journals, receivables, payables, and accountant handoff without claiming bank sync or live accounting integrations.
