Trust rules

Compliance boundaries

  • Engine-backed previews still need human review before payroll sign-off.
  • Compliance packs are evidence and checklist exports, not statutory filing.
  • Payment files are handoff drafts. AfroTools does not move money.
  • Salary drafts stay on this device until the user explicitly saves them to an account.
