Calculation methodology

The shared local engine adds or extracts the RSL 15% standard rate and rounds to the nearest lisente. The 10% electricity path and 0% Fourth Schedule or export path require explicit current evidence. Exemptions stay separate. Conflicting public RSL registration thresholds are disclosed and not calculated.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Electricity, Fourth Schedule, export and exemption classification require transaction-level evidence.
  • RSL public registration surfaces currently show conflicting general thresholds; confirm the operative threshold directly.
  • Sector-specific, regional, treaty, relief, and special-regime rules may not be fully modeled.
  • Confirm filing, registration, and remittance duties with the official authority or a qualified adviser before submission.

Test-case examples

Expected: VAT is LSL 150 and total is LSL 1,150.
Why: Confirms the core VAT-exclusive calculation path and makes the rate dependency auditable.

Input: VAT-inclusive amount LSL 1,150 at 15%.
Expected: Net amount is LSL 1,000 and VAT is LSL 150.
Why: Confirms reverse VAT handling for invoices that already include tax.
