Formula and assumptions

Total cost = buy price × quantity + buy fee
Net proceeds = sell price × quantity − sell fee
Net P/L = net proceeds − total cost
ROI = net P/L ÷ total cost

A percentage fee is calculated from that side of the trade. A flat fee is entered in the same display currency. Break-even is the sell price where net proceeds exactly equal total acquisition cost.

Source, privacy and limits

  • This is planning arithmetic, not financial, tax or trading advice.
