How cost coverage works

A lot with no acquisition cost can still contribute to current value, but it cannot contribute to P/L. Coverage shows what share of current value has a known cost.

What happens when data is unavailable?

The worksheet shows no estimated, stale, converted or zero substitute values. If a held asset is missing from the fresh snapshot, every dependent total is withheld.

Is this a transaction or tax ledger?

No. It does not model disposals, historical exchange rates, taxable events or jurisdiction-specific cost-basis rules. Use exchange records and a qualified adviser for reporting.

Where do prices come from?

The AfroTools crypto-prices endpoint validates CoinGecko rows and supports NGN and ZAR only. Each accepted row must be no older than 30 minutes.
