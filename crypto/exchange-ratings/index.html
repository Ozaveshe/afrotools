Due-diligence workbook questions

Does this workbook rate or recommend an exchange?

No. It counts documented and unresolved evidence items only. It does not produce a trust score, ranking, safety verdict, licensing verdict or recommendation.

Does AfroTools verify the sources or receive my notes?

No. You enter and assess the sources yourself. The workbook and exports run locally in your browser without submission or browser storage.

What should I verify before depositing funds?

Check the provider domain, intended-country availability, regulator register, current fee schedule, deposit and withdrawal rails, custody and withdrawal policy, security disclosures, support path and a small withdrawal test.
