Result

Your revenue, pool fee, energy, operating result, break-even price and conditional simple hardware payback will appear here.

Method: revenue = output × price. Pool fee is deducted once. Energy = kW × hours × kWh cost. Net = revenue − pool fee − energy − other cost. Break-even excludes hardware. Simple payback requires positive hardware cost and daily net.

Evidence you control

Local and private

Planning boundary

This is operating arithmetic, not financial advice. It does not model changing network conditions, outages, hardware failure, depreciation, tax or resale value.

Questions about the worksheet

Does it fetch coin prices or network data?

No. Enter the gross daily output and price from evidence you trust. That keeps the reliable calculation available even when external services fail.

Why might simple hardware payback be absent?

It is only mathematically meaningful here when hardware cost and daily net operating result are both positive. It does not promise that future days will repeat.

What is excluded?

Any item you do not enter, including tax, depreciation, financing, repair, downtime, changing output and coin-price movement.
