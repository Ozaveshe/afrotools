Receipt comparison

—

The highest recipient amount is not a provider recommendation. This worksheet cannot verify that a quote remains executable or assess safety, identity checks, limits, reversibility, delivery reliability or suitability. Crypto transfers can be irreversible.

Exact comparison rule

Two entries are comparable only when their send currency, receive currency and total debit match exactly. Explicitly expired entries are excluded.

effective rate = recipient amount ÷ total debit

Expiry and evidence

Copy values from the final quote or confirmation screen and record when you checked it. An unknown expiry is allowed but carries a warning; it does not mean the quote is current.

Total debit, not headline fee

Use the full amount that would leave you. A separately stated fee is recorded for context but is not added again. Payout method and delivery time are descriptive and never change the arithmetic.

Privacy and exports

Calculation, CSV, JSON and parser-readable PDF generation happen on this device. Use generic route labels and never enter personal recipient, account, wallet or login information.

Remittance quote comparison questions

Why was an entry excluded?

It was explicitly expired, or no other eligible entry had the exact same send currency, receive currency and total debit.

Can I compare a bank transfer with a crypto route?

Yes, only as user-entered receipts with the same currencies and total debit. The arithmetic does not evaluate custody, reversibility, KYC, cash access, network risk or legal availability.

Why does the tool avoid a provider ranking?

Provider prices, eligibility and availability change. The tool reports only the highest recipient amount among the eligible comparable entries you supplied.
