Method and limitations

UTC schedule

Daily, weekly and two-week schedules use fixed UTC-day intervals. Monthly schedules keep the chosen calendar day and clamp to month-end when needed.

No look-ahead

Each event uses the latest unused CoinGecko reference point at or before the end of that UTC date, only when the gap is 36 hours or less.

Gross-outlay comparison

Percentage and fixed costs reduce acquisition cash. Modeled change compares value at the last source price with the gross cash actually used on accepted events.

  • Historical daily reference prices are not exchange execution quotes and can differ from a platform’s price, spread or timing.
  • No stale, synthetic or converted price is inserted when the provider is unavailable or an event lacks an eligible point.
  • This replay does not include tax, custody, platform availability or costs you did not enter. It does not predict returns or recommend an asset.
