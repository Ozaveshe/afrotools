Frequently asked questions

Does this worksheet load live crypto or platform prices?

No. It makes no provider, exchange, venue or rate request. You must enter two executable receipts you checked directly.

Why are all-in debit and credit required?

Headline rates omit fees and quantity effects. All-in receipt totals for the exact same asset amount make the two legs arithmetically comparable.

What if an expiry is unknown or already passed?

An expired receipt blocks the result and exports. An unknown expiry allows calculation only with a warning to recheck both receipts.

Is a positive modeled difference a recommendation?

No. It does not verify liquidity, settlement, counterparties, withdrawals, eligibility, platform terms, tax or suitability. It is planning arithmetic, not financial advice.
