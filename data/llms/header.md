> AfroTools provides practical AI routing, calculators, documents, country guides, data tools, and exports for African workflows.

AfroTools connects an AI routing layer to existing calculators, document builders, checklists, country pages, datasets, widgets, and exports. The AI layer helps select and prefill workflows; it does not replace the underlying tools, source checks, or professional judgment.

Many calculators and document workflows run in the browser. Data-driven pages show source, freshness, assumptions, confidence, or fallback status where available. Tax, legal, immigration, customs, medical, financial, and engineering outputs are planning aids and should be verified with the relevant authority or a qualified professional.
