# AfroTools 50K Outreach Batches

Updated: 2026-05-05

These batches are execution queues, not lists of real companies. Add real prospects only after manual sourcing. Do not invent contacts.

## Batch 1: 50 Highest-Fit Widget and Backlink Prospects

Goal: win free widget installs, backlinks, and the first Widget Pro conversations.

Primary route: `/widgets/`

Secondary route: `/widgets/demo/`

Segment split:

| Segment | Count | Fit signal |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Business media and publishers | 15 | Resource pages, SME guides, calculators, or newsletter content |
| Accounting and tax firms | 10 | VAT, PAYE, invoice, tax-calendar, or SME tax guides |
| HR/payroll providers | 10 | Payroll education, employer guides, salary calculators, hiring cost content |
| Schools and edtechs | 10 | Admissions, fees, grades, scholarship, student budget, or parent resources |
| Employer-service and trade targets | 5 | Immigration, recruitment, customs, trade, or SME support pages |

Sourcing instructions:

- Search for existing content pages where a calculator would make the page more useful.
- Prioritize pages that already link out to useful resources.
- Start with a free attributed widget, not a paid ask.
- Move to Widget Pro only when the buyer asks for branding, setup, analytics, lead capture, or attribution terms.

Qualification note:

- High priority requires a live URL where one AfroTools widget could be placed.

## Batch 2: 50 HR/Payroll/API Pilot Prospects

Goal: sell HR/payroll Widget Pro, custom payroll calculators, and API pilots.

Primary routes: `/for-hr-payroll/`, `/api/pricing.html`

Secondary routes: `/widgets/`, `/custom-calculators/`

Segment split:

| Segment | Count | Fit signal |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| HR/payroll providers | 35 | Payroll software, HR platform, payroll bureau, employer of record, payslip or PAYE content |
| Employer-service targets | 10 | Recruitment, benefits, employer services, hiring guides, country hiring content |
| Accounting and tax firms | 5 | Payroll advisory or employer tax content |

Sourcing instructions:

- Prioritize teams with employer cost, payroll education, or multi-country payroll content.
- Send `/for-hr-payroll/` to commercial buyers.
- Send `/api/pricing.html` only when the prospect has a product or developer use case.
- Do not promise official payroll filing, compliance certification, or full country coverage.

Qualification note:

- High priority requires either a visible payroll workflow or a product that could call payroll, PAYE, VAT, or country data.

## Batch 3: 50 Sponsored/Media Prospects

Goal: open sponsored tool, media-kit, and category/country placement conversations.

Primary routes: `/sponsored-tools/`, `/media-kit/`

Secondary route: `/custom-calculators/`

Segment split:

| Segment | Count | Fit signal |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Business media and publishers | 30 | Advertise page, media kit, newsletter sponsorships, or SME audience |
| Fintechs | 10 | Consumer finance, remittance, savings, merchant, SME lending, or financial education |
| Schools and edtechs | 5 | Admissions, scholarship, student finance, education advertising |
| Employer-service and trade targets | 5 | Recruitment, immigration, customs, trade finance, SME service ads |

Sourcing instructions:

- Prioritize buyers that already buy audience, newsletter, or content placements.
- Match each prospect to one tool, category, country, or audience.
- Keep sponsor copy clearly labeled.
- Never imply sponsors can alter independent formulas, source notes, or calculator outputs.

Qualification note:

- High priority requires a clear audience match and a sponsor CTA that helps the user after calculation.

## Batch 4: 50 Custom Calculator Prospects

Goal: sell scope sprints and MVP calculator builds.

Primary route: `/custom-calculators/`

Secondary routes: `/for-accountants/`, `/for-fintechs/`, `/for-hr-payroll/`, `/for-schools/`

Segment split:

| Segment | Count | Fit signal |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Fintechs | 15 | Fee, loan, savings, remittance, affordability, merchant pricing, or FX workflows |
| Accounting and tax firms | 15 | VAT, PAYE, invoice, tax calendar, advisory readiness, import duty workflows |
| HR/payroll providers | 10 | Employer cost, payroll readiness, payslip, leave, severance, hiring cost workflows |
| Schools and edtechs | 5 | Fee estimator, admissions score, scholarship fit, student budget workflows |
| Employer-service and trade targets | 5 | Immigration, work permit, customs, landed cost, trade finance workflows |

Sourcing instructions:

- Look for repeated customer questions with numbers, rules, eligibility, pricing, or documents.
- Pitch a scope sprint first when the workflow is complex.
- Pitch an MVP build only after the prospect names the target workflow.
- Include source notes and compliance limits in every custom calculator conversation.

Qualification note:

- High priority requires a proprietary or brand-specific workflow that an existing free widget cannot fully solve.

| --- | ---: | --- |

| Edtechs | 15 | Admissions, tutoring, scholarship, grade, school fees, student budget products |
| Scholarship and education publishers | 10 | Scholarship portals, exam resources, student finance content |

- High priority requires a visible student, parent, admissions, fee, or scholarship workflow.

## Batch 6: 50 Mixed Follow-Up and Reserve Prospects

Goal: keep the sprint moving without lowering quality.

Primary route: match the use case.

Segment split:

| Segment | Count | Fit signal |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Accounting and tax firms | 10 | High-fit rows sourced after Batch 1 or 4 fills |
| HR/payroll providers | 10 | Late-discovered payroll and employer service targets |
| Fintechs | 10 | Technical or sponsored use cases that need more research |
| Schools and edtechs | 10 | Education prospects found through referrals or search |
| Business media and publishers | 5 | Publisher or newsletter targets not ready for Batch 1 or 3 |
| Employer-service and trade targets | 5 | Trade, customs, immigration, recruitment, and SME service targets |

Sourcing instructions:

- Use this batch to replace bounces, weak fits, and no-contact rows.
- Add referrals and second contacts from qualified replies.
- Do not use reserve prospects to avoid follow-ups.
- If a reserve prospect is high-fit, send the matching batch template and log it under the actual offer fit.

Qualification note:

- High priority requires a clearer use case than the row it replaces.
