Sources & verification for Document & PDF

Do the PDF tools upload my files?

Core PDF actions are designed to run in the browser, so source files stay on this device unless a tool clearly explains a server-backed feature.

What is the methodology?

Use the planner or route cards first. Choose pack work, sending, publishing, review, business documents, or career documents, then open the matching tool and verify its output before export.

What should I verify before exporting?

Check document order, page ranges, passwords, visible redactions, page numbers, signatures, client details, dates, totals, and any external filing or legal requirement before using the export as a final record.

What are the limitations?

This hub is not official filing, legal advice, tax advice, or document certification. PDF-category exports use the shared account gate for continuity and workspace metadata. The gate should not upload source PDFs, generated files, passwords, OCR text, or document contents.
