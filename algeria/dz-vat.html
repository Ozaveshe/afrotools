What this tool will not guess

  • DGI currently lists a 19% normal rate and 9% reduced rate.
  • The 9% rate applies only to operations enumerated in article 23 of the TCA Code.
  • Exemptions are enumerated in articles 8–13; an industry label alone is not enough.
  • DGI states IFU taxpayers are not concerned by VAT and invoice TTC; confirm your regime.
  • Displayed DZD amounts are rounded to two decimal places after each result.

Calculation methodology

The shared local engine adds or extracts the 19% normal rate, or the 9% reduced rate only after the user confirms an operation listed in article 23 of the TCA Code. Exemption treatment under articles 8-13 is confirmation-only. The regime screen distinguishes real or simplified VAT treatment from IFU taxpayers, whom DGI says are not concerned by VAT and invoice TTC.

Known limitations

  • The tool does not classify a product, service or taxpayer automatically.
  • No generic numeric VAT-registration threshold is supplied; the taxpayer's current DGI regime must be confirmed.
  • Exempt and reduced-rate treatment requires the exact current TCA provision.
  • Input-tax recovery, franchise, filing, payment and refund conditions are outside the calculation.
