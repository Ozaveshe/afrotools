FAQ

Gabon VAT — Common Questions

What is the standard VAT rate in Gabon?
The standard VAT rate in Gabon is 18% as of 2026. This is levied on most goods and services sold within Gabon.
How do I add VAT to a price in Gabon?
Multiply the pre-tax price by 1.1800 (i.e., price × (1 + 0.18)). Example: FCFA10,000 × 1.1800 = FCFA11,800 including VAT.
How do I remove VAT from a price in Gabon?
Divide the VAT-inclusive price by 1.1800. Example: FCFA11,800 ÷ 1.1800 = FCFA10,000 pre-tax.
What goods are VAT exempt in Gabon?
VAT-exempt goods and services in Gabon include: Food staples, Medical services, Agricultural inputs, Educational materials. Zero-rated (taxable at 0%) include: Export goods, Export services.
