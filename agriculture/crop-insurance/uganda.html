Farming context: Uganda

In Uganda, agriculture contributes about 24% of GDP, employs roughly 72% of the labour force, the average farm is around 0.8 ha — so plan budgets, wages, and repayments around smallholder cash-flow cycles.

Agriculture share of GDP~24%
Arable land9,000,000 ha
Irrigated share~0.5%
Main rainy seasonMarch to November
