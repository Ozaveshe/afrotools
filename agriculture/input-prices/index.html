Why Input Prices Matter

💰

60–70% of Cost = Inputs

Seeds, fertilizer, and chemicals account for the majority of crop production costs. Even small savings compound across a season.

🏷️

Brand vs. Generic

Premium brands cost 2–3× more than generics. Our quality notes help you decide when the premium is worth paying.

🏛️

Government Subsidies

Most African governments subsidize fertilizers by 25–50%. Know what's available in your country and how to access it.
