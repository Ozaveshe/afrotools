Frequently Asked Questions

Are these calculators free?

Yes, all AfroTools agriculture calculators are completely free to use — no registration required.

How accurate are the calculations?

The tools use country-specific agronomic data, soil types, and regional climate data based on FAOSTAT and CGIAR research. Results are indicative — always consult your local extension officer for site-specific advice.

Which countries are supported?

All 54 African Union member states are supported, each with country-specific crop data, fertilizer products, regional zones, and pricing.

What crops are covered?

Over 60 crop varieties including cereals (maize, rice, sorghum, millet, wheat), roots and tubers (cassava, yam, potato, sweet potato), pulses, cash crops (coffee, cocoa, cotton), fruits, and vegetables — with local names in relevant languages.
