What Changed
Key 2025/26 Facts

📋 For Employees

  • Tax brackets unchanged from 2024/25 — no inflation adjustment
  • Retirement fund deductions: up to 27.5% of income (max R350,000/yr)
  • Medical scheme credits: R364/month per main member
  • Primary rebate: R17,235 (no tax below R95,750 income)
  • Travel allowance: deemed rates per km for business use
  • Work-from-home deductions (if dedicated office space)

🏢 For Employers

  • UIF employer match: 1% of remuneration (cap R17,712/mo)
  • SDL (Skills Development Levy): 1% of total payroll
  • PAYE remittance: 7th of following month
  • EMP201 filing: monthly submission to SARS
  • IRP5/IT3(a) certificates: issued to employees annually
  • ETI (Employment Tax Incentive) for qualifying young workers

⚠️ Compliance Deadlines

  • PAYE/UIF/SDL: 7th of following month
  • Tax year: 1 March – 28/29 February
  • Tax season (eFiling): opens July, closes October
  • Provisional taxpayers: January deadline
  • P9 reconciliation: bi-annual (August + February)
  • Late payment: 10% penalty + interest at prescribed rate
