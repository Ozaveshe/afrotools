Method and boundary

The planner capitalizes grace-period interest only when you select that contract term, then calculates a reducing-balance fixed payment. Extra payments shorten repayment. Monthly fees are added to cash payments, not silently financed. It does not model changing rates, income-contingent deductions, waivers, penalties or forgiveness.

Official programme context reviewed 22 July 2026

  • HELB undergraduate loans currently describe a 4% rate and KSh 1,000 annual ledger fee for that product; neither is prefilled here.
  • NSFAS student-loan information and repayment channels require scheme-specific checking; no zero-rate preset is supplied.
  • TETFund’s mandate is institutional intervention funding, so it is not represented as an individual student loan.

Privacy

No input is stored, put in a URL, or sent to analytics, AI or an API. CSV, JSON and PDF are created locally.

Unverified names

No SOBA preset or claim is included because an official student-loan programme under that name could not be identified. Use the exact provider name from your statement.
