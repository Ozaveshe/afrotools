Event promotion in Africa relies heavily on eye-catching flyers shared via WhatsApp, Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook. Whether you're organizing a church event, party, wedding celebration, business conference, or community gathering, a professional flyer is essential. Design Tips

Keep your flyer simple and focused. Use large, bold text for the event name — it should be readable even as a small WhatsApp thumbnail. Include the essentials: what (event name), when (date and time), where (venue), and how (contact/tickets). Use high-contrast colours and limit your palette to 2-3 colours for a professional look.

Social Media Sizes

This tool supports multiple sizes: Instagram post (1080x1350), Instagram/WhatsApp Story (1080x1920), Square (1080x1080), and A4 print (2480x3508). For maximum reach, create versions in multiple sizes. Instagram stories have the highest engagement for event promotion, while square formats work well across all platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size should my flyer be?

For social media sharing (WhatsApp, Instagram), use 1080x1350 or 1080x1920. For print, use A4 (2480x3508 at 300 DPI). Square (1080x1080) works across all platforms. This tool supports all these sizes.

Can I upload my own background?

Yes. Use the "Background" file upload to add your own image. It will be scaled to fill the canvas. Add text overlays on top of your custom background for a professional look.

Is the output high resolution?

Yes. The canvas renders at the exact pixel dimensions shown (e.g., 1080x1350 for Instagram). The A4 option renders at 2480x3508 pixels, suitable for high-quality printing at 300 DPI.
