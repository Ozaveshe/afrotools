FAQ

Common questions

How is CreatorPolish different from Grammarly?

Grammarly flags African English as errors. CreatorPolish understands Nigerian, South African, Kenyan, and Ghanaian English natively. It fixes actual mistakes without stripping your voice or local expressions.

Does CreatorPolish store my text?

No. Your text is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. Session history is kept locally in your browser only — you can clear it anytime.

Which English variants does it support?

Standard English, Nigerian English, South African English, Kenyan English, Ghanaian English, and Pan-African Neutral. Each preserves local idioms and spelling conventions.

How many polishes can I do per day?

Free users get 15 AI-powered polishes per day. Readability stats and local history are unlimited. Limits reset at midnight.

Does it show inline suggestions?

Yes. Green for additions, red strikethrough for removals, yellow for word changes. Accept individually or all at once.

Can I export the polished text?

Yes. Copy to clipboard with one click, or download as a .txt file. Review the diff before accepting changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should a polish pass fix?

Clarity, grammar, unsupported claims, tone, CTA, local idioms, and unnecessary filler.

Should every caption sound polished?

No. Keep natural voice, but remove confusion and claims you cannot support.

What should creators check before posting?

Audience, promise, proof, CTA, brand safety, spelling, links, and platform length.
