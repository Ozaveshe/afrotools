Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tracker fetch live prices?
No. Enter the latest price from a source you trust. The result is a local point-in-time calculation and does not update automatically.
Can I mix currencies?
No. Every row must use the single portfolio currency selected above. Convert holdings first if you need one combined view.
Are dividends and trading fees included?
No. The gain or loss is price-only: shares multiplied by the difference between current price and buy price. Account for dividends, taxes and fees separately.
