Mobile Money vs Bank Transfer FAQ

Where do the fee values come from?

They come from you. The calculator no longer prefills provider tariffs because those can change by amount, channel, country and customer tier.

What does effective fee rate mean?

It is the total entered fee divided by the amount sent, expressed as a percentage. It helps compare a flat fee with a percentage fee.

Does the result include every possible charge?

No. It includes only the flat and percentage fees entered. Add or separately compare cash-out, tax, recipient and account charges shown in the provider quote.

Which method is cheapest?
There is no permanent winner. Use each provider's current quote for the exact amount, then include any cash-out or recipient charge that is outside the send fee.
Does country selection load live fees?
No. Country selection supplies a currency label and neutral method names only. It does not contact a bank, wallet or price service.
Can I compare two named providers?
Yes. Treat the Mobile Money and Bank Transfer columns as the two quotes you are comparing, and enter the current charges shown by those providers.
