Professional Indemnity Calculator — What You Need to Know

Estimate professional indemnity insurance premiums for African professionals — lawyers, doctors, accountants, engineers, consultants. Free calculator. Use this free calculator to get a quick estimate before contacting insurance providers.

Professional indemnity insurance protects against claims of negligence or inadequate work. It is mandatory for certain professions in many African countries including lawyers, accountants, architects, doctors, and insurance brokers. Premium rates range from 0.5% to 4% of annual professional fees depending on profession, claims history, and coverage limit.

Disclaimer: These are estimates based on market data and published rates. Actual premiums depend on individual circumstances, insurer assessment, and negotiation. Always get formal quotes from licensed providers.
