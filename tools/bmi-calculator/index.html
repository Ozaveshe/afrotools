What is BMI?
Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measure using height and weight to estimate body fat. Formula: weight(kg) / height(m)². While useful for screening, BMI doesn't distinguish between muscle and fat.
What is BMR & TDEE?
BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the calories your body burns at complete rest. TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) factors in your activity level. Eat below TDEE to lose weight, above to gain.
Health Tips
• Aim for 150+ min of moderate exercise/week
• Eat whole foods, fruits, vegetables
• Drink 2-3 liters of water daily
• Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep
• Monitor waist circumference regularly
• Consult a doctor before major diet changes
