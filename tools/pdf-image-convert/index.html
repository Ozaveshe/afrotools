Free PDF to Image and Image to PDF Converter

The AfroTools PDF Image Converter is a free, browser-based tool that lets you convert between PDF documents and images without uploading your files to any server. Export selected PDF pages as high-quality PNG, JPG, or WebP images, or combine multiple photos and scans into a single PDF document.

PDF to Image Conversion

Upload a PDF and convert every page, or only the page ranges you need, into crisp images. Choose PNG for lossless text and screenshots, JPG for broad compatibility and smaller files, or WebP for compact modern image output. Resolution controls range from quick 72 DPI exports to high-detail 288 DPI output.

Image to PDF Conversion

Combine multiple images into a single, professionally formatted PDF document. Upload PNG, JPG, or WebP images, arrange them in your preferred order, and choose page size, orientation, margins, fit mode, output filename, and image compression behavior.

Why Use This Tool?

  • 100% Private: All processing happens in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
  • Batch Processing: Convert all pages of a multi-page PDF at once, export page ranges, or combine dozens of images into one PDF.
  • High Quality: Render PDF pages at up to 4x scale for sharp, detailed images.
  • ZIP Download: Download all converted images as a single ZIP file for easy organization.
  • No Registration: No account needed. No limits. Just open and use.

Common Use Cases

  • Extract charts and diagrams from PDF reports for presentations
  • Convert scanned documents to individual image files
  • Create a PDF portfolio from a collection of photos
  • Turn presentation slides into shareable images
  • Combine receipts and invoices into a single PDF for filing

Methodology

  • Renders selected PDF pages to local image canvases at the chosen scale and packages multi-page exports as ZIP files.
