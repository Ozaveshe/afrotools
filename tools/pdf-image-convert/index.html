Free PDF to Image & Image to PDF Converter

The AfroTools PDF Image Converter is a free, browser-based tool that lets you convert between PDF documents and images without uploading your files to any server. Whether you need to extract pages from a PDF as high-quality PNG or JPG images, or combine multiple photos into a single PDF document, this tool handles it all instantly in your browser.

PDF to Image Conversion

Upload any PDF and convert each page into a crisp, high-resolution image. Choose between PNG format for lossless quality (perfect for text-heavy documents and screenshots) or JPG format for smaller file sizes (ideal for photos and presentations). The resolution scale option lets you control the output quality — 2x scale produces images at 144 DPI, suitable for most uses, while 3x scale gives you 216 DPI for print-quality output.

Image to PDF Conversion

Combine multiple images into a single, professionally formatted PDF document. Upload PNG, JPG, or WebP images, arrange them in your preferred order, and choose from standard page sizes including A4 and US Letter. The fit options let you control how images appear on each page — preserve aspect ratios, fill the entire page, or stretch to fit.

Why Use This Tool?

  • 100% Private: All processing happens in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
  • Batch Processing: Convert all pages of a multi-page PDF at once, or combine dozens of images into one PDF.
  • High Quality: Render PDF pages at up to 3x scale for sharp, detailed images.
  • ZIP Download: Download all converted images as a single ZIP file for easy organization.
  • No Registration: No account needed. No limits. Just open and use.
