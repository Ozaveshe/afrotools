Frequently Asked Questions

Why can my bill differ from this estimate?

Tariff bands, fixed charges, VAT, arrears, estimated billing, service fees, and meter debt recovery can change the final bill.

Should I use this for a landlord dispute?

Use it as a planning check only. For disputes, keep bills, meter photos, tokens, and official tariff notices.

What is the best input to use?

Use kWh from your meter or past bills, then enter the tariff printed by your utility or regulator.
