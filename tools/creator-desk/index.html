FAQ

Common Questions

Is my client data private?
Yes. Your client and project data is stored securely and is only visible to you. We never share or sell your data.
Can my team use CreatorDesk?
Currently CreatorDesk is designed for solo creators. Team collaboration features are on our roadmap.
Does it integrate with invoicing?
Yes. CreatorDesk connects with CreatorInvoice to link quotes and invoices directly to projects.
Can I use it offline?
Yes. Data is cached locally. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.
How many clients can I add?
No limits on clients or projects. Free users get all core features.
Is this free?
Yes. Pipeline, clients, and tasks are free. AI features have daily limits for free users, unlimited for Pro.
