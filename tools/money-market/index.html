Money Market Fund Comparator FAQ

Which MMF yield should I enter?

Enter an annual effective yield. If the provider quotes a different convention, convert it first or do not treat the comparison as equivalent.

Does the calculator include fees and tax?

No. Adjust rates consistently or review those deductions separately.

Does it use live rates?

No. Both figures are user-entered and should be checked against current product documents.

What is a money market fund?
It is a pooled fund that invests according to its mandate in short-term instruments. Holdings, credit risk, fees, redemption rules and capital protection vary by fund.
Which option is better?
The calculator only compares entered returns. Review access timing, guarantees, penalties, fees, tax, credit risk and rate variability before deciding.
Is a money market fund guaranteed?
Do not assume so. Check the current prospectus, factsheet and applicable investor-protection rules for the exact product.
