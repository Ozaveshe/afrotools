Final salary means annual pensionable salary, generally based on the final 24 months. Use the three service records after any savings-pot service reduction.

Rules used

  • Vested gratuity: 6.72% × final annual salary × vested service.
  • Savings gratuity: 3 × 7.75% for other members or 3 × 8.45% for services members.
  • Annual annuity: 1/55 on vested service plus 1.5/58.65 (other) or 1.5/60.71 (services) on retirement service, plus R360.
  • Standard early retirement: one-third of 1% reduction per month before age 60.
  • Members contribute 7.5%; employers contribute 13% or 16%.

GEPF says employer-authorised early retirement may avoid the reduction because the employer pays the additional liability.

What this estimate excludes

No resignation, retrenchment, savings withdrawal, home-loan eligibility, GEMS, UIF, PAYE, survivor amount, enhanced spouse option, pension increase or investment-growth projection is calculated. The calculator also fails closed below 10 years of vested service, where other benefit cases apply.

Official sources reviewed 23 July 2026

  • Government Gazette 51202 supplies the exact vested, savings and retirement-service factors.
  • GEPF retirement benefits gives the service boundary and one-third-of-1% monthly early-retirement reduction.
  • GEPF employer contributions gives 7.5%, 13%, 16% and the seventh-day remittance rule.
  • GEPF 2025 actuarial-factor notice is why this tool does not estimate resignation or other early exits.

How to enter service

Use the service values on your latest GEPF statement. For post-September 2024 service, savings service generally accrues as one-third and retirement service as two-thirds; a savings withdrawal reduces the relevant service record.

Privacy

No input is stored, placed in a URL or sent to analytics, AI or an API. Do not enter an ID, member number or beneficiary details. CSV, JSON and PDF are created locally.
