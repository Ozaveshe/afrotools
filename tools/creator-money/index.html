FAQ

Common Questions

Does it connect to my bank account?
Not yet. You manually log income and expenses. This keeps you intentional about every naira. Bank integration is on the roadmap.
Is my financial data private?
Completely. Your data is stored locally on your device first, then encrypted in Supabase if you create an account. We never share individual financial data.
Does it work offline?
Yes. All transactions are stored locally first and synced when you're back online. You can log expenses with no internet at all.
Which currencies are supported?
NGN, KES, ZAR, GHS, TZS, EGP, UGX, CFA (XOF/XAF), MAD, and USD. All amounts display in your chosen currency.
Can I export my data?
Yes. Monthly P&L and annual reports can be exported as PDF or CSV. Perfect for your accountant or visa applications.
Is this free?
The full dashboard, tracking, and reports are Core use without a paid subscription. The AI advisor has daily limits for free users.
