FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the fee data come from?
Our data is community-reported by parents and students. We verify submissions and update regularly. You can also contribute by using "Report a Fee".
How accurate are the fees?
Fees are approximate and based on the most recent reports. Schools update their fees annually, so always confirm directly with the school.
Which countries are covered?
We have the most data for Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, and Ghana. Other African countries are being added as the community grows.
Can I add a school that's not listed?
Yes! Use the "Report a Fee" form to add any school. After verification, it will appear in the comparator.
What costs are included?
We break down tuition, books/materials, uniform, transport, meals, and extra fees (PTA, development levies, etc.) where reported.
Is this free?
Completely free. Compare schools, save shortlists, and download PDFs at no cost.

Compare School Fees Across Africa

Choosing the right school for your child is one of the most important decisions a parent makes. In Africa, where education costs vary enormously between public, private, and international schools, having reliable fee data is essential. The AfroTools School Fees Comparator brings transparency to education costs across the continent.

Why Fee Comparison Matters

In Nigeria alone, annual school fees range from under N50,000 for public schools to over N5,000,000 for premium international schools. In Kenya, the gap between public and international education is equally wide. Without a central comparison tool, parents spend weeks calling schools, visiting websites, and asking friends for fee information.

Community-Powered Data

Unlike directory sites that rely on outdated information, our data comes directly from parents and students who report actual fees they've paid. Every submission is reviewed before publication. This community-driven approach ensures our data reflects real costs, not advertised prices.

Comprehensive Cost Breakdown

Tuition is just one part of education costs. Our comparator breaks down the true annual cost including books and materials, school uniforms, transport, meals, PTA levies, development fees, and extracurricular activities. No hidden costs, no surprises.

Save and Share

Save school shortlists to compare later, or download a professional PDF comparison to share with your spouse, family, or financial advisor. Making the right choice is easier when everyone has the same clear information.

Whether you're choosing a primary school in Lagos, a secondary school in Nairobi, or a university in Johannesburg, the AfroTools School Fees Comparator gives you the data you need to make an informed decision.
