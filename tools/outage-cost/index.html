      Sources & verification

      Last verified: 16 May 2026. Assumptions are user-entered and designed for African business outage planning, not a live utility tariff feed.

      Use your meter logs, generator fuel receipts, POS reports, wage sheet, inventory register, internet provider outage log, and local utility or energy regulator notices for final decisions.

      Methodology

      1. Revenue per hour is entered directly or derived from daily revenue divided by operating hours.
      2. Lost revenue is reduced by backup coverage and recoverable sales assumptions.
      3. Staff idle, backup running, spoilage, payment downtime, and recovery buffer are added to the incident cost.
      4. Break-even backup budget equals monthly outage exposure. A backup investment below that amount may pay back within about one month.
