    How to shortlist a business name responsibly

    Start with the brief

    A useful shortlist reflects what the business does, who it serves, the desired tone and the markets where the name must work. This tool combines those user-supplied signals through fixed patterns, so the same brief and set number produce the same ideas.

    Separate ideation from verification

    Readable does not mean registrable. Company-name rules, trademark conflicts, language associations, domains and social handles are different checks. Keep evidence from the relevant official registry and professional advisers when the decision matters.
