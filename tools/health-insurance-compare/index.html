Frequently Asked Questions

What should I compare besides premium?

Compare inpatient limit, exclusions, waiting periods, provider network, emergency cover, chronic care, and claim turnaround.

Can the cheapest plan be the best plan?

Not always. A low premium can come with small limits, narrow hospital access, or exclusions that matter for your household.

Is this tool private?

Yes. The quick score runs in the browser and does not send your health details to AfroTools.
