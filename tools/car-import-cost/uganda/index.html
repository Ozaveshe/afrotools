    Frequently asked questions

    How do I estimate the landed cost of importing a car into Uganda?

    Enter the purchase price, FOB or CIF value, or a make, model and year. The calculator estimates the customs value, applies the current Uganda duty and levy pack, then adds practical clearing, port, inland delivery and registration costs to show a real on-road budget.

    What charges are included in the Uganda car import estimate?

    The estimate separates official charges — customs duty, levies and administrative fees on the CIF basis — from practical costs such as port handling, clearing agent fees, demurrage risk, inland delivery and registration, so you can see where the money goes.

    Is the Uganda Car Landed Cost Calculator free and how accurate is it?

    Yes, it is completely free and works on any phone or computer. Accuracy depends on the values you enter; rules are stored as source-dated seed data and shown as assumptions rather than scraped from government pages at runtime, so confirm current rates with customs before you commit.
