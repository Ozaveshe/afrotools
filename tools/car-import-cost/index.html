African Car Landed Cost Calculator

Work out what a car really costs after source price, freight, insurance, official customs charges, clearing, port costs, inland delivery, registration, delay risk, financing, and resale margin.

Launch markets Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, Uganda, Zambia, and Tanzania Source markets: Japan, UAE, UK, South Africa, and local dealer.

How it works

The calculator normalizes your vehicle details, determines the customs value basis, checks age and steering rules, applies the active country rule pack, adds practical clearing and port costs, then produces best, normal, and painful-case scenarios.

  • Use purchase price, FOB, CIF, or make/model/year mode.
  • Compare Japan, UAE, UK, South Africa, and local dealer scenarios.
  • Save locally, save to account when signed in, export CSV/PDF, print, or share a URL.

Source verification

Rule packs are seed data, not runtime scrapers. Admins can update them through the maintenance screen when an official rule, valuation table, or port preset changes.
