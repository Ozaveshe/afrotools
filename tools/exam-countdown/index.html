The Exam Countdown Timer is purpose-built for African students preparing for major examinations including WAEC, NECO, JAMB, KCSE, and Matric. Set your exam date and the tool displays a live countdown in days, hours, minutes, and seconds, helping you stay focused and manage your revision schedule. You can track multiple exam dates simultaneously and the timer persists in your browser so it is always ready when you open the page. Teachers and parents also use it to help students visualise how much preparation time remains, turning an abstract deadline into a concrete, motivating number.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which exams are pre-loaded?
The tool includes 2026 dates for WAEC (May 11), NECO (June 29), JAMB UTME (April 11), KCSE (October 26), KCPE (October 26), and South Africa Matric (October 27). You can also add any custom exam date.
Does the countdown save when I close my browser?
Yes. Your active countdowns are saved to localStorage in your browser. They will still be there when you come back. No account or sign-up is needed.
Can I track multiple exams at the same time?
Yes. You can add as many countdowns as you want. Each one displays as a separate card with its own live ticking timer showing days, hours, minutes, and seconds.
How do I share a countdown on WhatsApp?
Each countdown card has a green WhatsApp share button. Click it to send a message with the exam name, date, and remaining time to any WhatsApp contact or group.
Are the exam dates accurate?
The pre-loaded dates reflect the officially announced or expected 2026 exam schedules at the time of publishing. Always verify with your exam body for the latest updates, as dates can change.
