Paystack fee planner questions

Is the amount below NGN 2,500 fee-free?

No. For a Nigerian local transaction below NGN 2,500, Paystack waives only the NGN 100 fixed component. The 1.5% percentage fee still applies.

Does the planner include tax on Paystack fees?

It adds 15% VAT only to South African fees because Paystack publishes those rates as VAT-exclusive. It does not infer tax for Nigeria, Ghana or Kenya.

Are payout and stamp-duty charges included?

No. Payout transfer fees, Nigerian transfer stamp duty, refunds, chargebacks, foreign exchange, custom pricing and settlement timing are excluded.

When were these Paystack rates checked?
