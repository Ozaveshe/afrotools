Profit margin calculator

Calculate gross, operating or net margin without tax, import-duty, FX, benchmark or pricing assumptions.

Formulas

Gross margin: (revenue − COGS) ÷ revenue × 100.

Operating margin: (revenue − COGS − operating expenses) ÷ revenue × 100.

Net margin: (revenue − all entered expenses) ÷ revenue × 100.

Markup: (revenue − COGS) ÷ COGS × 100; N/A when COGS is zero.

Example: revenue 150 and COGS 100 gives gross profit 50, gross margin 33.33%, and markup 50%. Losses produce negative margins.

Calculation boundary

Revenue must exceed zero. Every economically material expense is entered by the user. The optional unit label is display-only and no conversion occurs.
