What costs are usually missed on import budgets?
Freight, insurance, customs levies, VAT on a duty-inclusive base, port charges, and FX swings are the most common blind spots.
When should I use the calculator?
Use it before placing the order, again when freight is confirmed, and once more before final customs clearance.
