Why this exists

Freelancers need more than a printable receipt

Freelancers need to move quickly from agreed work to a clear bill: who is paying, what was delivered, what tax or withholding applies, how to pay, and what record to keep. This workspace keeps that path local-first: build the document, review the balance, copy the client handoff, then export the file you need.

Time, service, retainer, and expense line items. VAT, per-line tax, discount, withholding, paid amount, and balance due. Paystack, Flutterwave, PayPal, Wise, mobile money, and bank instructions. Saved drafts, saved clients, import, and multi-format export.
FAQ

Freelance invoice questions

Can I use this for retainers?

Yes. Add a retainer line, choose recurring terms in your notes, and copy the reminder message for the next billing cycle.

Can I make receipts?

Yes. Switch the document type to receipt, enter the amount paid, and export the paid document.

Can clients pay from the invoice?

You can add a Paystack, Flutterwave, PayPal, Wise, or other payment link. The tool also creates a QR code when a valid link is present.

Is this tax advice?

No. The tool calculates from the rates you enter. Confirm VAT, withholding, e-invoicing, and recordkeeping obligations with local rules or an accountant.
