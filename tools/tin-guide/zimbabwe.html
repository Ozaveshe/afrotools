TIN Registration in Zimbabwe — Complete Guide

The TIN (Tax Identification Number) / BP Number is issued by the Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) in Zimbabwe. Registration is free and typically takes 1-5 business days. Zimbabwe offers online TIN registration through the ZIMRA portal, making the process accessible from anywhere. The TIN is governed by the Income Tax Act (Chapter 23:06); Revenue Authority Act (Chapter 23:11); Finance Act (annual) and administered by the Ministry of Finance, Economic Development and Investment Promotion.

All businesses operating in Zimbabwe and individuals in formal employment are required to register for a TIN. The TIN is a 10-digit numeric (BP number for business partners) identifier needed for tax filing, business bank accounts, government contracts, and customs clearance. Failure to register carries penalties: uSD 30 per month of non-compliance.
