TIN Registration in Egypt — Complete Guide

The Tax Registration Number / رقم التسجيل الضريبي is issued by the Egyptian Tax Authority (ETA) in Egypt. Registration is free and typically takes 1-5 business days. Egypt offers online TIN registration through the ETA portal, making the process accessible from anywhere. The TIN is governed by the Income Tax Law No. 91 of 2005 (as amended); VAT Law No. 67 of 2016 and administered by the Ministry of Finance.

All businesses operating in Egypt and individuals in formal employment are required to register for a Tax Registration Number / رقم التسجيل الضريبي. The Tax Registration Number / رقم التسجيل الضريبي is a 9-digit numeric identifier needed for tax filing, business bank accounts, government contracts, and customs clearance. Failure to register carries penalties: fine of EGP 5,000 to EGP 50,000.
