TIN Registration in Gambia — Complete Guide

The TIN (Tax Identification Number) is issued by the Gambia Revenue Authority (GRA) in Gambia. Registration is free and typically takes 1-3 business days. Gambia offers online TIN registration through the GRA portal, making the process accessible from anywhere. The TIN is governed by the Income and Value Added Tax Act (IVATA) 2012; GRA Act 2014 and administered by the Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs.

All businesses operating in Gambia and individuals in formal employment are required to register for a TIN. The TIN is a 9-digit numeric identifier needed for tax filing, business bank accounts, government contracts, and customs clearance. Failure to register carries penalties: fine as per GRA Act.
