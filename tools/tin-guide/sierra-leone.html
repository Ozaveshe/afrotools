TIN Registration in Sierra Leone — Complete Guide

The TIN (Tax Identification Number) is issued by the National Revenue Authority (NRA) in Sierra Leone. Registration is free and typically takes 3-5 business days. Sierra Leone offers online TIN registration through the NRA portal, making the process accessible from anywhere. The TIN is governed by the Income Tax Act 2000; National Revenue Authority Act 2002 and administered by the Ministry of Finance.

All businesses operating in Sierra Leone and individuals in formal employment are required to register for a TIN. The TIN is a Variable numeric identifier needed for tax filing, business bank accounts, government contracts, and customs clearance. Failure to register carries penalties: le 2,000,000 fine.
