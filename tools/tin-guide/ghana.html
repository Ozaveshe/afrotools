TIN Registration in Ghana — Complete Guide

The TIN (Tax Identification Number) is issued by the Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA) in Ghana. Registration is free and typically takes Instant (online); 1-3 days (in person). Ghana offers online TIN registration through the GRA portal, making the process accessible from anywhere. The TIN is governed by the Revenue Administration Act 2016 (Act 915); Income Tax Act 2015 (Act 896) and administered by the Ministry of Finance.

All businesses operating in Ghana and individuals in formal employment are required to register for a TIN. The TIN is a 11-character alphanumeric (e.g., P0012345678 for individuals, C0012345678 for companies) identifier needed for tax filing, business bank accounts, government contracts, and customs clearance. Failure to register carries penalties: penalty of GHS 500 or imprisonment up to 1 year or both.
