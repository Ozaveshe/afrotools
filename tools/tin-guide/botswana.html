TIN Registration in Botswana — Complete Guide

The TIN (Tax Identification Number) is issued by the Botswana Unified Revenue Service (BURS) in Botswana. Registration is free and typically takes 1-3 business days. Botswana offers online TIN registration through the BURS portal, making the process accessible from anywhere. The TIN is governed by the Income Tax Act (Cap. 52:01); VAT Act (Cap. 50:03) and administered by the Ministry of Finance.

All businesses operating in Botswana and individuals in formal employment are required to register for a TIN. The TIN is a 9-digit numeric (prefixed with P for individuals, C for companies) identifier needed for tax filing, business bank accounts, government contracts, and customs clearance. Failure to register carries penalties: penalty of 200% of tax liability or BWP 5,000.
