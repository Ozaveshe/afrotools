TIN Registration in Guinea-Bissau — Complete Guide

The NIF (Número de Identificação Fiscal) is issued by the Direcção Geral das Contribuições e Impostos (DGCI) in Guinea-Bissau. Registration is free and typically takes 5-10 business days. Registration is currently available in person at DGCI offices. The TIN is governed by the Código Tributário da Guiné-Bissau and administered by the Ministério das Finanças.

All businesses operating in Guinea-Bissau and individuals in formal employment are required to register for a NIF. The NIF is a Variable numeric identifier needed for tax filing, business bank accounts, government contracts, and customs clearance. Failure to register carries penalties: fines per tax code.
