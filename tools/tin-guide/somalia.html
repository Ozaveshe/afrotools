TIN Registration in Somalia — Complete Guide

The TIN (Tax Identification Number) is issued by the Federal Member State Revenue Authorities / Inland Revenue Department (IRD) in Somalia. Registration is free and typically takes Variable (7-30 days depending on region). Registration is currently available in person at IRD offices. The TIN is governed by the Income Tax Act (varies by federal member state) and administered by the Ministry of Finance.

All businesses operating in Somalia and individuals in formal employment are required to register for a TIN. The TIN is a Variable identifier needed for tax filing, business bank accounts, government contracts, and customs clearance. Failure to register carries penalties: limited enforcement.

⚠️ Disclaimer This tool provides general information only. Tax laws and registration requirements change frequently. Always verify with IRD or a qualified tax advisor for the latest requirements.
