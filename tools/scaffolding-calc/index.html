Safety Checklist

  • Inspect all tubes for cracks, bends, or rust before use. Discard any damaged components.
  • All workers on scaffolding above 2m must wear a safety harness and hard hat.
  • Toe boards and guard rails are mandatory on all working platforms.
  • Load limit: standard scaffolding rated for 225 kg/m². Never exceed this — stack materials on floor, not on scaffold.
  • Scaffold must be inspected by a competent person before use, after modification, and after adverse weather.
  • Bamboo scaffolding: use fresh, mature bamboo (3+ years), lash with sisal or nylon rope. Replace immediately if cracked or dry.
