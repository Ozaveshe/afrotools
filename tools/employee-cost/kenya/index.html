Clear use case

Use it when budgeting a Kenyan hire, comparing gross salary with total monthly employer cost, or preparing a client-facing payroll estimate.

Worked example

A Kenyan HR team considering a KES 120,000 monthly salary can enter the gross amount, review estimated employer-side costs and use the total as a budget checkpoint before confirming statutory contributions.

Methodology and assumptions

The calculator uses the employer-cost rules currently loaded in AfroTools and the salary inputs entered by the user. It may not include every benefit, allowance, contract type, industry levy or company policy.
