Callback Checklist

        

          
1
Expose a public HTTPS POST URL such as /ussd.

          
2
Read sessionId, serviceCode, phoneNumber and text from the callback body.

          
3
Return plain text beginning with CON for menus or END for final screens.

          
4
Keep prompts short. Some Kenyan telco menus are around 160 to 184 characters.
