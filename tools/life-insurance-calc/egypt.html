Egypt compulsory third-party motor insurance is tiered by engine capacity: EGP 225/year up to 1030cc, EGP 350 for 1030-1330cc, EGP 750 for 1330-1630cc, and EGP 120 for EV/hybrid vehicles. Major insurers: GIG Egypt, AXA Egypt, Allianz Egypt, Misr Insurance. The market is regulated by FRA (Financial Regulatory Authority), with insurance penetration at 0.9% of GDP.
