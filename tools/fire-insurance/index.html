Estimate fire insurance premiums for buildings across Africa. Calculate based on property value, construction type, and fire protection measures. Use this free calculator to get a quick estimate before contacting insurance providers.

Fire insurance is mandatory for certain building categories across Africa. Commercial properties, factories, and multi-story residential buildings typically require cover. Premium rates range from 0.08% to 0.5% of property value depending on construction type, fire protection measures, and location. Major commercial insurers include Leadway, Jubilee, Santam, and AXA.
