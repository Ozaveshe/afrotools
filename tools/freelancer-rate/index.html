Frequently Asked Questions

Why use billable days instead of calendar days?

Freelancers need time for sales, admin, revisions, learning, unpaid calls, and late payments.

Should I charge hourly or per project?

Use day rate for planning, then convert to project pricing with scope, revision limits, and payment milestones.

What should a rate card include?

Deliverables, timeline, revision limits, payment terms, rush fee, usage rights, and what is excluded.
