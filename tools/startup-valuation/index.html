Evidence-led valuation worksheet

Build ranges from evidence you choose.

Use three independent methods without preset market multiples, country discounts, probabilities or automatic recommendations. Your data remains in this browser.

Shared assumptions

Use one currency or unit for every monetary input. Uncertainty applies symmetrically to scorecard and milestone point estimates; it does not replace due diligence.

1. Revenue multiple range

Enter annual revenue and low/base/high multiples supported by your own comparable evidence.

2. Evidence-weighted scorecard

Enter a verified comparable baseline, relative scores where 100 means comparable, and your decision weights.

3. Milestone build-up

Assign only values you can support to product, team, traction, relationships and risk-reduction evidence.

Independent method results

Method, uncertainty and evidence

Source boundary: your financial statements, contracts, cap table, verified comparable transactions and deal terms. AfroTools supplies no African market multiple, stage price, probability, FX rate or investment recommendation.

Revenue multiple = annual revenue × your low/base/high multiples. Scorecard = your comparable baseline × the normalized weighted average of your relative scores. Milestone build-up = the sum of the evidence values you enter. Methods remain separate; the cross-method span is not an average or a price.

Before using a range

Reconcile recurring versus one-off revenue, confirm the comparable’s date and deal terms, review dilution/debt/preferences, document every milestone value, and treat the output as a negotiation worksheet—not a valuation opinion, fundraising promise or investment advice.
