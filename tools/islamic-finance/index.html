      Methodology

      1. Financed amount is the asset price less deposit, unless you edit it to match the written quote.
      2. Total profit equals financed amount multiplied by the disclosed profit margin over the whole term.
      3. Total payable adds deposit, financed amount, disclosed profit, and fees.
      4. Payment per period divides financed amount, profit, and fees across the selected schedule.
      5. The annualized comparison rate is a cash-flow comparison to help compare quotes. It is not an interest label and does not decide Shariah compliance.

      Disclaimer

      This tool is not a fatwa or financial advice. It does not approve a Murabaha, Ijarah, Tawarruq, Musharakah, or any other contract. Ask a qualified Shariah scholar or Shariah board, and review the provider contract, fees, ownership transfer, late-payment clauses, takaful, and local law before signing.
