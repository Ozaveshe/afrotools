FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?
Yes. Create unlimited business plans, save drafts, and export PDFs for free. The AI review feature uses our API credits — also free for now.
What templates are available?
Restaurant, Tech Startup, Retail/E-commerce, Agriculture, and Manufacturing. Each pre-fills relevant sections with industry-specific guidance.
Can I use this for a bank loan application?
Absolutely. The output format follows the standard structure banks and investors expect. Add your financial projections and you have a solid lending document.
How does the AI review work?
After completing your plan, click "AI Review" to get feedback on completeness, market positioning, financial viability, and suggested improvements.
Is my data private?
All plan data is stored in your browser. It's never sent to our servers unless you explicitly use the AI review feature.
Can I collaborate with my team?
Currently the tool is single-user. Export your plan and share the PDF with team members for review.

Free Business Plan Builder for African Entrepreneurs

The AfroTools Business Plan Builder helps entrepreneurs across Africa create professional, investor-ready business plans without hiring expensive consultants. Whether you're launching a tech startup in Lagos, opening a restaurant in Nairobi, or starting an agricultural venture in Accra, this tool guides you through every section.

Guided 7-Section Structure

Every great business plan follows a proven structure: Executive Summary, Company Description, Market Analysis, Products and Services, Marketing Strategy, Financial Projections, and Funding Requirements. Our builder walks you through each section with prompts, examples, and tooltips designed for the African business context.

Financial Projections Made Simple

The financial projection table helps you forecast revenue, costs, and profit over three years. Enter your estimates and the tool automatically calculates margins, growth rates, and break-even points. Present these figures with confidence to investors and lenders.

Industry Templates

Don't start from a blank page. Choose from five industry templates — Restaurant, Tech Startup, Retail, Agriculture, and Manufacturing — each pre-filled with relevant section headers, market analysis frameworks, and financial categories specific to that industry.

AI-Powered Review

Once your plan is complete, use the AI review feature to get instant feedback. The system analyses your plan for completeness, market positioning, competitive advantage, financial viability, and overall coherence — then provides actionable suggestions for improvement.
