Passport Photo Generator: How It Works

The AfroTools Passport Photo Generator creates correctly sized passport and ID photos for popular African countries plus international destinations. Select a country and the tool applies the matching photo dimensions, then export a print-ready layout for local photo shops or home printing. Upload a clear headshot, and the tool crops and resizes your photo to meet common standards — whether that is 35x45mm for a Nigerian passport, 2x2 inches for a US visa, or other frequently used travel formats. Choose between white and blue backgrounds, both commonly accepted at embassies and consulates. The tool also generates print-ready layouts with multiple photos arranged on a single sheet, so you can print at home or at a local photo shop and cut to size. This saves you trips to expensive photo studios and gives you control over retakes until you get the perfect shot. All image processing happens in your browser, so your photos are never uploaded to a server. Ideal for travellers, visa applicants, students applying for international programmes, and anyone needing official ID photos.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many countries are supported?

The tool currently covers a set of popular African travel sizes plus major international destinations. We are expanding coverage over time, so always double-check the latest embassy requirement before submission.

Will the photo be accepted for visa applications?

The tool generates photos that meet the dimensional requirements specified by each country. For best results, use a well-lit photo with a neutral expression, taken against a plain background. Always check your specific embassy's latest requirements.

Can I print the photos at home?

Yes. The tool generates a print layout with multiple passport photos arranged on a standard paper size. Print it on photo paper using a home printer or take it to a local print shop and cut along the guidelines.

What background colours are available?

White and blue backgrounds are available. White is the most universally accepted background colour for passports and visas. Blue is required by some countries and ID card systems.

Is this free to use?

Yes, the Passport Photo Generator is completely free with no watermarks, no limits on the number of photos, and no account required. Process as many photos as you need.
