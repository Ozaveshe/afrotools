Calculation method and rounding

Add VAT: VAT = net × rate. Extract VAT: net = total ÷ (1 + rate). Calculations retain full precision through totals and round display values to two decimal places. Country presets are planning references, not a statement that the rate applies to your transaction.

Before you calculate

  • Check the transaction date and place of supply.
  • Confirm whether the item is standard-rated, zero-rated or exempt.
  • Check levies and special regimes separately.
  • Use the authority source or a qualified adviser before filing.

Privacy boundary

Amounts and descriptions stay in this browser tab. This page does not save calculation history or send entries to AI. Sharing copies only this page’s route; downloaded PDFs are created locally in your browser.

Preset coverage

The data pack distinguishes authority-bound planning presets from explicit source gaps. A gap never receives a legacy rate automatically. Even an available preset must be rechecked for the transaction.
