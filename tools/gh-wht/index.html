    What changes the answer

    Three checkpoints prevent a misleading blanket-rate result.

    Resident supply thresholdPublished resident goods, works and entity-service rows apply when the annual contract total exceeds GH₵2,000.
    Recipient details matterResident interest excludes individuals and resident financial institutions in the published table.
    Permanent establishmentGhana-PE-connected non-resident income needs classification outside this simple final-WHT workflow.
    Treaty relief is not automaticThis worksheet accepts a lower rate only when the user confirms beneficial ownership and a Commissioner-General approval letter.

    How to use the result

    1. Match the selected category to the contract and recipient facts.
    2. Check threshold, recipient exclusions, Ghana PE and treaty approval before relying on the rate.
    3. Keep the source, assumptions and approval evidence with the payment record.
    4. File and pay through the appropriate GRA workflow and issue the prescribed certificate.

    Rates and procedures can change. Recheck the linked GRA material before a real payment or filing.
