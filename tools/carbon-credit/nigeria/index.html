Frequently Asked Questions

Nigeria's tropical forests sequester approximately 5.2 tCO₂/ha/yr for intact forests, and 3.8 tCO₂/ha/yr for reforestation projects. These rates are based on IPCC Tier 2 data for West African tropical forests and validated by Verra project data from the Niger Delta and Cross River State regions.
Verra VCS is the most common for Nigerian projects (35+ registered projects). Gold Standard commands 20–40% price premiums but requires more rigorous social co-benefits documentation. Plan Vivo suits smaller community-based projects (minimum 500 ha for direct registration). NESREA oversees domestic carbon market compliance.
