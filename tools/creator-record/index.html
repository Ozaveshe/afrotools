FAQ

Common questions

Which browsers support screen recording?

Chrome, Edge, and Brave fully support all features. Firefox supports screen and webcam but may not capture system audio. Safari has limited support — screen capture requires Safari 16+.

What video format does it export?

Recordings export as WebM (VP9 codec). You can play WebM in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, VLC, and most video editors. For MP4, use any free converter.

Is there a max recording length?

No hard limit. Length depends on available RAM and disk space. We recommend under 30 minutes for best performance.

Is my recording private?

100% private. Everything happens in your browser. No video is uploaded anywhere. Only you have access to your recordings.

Can I change the webcam shape?

Yes — circle, rounded rectangle, or standard rectangle. You can also position the webcam in any of the four corners.

What annotation tools are available?

Red pen, yellow highlighter, arrows, text labels, eraser, and clear all. The toolbar appears during screen recording modes.
