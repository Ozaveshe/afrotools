Frequently Asked Questions

Why can Ramadan start shift by one day?

Many communities follow local moon sighting or an official council, so calculated dates remain planning dates until confirmed.

Should I publish mosque times from this tool?

Use this for drafting only. Public mosque timetables should use the mosque or council-approved prayer times.

What buffer should families use for suhoor?

Many households add 5-15 minutes before Fajr as a safety buffer, but local guidance should come first.
