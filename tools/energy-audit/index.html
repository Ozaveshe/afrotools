    Home Energy Audit FAQ

    Is this an appliance-by-appliance energy audit?

    No. It is a household-level planning estimate based on your bill, the selected country's residential tariff, home size, occupancy, cooling, lighting, and water-heating choices. It does not measure individual appliances.

    Can I add the savings percentages together?

    No. Recommendations can overlap, so their individual estimates are not additive. The calculator caps the combined household savings estimate at 50%.

    How should I verify an energy-saving recommendation?

    Test one change at a time, record meter or bill use before and after, and compare similar weather and occupancy conditions. Use the 30-day plan to record what to check.
