    Home Energy Audit in Africa — Why It Matters

    African households typically overspend on electricity by 20–40% due to inefficient appliances, wrong AC settings, and standby power drain. A simple audit identifies the highest-impact changes — often LED bulbs and AC temperature adjustment — that pay for themselves within weeks.
