Method and boundary

The engine converts annual net return and salary growth to effective monthly rates. It applies investment growth to the existing balance, adds each contribution at month end, and applies salary growth before the next month. It does not model fund-specific fees, changing rates, inflation, tax, benefit eligibility or provider performance.

Official sources reviewed 22 July 2026

  • PenCom: Pension Reform Act 2014 links the Act; section 4 sets the 10% employer and 8% employee minimum rates.
  • PenCom retirement and terminal-benefit regulation governs the member-specific benefit calculation.
  • PenCom lump-sum FAQ explains that a lump sum depends on the remaining balance supporting the regulated periodic payment threshold; this tool therefore does not assume 25%.

Privacy

No input is stored, put in a URL or sent to analytics, AI or an API. CSV, JSON and PDF are created locally. Avoid personal identifiers in source labels.

Interpretation

This is a planning scenario, not financial advice, a PenCom or PFA statement, or a guaranteed return. Confirm your scheme, pensionable-emoluments definition, rates, opening balance, fees and benefit choices with the responsible institutions.
