SADC Member States (16)

🌍 SADC (Southern African Development Community) has 16 member states. The SADC Free Trade Area was launched in 2008. SACU (Southern African Customs Union) members (ZA, BW, LS, NA, SZ) form a deeper economic union within SADC.
📋 The Tripartite Free Trade Area (TFTA) links SADC, COMESA, and EAC — creating a larger regional bloc covering 26 countries and 625 million people.
