FAQ

Common questions

What kind of scripts can ScriptPad generate?

5 formats: YouTube Standard, Podcast/Audio, Voiceover/Narration, Educational/Tutorial, and Storytime. Each uses the right structure for its medium.

How long are the generated scripts?

You choose: 3-5 min, 5-8 min, 8-12 min, or 12-20 min. Word count adjusts automatically — a 5-8 min video is roughly 1,200-1,800 words.

Does it include B-roll suggestions?

Yes! Every section includes visual cue suggestions — B-roll ideas, screen recording markers, graphic overlays, and camera direction notes.

What is the teleprompter feature?

A full-screen teleprompter that scrolls your script at adjustable speed (80-200 WPM). Mirror mode for front-camera, countdown, and tap to pause.

Can I edit the generated script?

Every section is directly editable. You can also regenerate individual sections, reorder, or delete them without affecting the rest.

Is ScriptPad free?

Yes. 5 generations per day, free. All formats, teleprompter, export, and editing included. Resets at midnight.
