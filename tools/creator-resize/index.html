FAQ

Common Questions

What image formats are supported?
PNG, JPEG, and WebP uploads up to 10MB. All exports are high-quality PNG with no compression artifacts.
How many sizes can I generate at once?
Up to 12 sizes covering Instagram, X, YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, Pinterest, and WhatsApp. Download all as a single ZIP.
What is the focal point feature?
Click on the important part of your image. All crops center around that point so faces, products, and text are never cut off.
Does it compress my images?
No. All processing maintains full quality using HTML5 Canvas at exact platform dimensions. No lossy compression applied.
Is my image uploaded to a server?
No. Everything runs 100% in your browser. Your image never leaves your device — nothing uploaded, stored, or tracked.
What are the background fill modes?
Blur Fill, Solid Color, Gradient, Edge Extend, or Crop Only. Each handles aspect ratio mismatches differently — choose what looks best.
