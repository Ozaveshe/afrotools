What this worksheet does

It normalizes an annual, monthly or per-payment quoted rate without guessing whether the contract is flat or reducing. It then models entered fees and equal end-of-period repayments. The source is your current written offer.

Before signing

Match the result to the lender’s dated repayment schedule. Confirm every mandatory charge, exact disbursement, late and early-settlement terms, insurance, compulsory savings, licence and the disclosure required by your regulator.

Privacy and exports

Calculations and exports run locally. Do not enter names, account numbers or identity details. Copy, CSV, JSON and PDF exports are available without signup or network submission.

Decision boundary

This is a planning estimate, not financial advice, an offer, approval, affordability assessment, provider ranking or recommendation. A lower modelled cost does not establish suitability.

Microfinance offer questions

Does the rate basis choose flat or reducing interest?

No. Rate basis and interest method are separate contract terms and separate inputs.

Why distinguish withheld and financed fees?

Withheld fees reduce the cash you receive. Financed fees increase the amount you owe, and you must declare whether they also accrue interest.

Why can the lender’s annual disclosure differ?

Legal conventions may use actual payment dates, prescribed day counts, taxes, rounding and different treatment of charges. Use the regulated disclosure as the legal reference.
