PDF Watermark Tool: How It Works

The AfroTools PDF Watermark Tool adds custom text watermarks to your PDF documents. Enter your watermark text � such as "CONFIDENTIAL," "DRAFT," "SAMPLE," or your company name � and customise the appearance with your preferred colour, opacity, rotation angle, font size, and position on the page. The watermark is applied across all pages of your PDF, creating a consistent branded or security overlay throughout the document. Adjust the opacity to make the watermark subtle enough to read through while still clearly visible, or make it bold for strong visual impact. The rotation control lets you angle the text diagonally (the most common watermark style) or keep it horizontal. Position options let you place the watermark at the centre, top, bottom, or corners of each page. This tool is essential for marking draft documents, protecting intellectual property, branding proposals and reports, and indicating document status. All processing runs in your browser � your PDF content and watermark settings are never sent to any server. Download the watermarked PDF instantly and share it with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customise the watermark appearance?

Yes. You can customise the text content, colour, opacity (transparency level), rotation angle, font size, and position on the page. This gives you full control over how the watermark looks.

Is the watermark applied to all pages?

Yes. The watermark is applied consistently across every page of your PDF document, ensuring uniform branding or security marking throughout the entire file.

Can the watermark be removed after it is applied?

The watermark is embedded into the downloaded PDF. While it may be possible to edit with advanced PDF software, the watermark is designed to be persistent. Keep your original unwatermarked file if you need a clean version.

What opacity level should I use?

For a subtle watermark that does not interfere with reading, use 10-20% opacity. For a clearly visible watermark on draft documents, 30-50% works well. Adjust until the watermark is visible but does not obscure the document content.

Is my PDF sent to a server?

No. The watermark is applied entirely in your browser using client-side processing. Your document never leaves your device, making it safe for confidential and sensitive files.
