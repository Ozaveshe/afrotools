How to cook it

Step-by-step method

Keep the rhythm calm, watch the texture, and adjust seasoning at the end.

4 steps 18 min total easy
1
Toast if needed
If using plain barley flour, toast it in a dry pan until nutty and lightly darker.
toast 05:00
2
Hydrate
Stir in hot water gradually until the flour becomes a thick porridge.
hydrate 04:00
3
Enrich
Mix in butter or oil, sugar, salt, and warm milk if using.
enrich 03:00
4
Serve warm
Rest briefly so the barley swells, then serve warm.
rest 03:00
