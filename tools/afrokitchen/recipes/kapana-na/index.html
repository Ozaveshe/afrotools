How to cook it

Step-by-step method

Keep the rhythm calm, watch the texture, and adjust seasoning at the end.

Back to Namibia
4 steps 20 min prep 10 min cook 30 min total easy
1
Mix salsa
Combine tomatoes, onion, chilies, vinegar, oil, and a pinch of salt. Let it sit while the grill heats.
2
Season beef
Rub beef with oil, kapana spice, and salt. Keep extra spice nearby for dipping.
Rest beef 10:00
3
Grill hot
Grill over very hot coals until charred outside and juicy inside.
Grill beef 07:00
4
Slice and serve
Slice into bite-sized pieces and serve immediately with salsa and extra kapana spice.
