Kitchen snapshot

Djiboutian Laxoox

Laxoox is breakfast bread with bubbles. The batter ferments until lively, then cooks on one side so the top stays spongy enough to catch honey, ghee, or stew.

How to cook it

Step-by-step method

Keep the rhythm calm, watch the texture, and adjust seasoning at the end.

Back to Djibouti
5 steps 150 min prep 25 min cook 175 min total easy
1
Mix the batter
Whisk flours, yeast, sugar, salt, and warm water until smooth.
2
Ferment
Cover and let the batter stand until bubbly and lightly tangy.
Kitchen noteA warm kitchen gives better bubbles.
Ferment 120:00
3
Cook one side
Pour a thin layer into a warm non-stick pan. Cook without flipping until the top is full of small holes and the edges lift.
Cook each 02:00
4
Stack
Lift the laxoox carefully and stack on a plate. Repeat with the remaining batter.
5
Serve
Serve with honey and ghee for breakfast, or use it to scoop soup or stew.
