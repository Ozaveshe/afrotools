Kitchen snapshot

Mullah Ahmar Tagalia

Mullah ahmar tagalia is a Sudanese red stew built around meat, tomato, onion, and powdered dried okra. The okra powder thickens the sauce into the texture that makes kisra or gurasa essential.

What to know before you cook

Mullah ahmar tagalia is a Sudanese red stew built around meat, tomato, onion, and powdered dried okra. The okra powder thickens the sauce into the texture that makes kisra or gurasa essential.

Chef watch-outs
  • Stopping the base before the pepper, onion, or spice edge has mellowed.
  • Thinning the pot before the body of the soup or stew has developed.
  • Rushing the rest or fermentation period.
How you know it is ready
  • The sauce should coat the spoon and taste rounded, not watery or raw.
  • The bread or batter should smell pleasantly fermented, toasted, or nutty rather than floury.
  • The aroma should smell rounded rather than raw or sharp.

How to cook it

Step-by-step method

Keep the rhythm calm, watch the texture, and adjust seasoning at the end.

Back to Sudan
4 steps 100 min total medium
1
Brown meat
Brown meat with oil and onions until the onions soften and the meat starts to color.
brown 12:00
2
Simmer red base
Add garlic, tomatoes, tomato paste, spices, salt, and water, then simmer until meat is tender.
simmer 55:00
3
Thicken with okra
Whisk dried okra powder with cool broth and stir it into the pot.
thicken 08:00
4
Finish slowly
Simmer gently until the stew thickens and tastes rounded.
finish 10:00

Dried okra powder may be called waika, and the stew can be red with tomato or pale and yogurt-based in other mullah styles.
