How to cook it

Step-by-step method

Keep the rhythm calm, watch the texture, and adjust seasoning at the end.

Back to Tanzania
5 steps 10 min prep 20 min cook 30 min total easy
1
Fry Chips
Heat oil in a deep pan to 170C. Fry potato chips in batches until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
Kitchen noteDouble frying makes crispier chips — fry once at lower heat, rest, then fry again at higher heat.
Deep fry 20:00
2
Prepare Egg Mix
Beat eggs with salt, pepper, diced onion, tomato, and chili.
3
Combine
Place fried chips in a hot oiled skillet. Pour beaten egg mixture over the chips, making sure eggs fill all the gaps.
Kitchen notePress chips down so they are fully coated in egg.
4
Cook Omelette
Cook on medium heat until the bottom is golden and set. Carefully flip using a plate and cook the other side until golden.
Kitchen noteUse a plate larger than the pan to flip — slide omelette onto plate, then invert back into pan.
Cook both sides 06:00
5
Serve
Slide onto a plate and cut into wedges. Serve hot with ketchup and pili pili sauce on the side.
Kitchen noteBest eaten immediately while chips are still crispy inside the omelette.
