How to cook it

Step-by-step method

Keep the rhythm calm, watch the texture, and adjust seasoning at the end.

4 steps 50 min total easy
1
Cook eggplant
Steam or roast eggplant until very tender.
eggplant 20:00
2
Cook tomato base
Cook tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper until reduced.
tomato base 15:00
3
Mash together
Add eggplant and mash into the tomato base. Cook until thick and glossy.
mash 10:00
4
Finish bright
Stir in herbs and lemon juice. Serve warm or room temperature.
finish 02:00

Eggplant may be roasted, boiled, or pan-cooked before being mashed with tomato.
