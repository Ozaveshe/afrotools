Recipe overview

What to know before you cook

Sardine kefta tagine is coastal Moroccan cooking at its smartest. Sardines are chopped with herbs and spices, shaped into small balls, and simmered in tomato chermoula sauce until tender.

What the dish tastes like

Moroccan sardine meatballs simmered in tomato, chermoula, olives, lemon, and peppers.

When to cook it

Best for seafood lunches, Friday meals, and bread-and-salad tables.. Plan on a medium cook and about 70 minutes total.

What to serve alongside it

Khobz, taktouka, olives, and preserved lemon.

How to cook it

Step-by-step method

Keep the rhythm calm, watch the texture, and adjust seasoning at the end.

Mix sardine kefta

Shape balls

shape 15:00

Simmer tomato sauce

Cook the kefta
