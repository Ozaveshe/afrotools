How to cook it

Step-by-step method

Keep the rhythm calm, watch the texture, and adjust seasoning at the end.

Roast peppers
Roast peppers until blistered, then steam briefly, peel, seed, and chop.

2
Cook tomatoes
Cook tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper until thick.
tomatoes 15:00
3
Combine
Add chopped peppers and cook until the salad is soft and glossy.
combine 10:00
4
Finish
Stir in herbs and serve warm or room temperature.
