How to cook it

Step-by-step method

Keep the rhythm calm, watch the texture, and adjust seasoning at the end.

Heat liquid
Warm milk or water with cinnamon until steaming but not boiling.
Warm liquid 05:00
2
Melt cacao
Add chocolate or cacao and whisk until completely smooth.
Melt cacao 03:00
3
Sweeten
Add sugar, vanilla if using, and a pinch of salt. Simmer gently until slightly thickened.
Simmer 04:00
4
Whisk and serve
Remove cinnamon, whisk until glossy and foamy, and serve hot.
