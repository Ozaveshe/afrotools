What are popular dishes from Ethiopia?

Popular starting points in this AfroKitchen country hub include Doro Wat, Kitfo, Genfo, Gomen, and Shiro Wat.

What ingredients are common in Ethiopia cooking?

In the stored Ethiopia recipes, common ingredients include garlic, salt, berbere spice, fresh ginger, onion, niter kibbeh, oil or niter kibbeh, and water.

What can I cook first from Ethiopia?

Gomen is a practical first pick from this hub at about 45 minutes.
