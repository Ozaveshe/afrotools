What are popular dishes from Lesotho?

Popular starting points in this AfroKitchen country hub include Papa le Moroho, Likhobe, Nyekoe, and Motoho.

What ingredients are common in Lesotho cooking?

In the stored Lesotho recipes, common ingredients include salt, water, onion, sorghum grain, vegetable oil, bulgur or wheat berries, dried sugar beans, and lukewarm water.

What can I cook first from Lesotho?
