Submission checklist

Help us preserve the story, not just the steps.

  • Add exact ingredient amounts when possible so the recipe is easier to test and publish.
  • Tell us when the dish is usually served: celebrations, weekends, family gatherings, or everyday meals.
  • Include regional variations or substitutions if your household cooks it differently.

If you only know the recipe informally, that is still useful. Share what you know and we can help shape it into a publishable entry.
