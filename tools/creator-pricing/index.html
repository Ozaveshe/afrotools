FAQ

Common Questions

How does CreatorPricing calculate my rate?
We combine market research signals, crafts, specialty, experience level, country, city, deliverables, usage rights, revision scope, and timeline pressure to generate a personalized rate range.
Which countries are supported?
We support 15+ African countries including Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, Tanzania, Egypt, Ethiopia, Uganda, Rwanda, Senegal, Cote d'Ivoire, Cameroon, Morocco, Tunisia, and Botswana.
Is my rate data kept private?
Yes. Your individual rate calculations are never shared. Aggregated, anonymous data helps improve rate accuracy for all users.
Can I use this to quote clients?
Absolutely. Use the Project Quoter to generate itemized quotes with line items, suggested totals, and forgotten charges like travel, editing time, and usage rights.
How often is the rate data updated?
Market rate data is refreshed quarterly based on survey data, industry reports, and community contributions from across the continent.
Is this free to use?
The rate calculator and project quoter are completely free. The AI Pricing Advisor has daily usage limits for free users, with unlimited access for Pro subscribers.
