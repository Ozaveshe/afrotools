PDF Redaction Tool: How It Works

The AfroTools PDF Redaction Tool lets you permanently remove sensitive information from PDF documents by drawing black boxes over text, numbers, or any content that needs to be hidden. Unlike simply highlighting text black in a word processor (which can be copied and pasted to reveal the hidden content), this tool flattens the redaction into the document so the original text is permanently destroyed. Upload your PDF, navigate to any page, and click and drag to draw redaction boxes over the areas you want to hide. Redact names, account numbers, addresses, signatures, confidential figures, or any other sensitive data. When you download the redacted PDF, the covered content is completely removed — not just visually hidden. This is critical for legal compliance, data protection, and sharing documents with third parties who should not see certain information. The entire process runs in your browser using client-side JavaScript, so your sensitive documents are never uploaded to any server. This tool is essential for lawyers, HR professionals, compliance officers, and anyone handling confidential documents that need selective disclosure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the redaction permanent?

Yes. Once you download the redacted PDF, the underlying text beneath the black boxes is permanently removed. It cannot be recovered by copying, selecting, or using any PDF editing tool.

How do I redact content?

Upload your PDF, then click and drag on any page to draw a black box over the content you want to hide. You can add multiple redaction boxes on any page. Preview your redactions, then download the final document.

Is this different from using a black highlighter in Word?

Yes, fundamentally. Black highlighting in Word only covers text visually — the text can still be selected and copied. This tool permanently removes the content underneath redaction boxes, making it truly secure.

Is my document kept private during redaction?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your PDF is never uploaded to any server, making it safe for legal documents, medical records, financial statements, and other highly sensitive files.

Can I undo a redaction after downloading?

No. Once the redacted PDF is downloaded, the redactions are permanent and cannot be reversed. Always keep a copy of the original unredacted document if you may need the full content in the future.
