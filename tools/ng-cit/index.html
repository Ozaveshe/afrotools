What changed under the 2026 regime?

What qualifies as a small company?

The enacted definition uses all three tests: gross turnover no more than ₦50M, total fixed assets no more than ₦250M, and no professional-services business. Passing only the turnover test is not enough.

Why are there two profit inputs?

Section 56 charges CIT on total profits. Section 59 charges development levy on assessable profits. A serious estimate must not silently use one number for both.

Where are medium-company 20%, education tax and minimum tax?

Those belong to the repealed framework. This calculator is scoped to the Nigeria Tax Act 2025 from 1 January 2026 and does not blend the regimes.
