African Influencer Marketing Insight
Micro-influencers (10k–100k followers) in Africa often outperform mega-influencers on ROI due to higher trust and engagement in tight-knit communities. A Nigerian food micro-influencer with 50k engaged followers can drive more product sales than a 1M-follower mega-influencer with low engagement.
