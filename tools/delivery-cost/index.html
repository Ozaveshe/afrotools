Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest delivery option in Nairobi?
For small packages under 5kg within Nairobi, Sendy and local boda-boda couriers are typically cheapest at KSh 250-400 base. For larger items, Lalamove offers competitive van and truck rates starting at KSh 800. For e-commerce, Max Delivery offers competitive same-day rates. Prices depend heavily on distance, time of day, and whether it's a dedicated delivery or shared route.
How does Lalamove pricing work in Africa?
Lalamove charges a base fee plus a per-km rate. For motorcycles in Kenya: KSh 350 base + KSh 50/km. For vans: KSh 800 + KSh 80/km. For trucks: KSh 2,500+ depending on size. Express and same-day services have surge multipliers. Lalamove is currently available in Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, and Ghana, with expansion ongoing.
How is cross-border delivery priced in Africa?
Cross-border delivery costs $0.50-2.00/km for trucks plus border fees, customs duties, agent fees, and waiting time. Nairobi to Kampala (650km) typically costs $600-1,200 for a 20-tonne truck including all fees. Regional logistics hubs like Nairobi, Lagos, and Johannesburg offer more competitive rates due to higher freight volumes and better return loads.
