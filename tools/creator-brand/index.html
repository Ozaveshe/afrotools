FAQ

Common questions

How does cross-tool sync work?

Your brand kit is saved to local storage. Other Creator Suite tools read your colors, fonts, and voice settings automatically, keeping everything consistent.

What logo formats are supported?

Any image URL — PNG, SVG, JPG, or WebP. For best results use SVG or transparent PNG. Each slot previews on both light and dark backgrounds.

Can I export my color palette?

Yes — as CSS custom properties, Tailwind config, or Figma JSON. One-click copy for each format.

Does it support Google Fonts?

Yes. Choose from 20 popular fonts or paste a custom Google Fonts URL. Live preview updates instantly.

Can I share my brand guide?

Generate a self-contained HTML brand guide with logos, colors, typography, and tone. Print or save as a file.

Where is my data stored?

All data stays in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to a server unless you use the AI voice sample generator.
