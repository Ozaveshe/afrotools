Method and limitations

Know what this estimate can and cannot prove

The calculator uses selected public points rules reviewed on 18 July 2026. It does not determine program eligibility, verify documents, read an official case, or replace the destination government's calculator.

  • Canada: assumes no accompanying spouse and one CLB level across all four abilities; job offers no longer earn CRS points.
  • Australia: caps combined overseas and Australian skilled-employment points at 20 and uses 5 points for subclass 190 or 15 for eligible subclass 491 nomination/sponsorship.
  • United Kingdom: awards the 20 tradeable points only when the selected salary floor and the route-specific occupation going rate are both met.
