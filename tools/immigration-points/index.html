Frequently Asked Questions

In 2025-2026, CRS cut-offs for all-program draws have ranged from 480-530. FSWP-specific draws sometimes have lower cutoffs (460-490). Provincial Nomination (PNP) adds 600 points, essentially guaranteeing an ITA. If your score is below 470, consider a PNP pathway or improving language/Canadian experience scores.
The minimum is 65 points to submit an Expression of Interest (EOI). However, in practice, most occupations require 75-90 points to receive an invitation in competitive pools. Popular occupations like engineering, nursing, and IT can require 80+ points. Having state nomination (190) adds 10 points to your EOI score.
You need exactly 70 points. 50 are mandatory: 20 for a licensed sponsor job offer, 20 for the job meeting skill level RQF3+, and 10 for English language. The remaining 20 come from salary (20 pts if above general threshold, 0-20 if below but meets going rate), shortage occupation (20 pts), or STEM PhD (20 pts). The general salary threshold in 2026 is £38,700/year or the specific SOC going rate.
