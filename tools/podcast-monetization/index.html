CPM Rates for Africa
African audiences command lower CPM rates ($3–8) vs US ($18–25) or UK ($15–20). But sponsorships from African brands — Flutterwave, GTBank, MTN, Jumia — can pay flat rates of $500–$5,000 per episode regardless of CPM. Pitch sponsors directly rather than relying solely on programmatic ads.
