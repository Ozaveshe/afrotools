Frequently Asked Questions

Is gratuity the same as severance?

Not always. Some countries or contracts separate gratuity, severance, notice, leave pay, and pension.

What can change the final payment?

Contract terms, reason for exit, service years, daily-pay formula, tax, unpaid loans, and leave balance.

Can employers use this for final payroll?

Use it as a review estimate only, then confirm with current labour law and payroll records.
