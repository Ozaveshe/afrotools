FAQ

Questions students usually ask when cost becomes real

Why compare upfront cash pressure separately from total cost?

Because many students can manage a route over time but still get blocked by the funds they must show or mobilize before departure. This page separates that barrier from the full-program estimate so the decision is more realistic.

Does the scholarship mode mean the route already has funding?

No. The scholarship toggle is a planning scenario. Partial support uses a reference tuition discount, while full support assumes tuition is covered but does not automatically assume living costs are funded too.

What should I do if my preferred destination only works with funding?

Do not just abandon it. Use Scholarship Finder, check the IELTS route that unlocks stronger schools or awards, and save the scenario into Education Hub so the rest of your student plan stays connected.
