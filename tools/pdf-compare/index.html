Compare PDFs Online for Free

The AfroTools PDF Compare tool lets you diff two PDF documents side-by-side directly in your browser. Whether you need to review contract changes, compare document drafts, or verify edits, this tool highlights every addition, deletion, and modification at both the text and visual level.

Everything runs locally in your browser. Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security for sensitive documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the text diff work?
The tool extracts text from each page of both PDFs, then uses a diff algorithm to find additions, deletions, and unchanged sections. Results are displayed side-by-side with color-coded highlights.
What does the visual diff show?
Visual diff renders both PDF pages as images and uses pixel-level comparison to highlight areas that differ between the two documents. You can also use a slider to blend between original and modified versions.
Is my PDF uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your PDFs never leave your device.
Can I compare PDFs with different page counts?
Yes. The tool compares pages that exist in both PDFs. Extra pages in either document are flagged as entirely added or removed.
