Markup and selling price calculator

Calculate from markup or target margin without hidden tax, FX, benchmark or pricing assumptions.

Formulas

From markup: selling price = cost × (1 + markup ÷ 100).

From target margin: selling price = cost ÷ (1 − target margin ÷ 100).

Profit: selling price − cost. Margin: profit ÷ selling price × 100. Markup: profit ÷ cost × 100.

Example: cost 100 and markup 50% gives selling price 150, profit 50 and margin 33.33%.
