Privacy
Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser using the Web Speech API. No files are uploaded to any server. Your documents remain completely private.
Supported Browsers
Works best in Chrome, Edge, and Safari. Firefox has limited voice support. The number of available voices depends on your operating system.

The PDF to Audio converter transforms your PDF documents into spoken audio using the Web Speech API built into your browser. Upload a PDF, select your preferred voice and speed, and listen to the content read aloud. This is ideal for consuming documents during commutes, exercising, or when you simply prefer listening over reading. The tool extracts text from your PDF locally and processes everything in the browser, so your documents remain private. Students reviewing study materials, professionals catching up on reports, and anyone with visual impairments or reading difficulties will find this a practical way to access written content.
