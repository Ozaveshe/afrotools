FAQ

How many titles does it generate?

Each generation produces 8 titles — one per style: Clickbait, SEO-Optimized, Storyteller, Listicle, Question, Bold Claim, How-To, and Viral. You can also generate 3 more variations of any style you like.

Which platforms does it support?

TitleSmith optimizes titles for YouTube, Blog/Article, Newsletter subjects, Instagram, X/Twitter, and LinkedIn. Each platform has specific character limits and formatting rules built in.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

TitleSmith is purpose-built for titles only. It knows African creator culture, generates 8 distinct styles per topic, shows character counts with platform limits, and includes A/B testing. No prompt engineering needed.

Is it free to use?

Yes! Free users get 10 generations per day. Each generation produces 8 titles plus A/B analysis. That is 80+ title options daily, completely free.

Can I edit the generated titles?

Every generated title is editable — just tap on it to make changes. You can also copy, favorite, or request more variations in the same style.

Does it save my titles?

Your last 20 generations are saved locally on your device. You can reload any past generation from the History panel. Logged-in users also get cloud sync.
