?? Format Quick Reference

Current Format: APA 7th Edition

American Psychological Association style, 7th edition. Commonly used in social sciences and education.

Format Key Points

  • Author Last Name, First Initial. (Year). Title of work.
  • Use "et al." for 3+ authors
  • Italicize book and journal titles
  • Hanging indent for all citations

Common Mistakes

  • Missing year in parentheses
  • Not italicizing titles
  • Incorrect punctuation order
  • Missing DOI or URL
