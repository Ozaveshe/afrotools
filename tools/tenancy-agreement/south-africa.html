South Africa Tenancy Agreement — Legal Requirements

Tenancy agreements in South Africa are governed by Rental Housing Act 50 of 1999. The maximum security deposit is 1-2 months (interest-bearing). Landlord notice period: 1 month written. Tenant notice period: 1 month written. Disputes are resolved through the Rental Housing Tribunal.
