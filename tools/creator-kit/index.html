FAQ

Common Questions

What is a media kit?
A media kit is a professional document that showcases your work, audience stats, rates, and past clients to potential brand partners. Think of it as your creative CV for brand deals.
How many templates are available?
CreatorKit offers 6 professionally designed templates: Bold, Editorial, Vibrant, Music, Professional, and Minimalist — each optimized for different creative niches.
Can brands see my rates?
You control this. You can show full pricing, show package tiers, or hide rates entirely with a "Contact for pricing" option.
What format is the media kit?
Your kit is available as a shareable link (a live micro-site), downloadable PDF, and PNG for Instagram stories. You can share it via WhatsApp with one tap.
How do I update my kit?
Just edit any section in the builder. Changes are saved automatically and reflected on your shared link instantly. No need to re-send links.
Is this free to use?
Yes. The full media kit builder, all 6 templates, and link sharing are completely free. AI features like bio writing have daily limits for free users, unlimited for Pro.
