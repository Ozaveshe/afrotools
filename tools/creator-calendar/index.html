FAQ

Common Questions

Does it auto-post to my accounts?
Not yet. CreatorCalendar is a planning tool — it helps you organize, write, and schedule your content plan. You still post manually or use your existing scheduling tool. Auto-posting is on our roadmap.
Which platforms are supported?
Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Newsletter. Each platform has its own character limits, content type suggestions, and optimal posting times.
Can my team see my calendar?
Team collaboration is coming soon. Currently your calendar is private to your account. You can export and share your content plan via PDF or link.
How does the AI content planner work?
Tell us your niche, platforms, and posting frequency. Our AI generates a full month of content ideas balanced across your content pillars, with hooks, captions, and platform-specific formats.
What are content pillars?
Content pillars are categories that organize your content strategy — Educational, Entertaining, Promotional, Personal, and Behind the Scenes. The tool helps you maintain a healthy balance.
Is this free?
The calendar, planner, and all views are completely free. AI-powered caption writing and content plan generation have daily usage limits for free users.
