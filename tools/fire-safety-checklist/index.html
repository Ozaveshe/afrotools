Fire Safety Readiness Checklist FAQ

Is the readiness score a certificate?

No. Only the competent authority or authorized professional can inspect a property and determine certification or compliance.

Are the remediation costs live quotes?

No. They are illustrative assumptions; obtain current itemized local quotes.

What should I do in an emergency?

Leave danger immediately and contact the local emergency service. Do not delay action to use this checklist.
