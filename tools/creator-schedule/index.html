FAQ

Common questions

Does CreatorSchedule auto-post to my accounts?

No. CreatorSchedule is a planning and reminder tool. When it's time to post, you get a notification with one-tap copy and a deep link to open the platform. This keeps you in control.

Which platforms are supported?

Instagram, X/Twitter, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Threads. Each is color-coded on the calendar.

Where is my data stored?

All posts, templates, and schedules are stored locally in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to any server.

What does Smart Queue do?

It suggests optimal posting times based on platform best practices and can auto-fill your week with suggested time slots.

How does bulk import work?

Paste multiple posts separated by --- in the Bulk Composer. Each block becomes a separate post you can assign platforms and times to.

Can I use content from other Creator Suite tools?

Yes. Captions from CaptionCraft or ideas from CreatorMind can be pasted directly into the composer. All Creator Suite tools work together.
