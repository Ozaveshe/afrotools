Frequently Asked Questions

Which countries are supported?
Currently Nigeria (NEPA/DISCO bands A-E), Kenya (KPLC domestic tiers), South Africa (Eskom Homepower blocks), and Ghana (ECG lifeline tiers). Each uses the official published tariff structure.
How accurate are the tariff rates?
The rates reflect publicly available tariff schedules. Actual bills may include fixed charges, taxes, levies, and VAT not captured here. Use results as estimates only.
What are Nigeria's electricity bands?
Nigerian electricity customers are classified into bands A through E based on hours of supply. Band A gets 20+ hours and pays the highest rate, while Band E gets minimal hours and pays the lowest rate.
How does the solar savings comparison work?
After calculating your bill, the tool estimates savings if 80% of your electricity came from solar panels. This gives a rough idea of potential monthly savings to help evaluate whether solar investment makes sense for your household.
Can I add custom appliances?
Yes. Select an appliance from the pre-loaded list or modify the wattage to match your specific device. You can adjust the hours of daily use and quantity for each appliance.
