Frequently Asked Questions

How does the difficulty weighting work?

Hard subjects get 3x the time allocation, Medium gets 2x, and Easy gets 1x. For example, with 3 subjects (1 hard, 1 medium, 1 easy), the total weight is 3+2+1=6. The hard subject gets 3/6 = 50% of study time, medium gets 33%, easy gets 17%.

Can I save my timetable?

Click "Print / Save" to open the browser's print dialog where you can save as PDF or print directly. Your subjects are also saved in your browser's local storage so they'll be there when you return.

How many hours should I study per day?

For exam preparation, 4-6 hours of focused study per day is effective. Quality matters more than quantity — use active recall and spaced repetition rather than passive reading. Take regular breaks (Pomodoro technique: 25 minutes study, 5 minutes break).
