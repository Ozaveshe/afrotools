How the model works

A reducing-balance offer uses the standard level-payment amortisation formula. A flat-rate offer calculates interest on the original principal for the full term. Upfront, deducted, recurring and final charges remain separate so the assumptions stay visible.

What the result cannot decide

  • Whether you qualify or a lender is trustworthy.
  • The legally defined APR or effective rate in your country.
  • Charges or rate changes not entered.
  • Affordability, legal suitability or the best lender for you.
