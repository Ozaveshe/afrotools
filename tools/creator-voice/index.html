FAQ

Common questions

What audio quality does CreatorVoice record in?

CreatorVoice records at your microphone's native sample rate (typically 44.1kHz or 48kHz). WAV export is lossless; MP3 and OGG use high-bitrate compression for smaller files.

Is there a maximum recording length?

No hard limit. Recording length depends on your device's available memory. Most modern devices handle 30+ minutes easily. Save periodically for very long sessions.

How does multi-track mixing work?

3 tracks: Voice, Music, SFX. Record on Track 1, upload music to Track 2, add effects to Track 3. Each has independent volume, mute, solo, and pan controls.

How does noise reduction work?

A noise gate analyzes quiet sections to find the noise floor, then attenuates audio below that threshold. Effective for background hum, fan noise, and room ambience.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari 15+. Chrome/Edge give the best experience. Microphone permission is required for recording features.

Are my projects saved?

Yes — locally in your browser via IndexedDB. Audio data never leaves your device. Save multiple projects and switch between them anytime.
