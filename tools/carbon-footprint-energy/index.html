Energy Carbon Footprint in Africa — Understanding Your Impact

Africa's per-capita energy carbon footprint is among the lowest globally, yet the combination of unreliable grid power and heavy generator use creates significant emissions. Understanding your footprint is the first step to reducing it through solar, LPG upgrades, and energy efficiency.
