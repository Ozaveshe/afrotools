Keep records separately

Questions before relying on the plan

Is ten percent built into the calculator?

No. The field starts at zero and the tool neither selects nor recommends a rate.

Does a negative remainder mean I should stop giving?

The number only shows that the entered plan plus entered essentials exceeds the reference amount. The tool does not make a moral, religious or financial decision.

Can I claim this contribution as tax deductible?

This tool cannot determine that. Eligibility can depend on the recipient, receipt, jurisdiction, date and current law.

Are my amounts uploaded or saved?

No. Calculation and exports run locally without storage or transmission.
