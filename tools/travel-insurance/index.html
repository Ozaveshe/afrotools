Travel Insurance Cost Estimator — What You Need to Know

Free travel insurance cost estimator for African travelers. Calculate premiums for trips within Africa, to Europe, Americas, Asia or worldwide. Use this free calculator to get a quick estimate before contacting insurance providers.

Travel insurance is increasingly required for visa applications to many destinations. Schengen visa applications require minimum EUR 30,000 medical coverage. Intra-Africa travel insurance covers medical emergencies, trip cancellation, lost luggage, and repatriation. Major providers include AXA, Allianz, Jubilee, and local insurers partnered with global networks.
