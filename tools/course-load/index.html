How should I use the workload diagnosis?

Use it to decide whether to keep the semester, reduce credits, speak to an adviser, or move difficult courses to a lighter term.

Does this replace my university adviser?

No. It is a planning estimate. Confirm prerequisites, minimum-load rules, graduation requirements, and overload approvals with your department or registry.

Why include work and commute hours?

A credit load can look fine on paper but become risky when paid work, caregiving, travel time, labs, and project work leave too few weekly study hours.
