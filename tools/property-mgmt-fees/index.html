💡 NEGOTIATION TIPS
  • Letting fee is most negotiable — try 50% of 1 month for renewals
  • For multi-property landlords, negotiate a lower management % (7–8%)
  • Ask agents to cap the monthly management fee for high-value properties
  • Full-service vs. tenant-find only — compare both options
  • Get quotes from 3 agents before signing any management agreement
