Method and checks

This local planner compares an optional current salary or offer with the benchmark midpoint you enter, then produces transparent lower, target, counter-offer, and stretch scenarios plus a negotiation script.

  • The lower scenario is 90% of your benchmark, the midpoint is 100%, the counter-offer is 105%, and the stretch scenario is 110%. These are planning bands, not statistical percentiles.
  • Country, industry, role, experience, company size, and location are report context only; they do not silently adjust the benchmark you supplied.
  • The calculation and TXT export run locally and make no network or external-function request.
  • Check written benefits, bonus rules, equity terms, probation, notice period, tax effects, remote-work terms, non-compete clauses, and timing before accepting or rejecting an offer.
  • This is career planning support, not legal, tax, immigration, financial, employment, union, or HR policy advice, and it does not guarantee a raise, offer, or promotion.
