Farm area is a starting proxy, not a pump specification

If the pump is unknown, farm-size mode lets the protected engine estimate 0.5 kW per hectare with a 1 kW minimum. If you know the pump rating, select it directly and the hectares will not alter the cost model.

Diesel vs Solar Farm FAQ

How does farm-size pump estimation work?

Farm-size mode uses the protected engine's simple planning proxy of 0.5 kW per hectare, with a 1 kW minimum. It is not hydraulic pump sizing and must be checked against water demand, total dynamic head, flow, and borehole yield.

What happens when I select a known pump size?

The selected pump kW drives the calculation. Farm hectares remain useful context for the copied quote brief but do not change costs, solar array size, fuel use, or payback.

What should a solar-pump supplier verify?

Ask the supplier to verify daily water demand, pumping hours, static and dynamic head, pipe friction, pump curve, borehole recovery, solar yield, mounting, controls, dry-run protection, and maintenance support.
