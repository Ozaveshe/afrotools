Frequently Asked Questions

What breaks gym ROI fastest?

Low retention, power cost, rent, equipment downtime, trainer payroll, and overestimating paid memberships.

Should I buy all equipment at once?

Often no. Start with demand-proven zones, then add machines based on member usage and cash flow.

What metric should I track monthly?

Active paid members, churn, revenue per member, opex, equipment repairs, and cash reserve.
