      Frequently Asked Questions

      What makes a career plan realistic?

      One target role, measurable skill gaps, proof projects, applications, networking, and a salary negotiation date.

      Should I change jobs or negotiate?

      Compare internal promotion path, market demand, proof strength, and risk before deciding.

      What should I track weekly?

      Applications, portfolio proof, recruiter conversations, interviews, skill practice, and salary data.
