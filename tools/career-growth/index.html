      Method and checks

      This MVP projects a 10-year career path from country, industry, current level, salary, education, growth track, learning investment, networking level, and employer-mobility assumptions.

      • Salary paths combine local baseline assumptions with level multipliers, annual raises, promotion intervals, learning and network boosts, and job-hop premiums.
      • The action plan is tailored from the weak points in the inputs, such as low learning time, low networking, or no willingness to compare external offers.
      • This is career planning support, not a job offer, salary guarantee, immigration advice, tax advice, or employment-contract review.
