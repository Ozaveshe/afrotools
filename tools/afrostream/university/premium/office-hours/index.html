Office Hours

Monthly live Q&A sessions. No prep required — just bring the problem you are stuck on. Open format, real answers, recorded replay for registered attendees.

What office hours is

A live session where your real question gets a real answer

Most creator advice is generic. Office Hours is the opposite — it is a live session where you can ask about your specific channel, your specific platform, your specific situation. No polished presentations. No broad advice that applies to everyone and helps no one. Just honest, direct answers to your actual questions.

Session format

How it works

1
Register and submit a question
When you register, you can optionally submit a question in advance. Questions are grouped by theme to make the session efficient.
2
Join live on the first Saturday of each month
Sessions run for 60 minutes. The first 45 minutes cover submitted questions. The last 15 are open for live questions in the session.
3
Receive the session replay
Registered attendees get the full replay link by email after the session — even if you could not attend live due to connectivity or schedule.
