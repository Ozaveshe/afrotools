Creator Audit

A written review of your entire creator setup — profile, positioning, content packaging, workflow, and monetization readiness — delivered with a prioritised action plan you can act on immediately.

What we review

Six areas, one honest assessment

We look at your actual creator output and setup — not just what you think you have. Six review areas, each scored with specific notes on what is strong and what needs work.

👤 Profile & first impression
Channel name, bio, banner, profile photo, and link placement — does your page make someone want to stay?
🎯 Positioning & niche clarity
Is it immediately clear who you are for and what you make? Does your positioning differentiate you from the crowd?
🎬 Content packaging
Titles, thumbnails, hooks, and format consistency — do your videos earn watch time or lose it in the first 15 seconds?
⚙ Workflow & systems
How you create, edit, repurpose, and distribute. Is your system sustainable or are you always starting from scratch?
📈 Monetization readiness
Income streams, pricing, rate card quality, media kit status — are you commercially ready or still leaving money on the table?
▷ AfroStream alignment
How well your profile is optimised for discovery on AfroStream — searchability, category clarity, and brand partnership signals.
