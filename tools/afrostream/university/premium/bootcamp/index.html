Program structure

4 weeks, one deliverable per week

Week 1
Foundation — Clarity, format, and schedule
Define your lane and niche. Choose your platform. Build your minimum content format. Lock in your 30-day schedule. Deliverable: written creator one-liner + content calendar set up in CreatorCalendar.
Week 2
Production — Setup, hooks, and packaging
Build your minimum viable setup. Learn the hook structure that earns watch time. Improve your titles and thumbnails. Deliverable: 3 pieces of content published with optimised titles and thumbnails.
Week 3
Distribution — Repurposing and multi-platform
Set up your repurposing workflow. Cross-post to one secondary platform. Extract clips, quotes, and captions from your Week 2 content. Deliverable: 5 pieces from one source — proven your repurposing system works.
Week 4
Monetization — Income setup and system lock-in
Review your income options. Set up one passive income method. Build or update your rate card. Document your complete creator workflow. Deliverable: written Month 2 plan + rate card ready to share.
