Benchmark framework

9 dimensions of creator readiness

Creator readiness is not one thing. It is a combination of clarity, operational discipline, commercial skill, and resilience. The benchmark measures these separately — so you know exactly where to focus.

Benchmark tiers

What each tier means for African creators

Note: These benchmark ranges are directional — based on creator stage patterns and assessment data across Streamer University users. They are not statistically validated population-level data. As more creators complete assessments, benchmarks will become more precise.
🥉 Bronze
0 – 20
Early-stage creator — still finding direction
Most new creators land here. Positioning is unclear, consistency is irregular, and no monetization systems exist yet. This is the starting point — not a failure. Focus: clarity, format, and publishing regularly.
🥈 Silver
21 – 40
Developing creator — building the fundamentals
You are posting, but not yet systematically. Some packaging skills exist but packaging is inconsistent. No reliable income yet. Focus: locking in a real schedule, improving packaging and hooks, and exploring first income options.
🥇 Gold
41 – 55
Structured creator — operating with systems
This is the target stage for most working creators. You post consistently, repurpose content, package yourself professionally, and have started generating income. Focus: growing income reliability, building commercial infrastructure, and diversifying platforms.
🌟 Elite
56 – 70
Commercially ready — brand-deal capable creator
You have a full operating system — consistent output, professional profile, monetization infrastructure, and brand-ready commercial materials. You are in the top tier of creator readiness on the continent. Focus: scaling income, building a team, or expanding into new revenue models.
