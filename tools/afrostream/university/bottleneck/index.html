Bottleneck Diagnostic

5 questions. Most creators are not stuck because of bad content. They are stuck because of one specific, fixable problem. Find yours now.

Step 1 of 5

Question 1

What feels hardest about your content right now?

Pick the one that feels most true — not the one that sounds best.

Question 2

How often do you repurpose content into clips, posts, or other formats?

Repurposing means taking one piece and turning it into others — clips, quotes, threads, shorts.

Question 3

What best describes your brand deal or client work status?

Be honest — this tells a lot about where your business packaging stands.

Question 4

Where does your content performance feel weakest?

Think about where you lose people — before they click, or after they start watching.

Question 5

How would you describe your current posting schedule?

This question reveals your system maturity — be honest about what you actually do.
