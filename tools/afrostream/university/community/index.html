Creator Community

Not a Discord where people spam links. A structured creator community built around goals, progress, and accountability — in small groups where you actually get seen.

Community philosophy

Built around outcomes, not chat

Most creator communities fail because they are open chatrooms with no structure. People drop in, spam content, get no useful feedback, and leave. This community is different. Every group has a clear goal. Every week has a structure. Every member has a role — learner, creator, or encourager. If you join to just lurk and consume, this is not for you.

How we organise

Three types of community spaces

👥
Creator Circles
Small groups of 8–12 creators at the same stage or creator type. Regular check-ins, shared wins and blockers, peer feedback.

Community rhythm

What happens every week

Community is not a suggestion. Active members show up to the weekly ritual — even when they only have 5 minutes.

📅
Weekly check-in (Monday)
One sentence: what is your main creator focus this week? Posted in your circle or goal room.
💬
Win / blocker update (Thursday)
One win this week. One thing still blocking you. Peers respond with feedback or encouragement.
🏭
Monthly live clinic (first Saturday)
Open Q&A session. Bring your current problem. Get live feedback in front of the group.
🏆
Monthly spotlight creator
One community member is featured for their progress, consistency, or milestone hit that month.
