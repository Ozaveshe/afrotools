Creator Stage Finder

6 questions. Know your exact creator stage, your biggest current risk, and the top three moves to make next. Takes about 90 seconds.

Step 1 of 6

Question 1

How long have you been creating content?

Count from when you started — even if you have not posted yet.

Question 2

How often do you post or go live?

Be honest — count what you actually do, not what you plan to.

Question 3

What is your total audience across all platforms?

Add up followers, subscribers, and regular viewers across everything.

Question 4

Where are you with monetization?

Any income that came from your content counts — tips, affiliate, brand deals, products.

Question 5

How would you describe your content workflow?

Think about how you plan, create, and publish — honestly.

Question 6

How prepared are you for brand deals or client work?

Think about whether you have the materials to pitch yourself professionally today.
