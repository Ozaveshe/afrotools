Creator Readiness Score

7 questions across 7 creator dimensions. Know exactly where you are strong, where you are weak, and what to build next. Score out of 70.

Step 1 of 7

Dimension 1 — Clarity

How well-defined is your niche and target audience?

Clarity means someone who sees your content immediately knows who it is for and what it is about.

Dimension 2 — Consistency

How consistent is your posting rhythm right now?

Count what you have actually done in the last 4 weeks, not what you plan to do.

Dimension 3 — Packaging

How polished is your content presentation — titles, thumbnails, hooks, and audio?

Packaging is how professional your content looks and sounds before and during the first few seconds.

Dimension 4 — Distribution

How actively are you distributing content across platforms?

Distribution means how many surfaces your content reaches — not just your main platform.

Dimension 5 — Monetization Readiness

How set up are you to earn money from your content?

This covers any monetization method — ads, affiliate, tips, brand deals, products, memberships.

Dimension 6 — Business Readiness

Do you have professional creator materials ready to send?

This means a media kit, rate card, and brand positioning — the materials a brand needs to say yes to you.

Dimension 7 — System Maturity

How systematized is your creator workflow?

A mature system means you plan, create, post, and repurpose in a repeatable, documented way — not ad hoc.
