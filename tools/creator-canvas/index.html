FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CreatorCanvas free?
Yes, completely free. All templates, design tools, and exports are available at no cost.
Can I upload my own images?
Yes. Tap the Image tool to upload from your gallery or camera. Images are automatically compressed for fast editing.
What formats can I export?
PNG (high quality) or JPG (smaller file, great for WhatsApp). You can also share directly to WhatsApp and Instagram.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes. Built mobile-first for Android and iPhone. The lightweight HTML/CSS editor works even on 2GB RAM devices and 3G.
How is this different from Canva?
Canva does everything for everyone. CreatorCanvas does 5 things exceptionally well for African creators — 10x faster, works on low-end phones, with authentically African templates.
Can I save my brand colors and fonts?
Yes. The Brand Kit feature lets you save up to 6 colors, your logo (light + dark), and primary/secondary fonts. New designs start on-brand automatically.
