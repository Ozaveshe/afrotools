Frequently Asked Questions

Does the calculator contain provider rates?
No. Every compared rate and flat fee comes from the values you enter, so you can use a current quote from any provider.
What is included in the monthly fee?
For QR, POS and mobile money it adds the entered percentage charge on volume to the entered flat charge per transaction. Cash uses the entered handling percentage.
Is the lowest result always the best option?
No. The result compares entered processing costs only. Also compare settlement time, reliability, customer access, hardware, taxes and contract terms.
