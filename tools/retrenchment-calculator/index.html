Frequently Asked Questions

Does this page confirm that retrenchment is lawful?

No. It only reconciles amounts entered. Consultation, notice, selection, alternatives, eligibility, and procedural fairness require separate legal review.

Why does severance use weekly pay?

The formula converts monthly eligible pay to weekly pay using monthly pay times 12 divided by 52, then applies the verified weeks per service year you enter.

Does AfroTools receive employee pay data?

No. This workflow runs locally in the browser and does not request employee identity details.
