Calculation contract

Annual gross earnings = annual base + recurring cash allowances + guaranteed annual bonus. Non-cash benefits and employer contributions are shown separately, then added only to the broader package total. Hourly gross divides annual gross earnings by entered weekly hours and paid weeks.

ILO method context

ILOSTAT wage guidance describes gross earnings as remuneration before employee deductions and distinguishes employer social-security or pension contributions. It does not supply the market figures entered here.

Privacy

No value is persisted, placed in a URL, sent to analytics, or transmitted to AI or an API. Avoid employer names and personal identifiers in private labels. CSV and PDF are created only after your action.

Limits

  • No role, country, city, recruiter, remote-work, skill, gender-gap, inflation, PPP or FX benchmark is supplied.
  • No tax, take-home pay, minimum wage, classification or legal compliance is calculated.
  • Benefit valuation and work time must be checked in the written terms.

Questions people ask

Does this contain market salary benchmarks?

No. Every amount comes from you because the old bundled dataset lacked adequate row-level evidence.

Can I compare different currencies?

Only after you convert both offers yourself using the same dated rate. The tool deliberately supplies no FX rate.

Is the higher package automatically better?

No. Consider tax, stability, leave, workload, location, risk, growth, equity terms and personal priorities separately.
