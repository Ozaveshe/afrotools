Frequently Asked Questions

Are the exchange rates live and up to date?
The AfroTools Currency Converter fetches rates from open forex APIs and updates them regularly. Rates are cached for performance and may have a short delay. These are indicative mid-market rates and may differ from actual bank or bureau de change rates.
How many African currencies are supported?
The converter supports 40+ African currencies including NGN, KES, ZAR, GHS, EGP, and many more, along with major global currencies like USD, EUR, and GBP.
Is the currency converter free to use?
Yes, completely free with no sign-up required. Convert between any supported currencies as many times as you want.
What is the difference between CBN rate and black market rate?
The Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) publishes an official exchange rate, while the parallel market rate can be significantly different. Our converter shows the mid-market rate, which typically falls between the official and parallel rates.
