    Select a country and project your pension to see AI-powered insights on the local pension system, contribution rules, tax benefits and retirement planning tips.
    ⚖️ HOW IT WORKS
    • Monthly contribution = Salary × contribution rate (employee + employer)
    • Fund growth = Previous balance × (1 + annual return / 12) + monthly contribution
    • Salary grows each year at your specified annual growth rate
    • Monthly pension estimated using 4% safe withdrawal rule (fund × 4% / 12)
    • Replacement ratio = Monthly pension ÷ final monthly salary
