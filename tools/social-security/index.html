📋 Verify Your Contributions Are Being Paid
Are you actually receiving the contributions deducted above?
  • 🧾
    Does your payslip show the exact deductions listed in the breakdown above? If different amounts appear, your employer may be deducting but not remitting the full amount.
  • 📱
    Log into your national social security portal and confirm contributions appear for the current month. Missing months are a red flag.
  • 📜
    Have you received a formal contribution statement in the last 12 months? Your social security authority should issue these. Request one if you haven’t.
If you find discrepancies: Contact your employer’s HR department and request written confirmation of remittance dates. If unresolved, file a formal complaint with your national social security authority. Keep all payslips showing deductions as evidence — these are your legal proof.
