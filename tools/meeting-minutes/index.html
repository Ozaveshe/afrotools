FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this free to use?
Yes. There is no registration, watermark, or paid export step.
Does it record audio?
No. This is a structured text tool for typed or pasted notes. It does not record, transcribe, or join meetings.
Can I save multiple meetings?
Yes. Saved minutes are stored locally in your browser and can be reopened from this page.
Can I track action status?
Yes. Each action can be open, in progress, or done, with owner, priority, due date, and source.
What formats can I export?
PDF, Word-compatible DOC, TXT, action-items CSV, next-meeting ICS, JSON backup, print, and copy.
Is my meeting data private?
Yes. The app runs locally in your browser. Data is not uploaded unless you choose to share it outside AfroTools.

Free Meeting Minutes Generator

Good meeting minutes do more than summarize a conversation. They preserve decisions, assign owners, clarify due dates, and give every participant the same record of what happens next. AfroTools helps teams create minutes that can move from discussion to follow-up without a complicated workspace or recording bot.

Structured for accountability

The generator separates attendees, agenda items, discussion notes, decisions, action items, risks, parking-lot topics, and next meeting details. The readiness score highlights missing owners, missing due dates, and incomplete decision capture before the minutes are shared.

Useful for African teams

Whether you are recording SME management meetings in Lagos, school committee minutes in Accra, project reviews in Nairobi, NGO donor updates in Kigali, or board minutes in Johannesburg, the workflow keeps the output practical and professional.

Private local workflow

Many AI meeting tools work by recording or joining meetings. AfroTools is different: it is a private local builder for typed notes. You stay in control of what is captured and what leaves your device.
