FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this free to use?
Completely free. No registration, no watermarks, unlimited minutes.
Can I share the minutes with my team?
Yes -- export as PDF and email it to all attendees. The PDF includes all sections: attendees, agenda, discussions, action items, and next meeting details.
Can I update action items later?
Yes, save your minutes and reload them anytime to update action item completion status.
What templates are available?
Board Meeting (formal with resolutions), Team Standup (brief with blockers), and Project Review (deliverables-focused). Plus a blank template.
Does it record audio?
No, this is a structured text tool. You type or paste notes during or after the meeting. For audio transcription, use a dedicated recording tool.
Is my data private?
100% private. Everything is stored in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server.

Free Meeting Minutes Generator

Good meeting minutes are the difference between productive follow-through and forgotten decisions. Yet most professionals still take minutes in unstructured Word documents or scattered notebook pages. The AfroTools Meeting Minutes Generator provides a structured framework that ensures every meeting produces clear, actionable documentation.

Why Structure Matters

The best meeting minutes follow a consistent format: attendees, agenda items with discussions and decisions, action items with owners and deadlines, and next meeting details. Our tool enforces this structure while remaining flexible enough for any type of meeting, from informal team standups to formal board meetings.

From Boardroom to Startup

Whether you're a company secretary documenting board resolutions in Lagos, a project manager tracking deliverables in Nairobi, or a team lead running weekly standups in Cape Town, professional minutes keep everyone aligned and accountable. Export clean PDFs to share with stakeholders, and save your minutes locally to track action item completion over time.
