Method and limits

  • Stock cost value = unit cost × quantity on hand. This is not FIFO, weighted-average valuation or COGS.
  • Potential sales = selling price × quantity. Potential gross profit = (selling price − unit cost) × quantity and may be negative.
  • Low stock means quantity on hand is less than or equal to your reorder point. Optional reorder units = max(target stock − quantity, 0).
  • No tax, VAT, FX rate, demand forecast, supplier price, lead time or purchase advice is supplied.
