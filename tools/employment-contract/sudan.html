Sudan Employment Contract — What the Law Requires

Sudan employment contracts are governed by Labour Act 1997 (as amended). All employers are required to provide written terms of employment. Key statutory requirements include: minimum wage of ج.س425/month, maximum 48 working hours per week, 20 days annual leave, and 8 weeks maternity leave (100% (employer)).

Employers in Sudan must make mandatory deductions for National Pension Fund (8% employee, 17% employer). The notice period for termination is 1-2 months. Employment disputes are resolved through the Labour Court. Severance pay: Per Labour Act.
