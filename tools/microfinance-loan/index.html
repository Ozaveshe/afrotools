Frequently Asked Questions

Does the calculator load lender rates?

No. Every rate and fee comes from the offer values you enter.

Why are fees deducted from proceeds?

The APR cash flow assumes mandatory upfront fees reduce the money available to you while repayments remain based on the stated loan amount.

Does it test affordability?

No. Compare the repayment with stable disposable income and essential expenses separately.

What should I copy from the offer?
Use the disbursed amount, rate period, flat or reducing method, repayment dates, mandatory fees, insurance, compulsory savings and total repayment.
Why might the lender's APR differ?
Different fee treatment, day counts, repayment dates, insurance or compulsory deposits change the cash flow. Prefer the lender's regulated disclosure when it includes every mandatory cost.
Can I get a loan without collateral?
Eligibility, guarantees, group liability and collateral rules are provider-specific. Confirm them in the current written terms; this calculator does not assess approval.

Limitations

  • Disclaimer: planning estimate only, not lending advice, credit approval, investment advice, official rate disclosure, or a loan offer.
  • Microfinance pricing can include flat fees, compulsory savings, insurance, group penalties, and non-rate costs that this simple calculator may not capture.
