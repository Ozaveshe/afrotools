FAQ

Common Questions

What sizes can I export?
16 preset sizes for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, X/Twitter, LinkedIn & Pinterest. Plus custom dimensions up to 4096px.
Do I need design experience?
Not at all. Pick a template, change the text, add your face, export. The templates are already optimized for clicks.
Can I use my own photos?
Yes. Upload any photo — the tool auto-suggests face-centered crop positions. Drag, resize, and clip to circles or rectangles.
Is ThumbnailForge free?
Completely free. All templates, fonts, export formats — no watermarks, no sign-up required to start designing.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes — designed mobile-first. Touch-friendly canvas, bottom sheet tools, swipe gestures. Design thumbnails from your phone.
What makes this different from Canva?
Purpose-built for African creators. Afrocentric templates, African patterns (Kente, Ankara, Mudcloth), cultural stickers, curated palettes. Free, no subscription, works offline.
