Tanzania mandatory third-party motor insurance costs TZS 118,000/year. NHIF contributions are 6% of basic salary (3% employer + 3% employee). A Universal Health Insurance system launching in 2026 will standardize coverage at TZS 150,000/year. The market is regulated by TIRA (Tanzania Insurance Regulatory Authority), with insurance penetration at 0.62% of GDP.
