Kenya Microinsurance Premium Calculator — What You Need to Know

Calculate microinsurance premiums in Kenya. Mobile money-linked insurance for informal sector — hospital cash, personal accident, funeral cover with local low-cost premium estimates. Use this free calculator to get a quick estimate before contacting insurance providers.

Kenya motor third-party costs KES 5,000-8,000/year. Comprehensive runs 3.5-7% of vehicle value. Since Oct 2024, NHIF was replaced by SHIF at 2.75% of gross salary with no cap. Major insurers include Jubilee, Britam, CIC, and APA. The market is regulated by IRA (Insurance Regulatory Authority), with insurance penetration at 2.4% of GDP.
