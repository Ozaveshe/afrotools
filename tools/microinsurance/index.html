Methodology

The estimator applies a simple planning rate to the insured amount, then adjusts it for country, cover type, household size, age band, deductible and selected risk factors.

  1. Base monthly premium = insured amount x cover rate.
  2. Country, age, household and risk factors adjust the base.
  3. The final number is shown as a range because insurer underwriting varies.

Next-step checklist

  • Confirm the licensed insurer, intermediary and claims channel.
  • Ask what is excluded, waiting periods, limits and documents required.
  • Check whether missed daily, weekly or mobile payments cancel cover.
  • Compare at least two quotes before buying.

Insurance disclaimer

This is an estimate only and not financial, legal or insurance advice. It does not replace a policy schedule, regulator notice, broker recommendation or licensed insurer quote.

  • Premiums can change by region, age, medical history and claims rules.
  • Some microinsurance products use fixed bundles, not percentage rates.
  • Always verify with a licensed insurer or regulator before payment.
