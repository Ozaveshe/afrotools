PDF eSignature Tool: How It Works

The AfroTools PDF eSignature Tool lets you add electronic signatures to PDF documents directly in your browser. Create your signature in three ways: draw it freehand using your mouse, trackpad, or touchscreen for a natural handwritten look; select from styled typed signature fonts for a clean, professional appearance; or upload an image of your existing signature. Once created, position your signature anywhere on any page of the PDF by clicking where you want it placed. Resize and adjust the signature until it fits perfectly on the signature line. You can sign multiple pages and add multiple signatures to the same document - useful for contracts, agreements, and forms that require initials on every page. The tool also lets you add the date alongside your signature. When you are finished, download the signed PDF ready to send. All processing happens client-side in your browser using JavaScript - your documents and signatures are never transmitted to any server. This tool is ideal for freelancers, business owners, remote workers, and anyone who needs to sign documents digitally without printing, signing by hand, and scanning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the ways to create a signature?

You can draw a freehand signature using your mouse or touchscreen, type your name and choose from styled signature fonts, or upload an image of your existing handwritten signature.

Is the electronic signature legally binding?

Electronic signatures are recognised as legally valid in many jurisdictions. However, requirements vary by country and document type. For high-value contracts or legal proceedings, consult with a legal professional about your specific jurisdiction's requirements.

Can I sign multiple pages in one document?

Yes. Navigate to any page in the PDF and place your signature. You can add signatures to as many pages as needed - useful for initialling every page of a contract or signing in multiple required locations.

Can I resize and reposition the signature?

Yes. After placing the signature, you can drag it to reposition and resize it to fit the signature area on your document perfectly.

Is my signature stored or sent to a server?

No. Your signature and document are processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Your signature data exists only in your browser session and is discarded when you close the page.
