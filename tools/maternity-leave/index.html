Methodology, workflow, and next steps

Calculation methodology: the tool selects statutory paid maternity or paternity days for the chosen country, converts monthly gross pay into average daily pay by dividing by 30.4375, applies your pay replacement assumption, and compares statutory baseline, planned employee request, employer policy target, and a second country.

HR workflow: confirm employee category, service length, start date, birth or adoption timing, social insurance claim process, and collective agreement; then agree any employer top-up and keep the exported summary in the payroll handoff packet.
