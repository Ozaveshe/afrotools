Methodology, workflow, and next steps

Calculation methodology: the tool converts monthly gross pay to a planning daily value by dividing by 30.4375, applies the verified minimum days and replacement rate you enter, and compares the employee request with the employer policy target. The divisor is an average-calendar-month planning convention, not a statutory payroll rule.

HR workflow: confirm employee category, service length, start date, birth or adoption timing, social insurance claim process, and collective agreement; then agree any employer top-up and keep the exported summary in the payroll handoff packet.
