💡 Agreement Tips
  • Have the agreement reviewed by a property lawyer before signing, especially for commercial or high-value leases
  • Both parties should sign every page, not just the signature page — prevents page substitution
  • Conduct a written inventory of the property condition before the tenant moves in
  • In South Africa, the deposit must go into an interest-bearing account — this is a legal requirement
  • In Nigeria, register the agreement at the land registry or stamp it at FIRS/LIRS for legal validity

Rental contract operational checklist

This generator should help users convert a rental deal into enforceable operating rules: rent due date, occupants, repairs, service charge, access, defaults, renewal and handover.

Decisions this clarifies

  • Whether the arrangement is a lease, short-let, room rental, commercial tenancy or licence
  • Which payment schedule and notice rules fit the jurisdiction
  • How repairs, utilities, service charge and shared facilities will be managed

Before you rely on it

  • Verify landlord authority and property title before paying
  • Attach receipts, ID copies, condition schedule and house rules
  • Put renewal, rent review and exit inspection dates in the contract

Red flags

  • Agent signs but cannot prove landlord authority
  • Service charges excluded from the agreement but demanded later
  • No default steps before lockout, eviction or deposit deductions
