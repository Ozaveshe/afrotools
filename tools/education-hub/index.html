Scholarship Tips

1. Start early. Most major scholarships have deadlines 9-12 months before the start date.

2. Apply widely. Target 5-10 scholarships that match your profile. Don't put all eggs in one basket.

3. Tell your story. Focus on leadership, community impact, and how you'll use education to contribute to Africa.

4. Meet minimums. If a scholarship requires IELTS 6.5 and you have 6.0, take the test again before applying.

5. Use our tools. Calculate your GPA accurately, check your IELTS band, and use match scoring to find your best-fit scholarships.
