Trade-finance planning model

Reviewed 2026. Fees, FX, margin rules, and compliance checks can change by bank and corridor.

Methodology

  • Compares instrument cost, processing time, buyer risk, seller risk, and cash-margin impact for a trade value.
  • Advisor answers map supplier trust, order size, credit need, and trade corridor to a suggested instrument.
  • CSV export preserves the local estimate for bank, supplier, broker, or finance review.

Before acting

  • Verify bank tariff, confirmation charges, FX, margin, document wording, sanctions screening, and insurance.
  • Confirm supplier history, shipping documents, inspection, import permits, and dispute process in writing.
  • Treat the result as an estimate only, not legal advice, tax advice, or a financing approval.
