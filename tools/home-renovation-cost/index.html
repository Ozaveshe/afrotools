💡 Renovation Budgeting Tips

✅Always add 15–20% contingency to your budget. Renovations almost always go over estimate.
✅Get at least 3 quotes from licensed contractors and check references.
✅Stage your renovation — do structural work first, then electrical, then finishes.
✅Buy materials yourself where possible. Contractor markups on materials are typically 20–40%.
✅Kitchen and bathroom renovations give the best return on investment (40–60% of cost).
✅Never pay more than 30% upfront. Pay in milestones tied to work completed.

These rates cover all labour and standard local materials. Imported materials add 30–60%.
