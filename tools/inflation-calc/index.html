Evidence checklist

  1. Prefer the national statistics office or central bank.
  2. Check whether the figure is annual, monthly or year-on-year.
  3. Match the geography and reference period.
  4. Record the publication and your checked date.

A constant-rate scenario is arithmetic, not a forecast. Future inflation can differ sharply.
