Method and limits

Administrative readiness, not eligibility

Why is there no eligibility score?

A generic score would invent lender policy and duplicate the affordability tools. This checklist reports only the status you assign to evidence.

Should I upload my statements here?

No. This page has no upload. Send sensitive documents only through the lender’s verified channel.

Does “not requested” mean unnecessary?

Only for the application you are tracking, after the lender confirms it. Requirements can change by applicant, property and lender.
