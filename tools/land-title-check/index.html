    💡 General Tips
    • Never rely on photocopies of title documents — always verify directly at the registry
    • Hire your OWN independent lawyer, not the one recommended by the seller or agent
    • Visit the land physically with a licensed surveyor before paying any deposit
    • If a deal seems too good to be true at an African price, it probably is
    • Check for active court cases (lis pendens) at the High Court registry
