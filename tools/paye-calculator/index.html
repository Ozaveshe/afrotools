Why this is a directory, not one Africa-wide calculator

PAYE is a way of withholding employment income tax, but each jurisdiction defines its own bands, reliefs, pension or social-security deductions, filing periods and effective dates. A single continental formula would be misleading. This page therefore routes to accepted country engines and performs no payroll arithmetic itself.

Before relying on a country result

  • Check the tax year, last-reviewed date and named source on the calculator.
  • Confirm whether the salary is monthly or annual and whether it includes taxable benefits.
  • Review voluntary deductions separately from statutory deductions.
  • Use the result as a planning estimate, not an official assessment or filing.

Need to browse by region or payroll task? Visit the Salary & Tax hub. PDF export is not applicable on this directory because it produces no calculation or report.

PAYE directory questions

Does one PAYE formula work across Africa?

No. Tax bands, reliefs, statutory contributions and tax years differ by country. Select the payroll country first.

Does this page store my salary?

No. This directory does not ask for or store a salary. It only resolves a country to a calculator route.

Why is Guinea-Bissau unsupported?

We have not published a country calculator with a verified source owner for that route. The directory will not substitute another country’s rules or invent a result.
