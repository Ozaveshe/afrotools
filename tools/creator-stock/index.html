FAQ

Common questions

Are the photos and videos really free?

Yes. All media comes from Unsplash, Pexels, and Pixabay which offer free-to-use licenses. Most are free for commercial and personal use without attribution required, though attribution is always appreciated.

Are there API rate limits?

Each source has its own rate limits (typically 50–100 requests per hour). CreatorStock pools results from all three sources so you rarely hit limits.

How does the African creators spotlight work?

We curate a list of talented African photographers. Clicking a spotlight card searches for their work, surfacing their best content first.

Can I search for videos too?

Yes. Toggle the media type filter to Videos to search for free stock video clips from Pexels and Pixabay. Unsplash is photo-only.

What are collections?

Collections let you save and organize photos into named groups — like mood boards. They are stored locally in your browser.

Do I need to credit the photographer?

Attribution is not legally required for most photos, but it is encouraged. CreatorStock provides a one-click "Copy Attribution" button for every photo.
