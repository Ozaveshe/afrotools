    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is my medical data safe?
    Yes. All lab result parsing and reference range comparison happens entirely in your browser. Your medical data is never stored on any server. The only data sent externally is to the AI for deeper interpretation, and it is not stored.
    Can I upload a photo of my lab report?
    Yes. You can upload a photo (JPG or PNG) or take a picture directly with your phone camera. The tool uses OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text from your lab report image, then analyzes the results automatically.
    Is this a medical diagnosis?
    No. This tool is for educational purposes only. It helps you understand what your lab results mean in plain language, but it is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider about your results.
    What tests are supported?
    The tool supports 80+ biomarkers across Complete Blood Count (CBC), Lipid Panel (Cholesterol), Liver Function Tests (LFT), Kidney Function Tests (KFT), Thyroid Panel, Diabetes Panel (HbA1c/Glucose), Iron Studies, Vitamins, Cardiac Markers, Urinalysis, and more. New biomarkers are added regularly.
    How accurate is the interpretation?
    The reference ranges used are standard medical ranges recognized by major health organizations. The AI provides general guidance to help you understand your results in plain language. However, reference ranges can vary by lab, age, sex, and other factors. Always discuss your specific results with your doctor for personalized interpretation.
