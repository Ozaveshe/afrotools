Every platform has rules. We follow them.

Instagram

Hook in first 125 chars. Proper line breaks. 5-15 hashtags separated from caption. CTA built in.

⚠ 2,200 char limit

𝕏

X / Twitter

Hard 280-char limit. Punchy, short sentences. Minimal hashtags. Thread option for longer content.

⚠ 280 char limit

LinkedIn

Strong first 2 lines. Short paragraphs. Professional tone. Engagement question at the end.

⚠ 3,000 char limit

TikTok

Super short (150 chars). Emoji-heavy. Hashtags are crucial for discovery. Trend-aware.

Facebook

FAQ

Common questions

How is CaptionCraft different from ChatGPT?

CaptionCraft knows platform rules — Instagram's 125-char preview, X's 280-char limit, LinkedIn's "see more" fold. It outputs 3 variations in platform-realistic previews so you see how your caption will look.

Which platforms does it support?

Instagram, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Facebook. Each has its own formatting rules, character limits, and hashtag placement.

Can I rewrite an existing caption?

Yes. Switch to "Rewrite Existing", paste your caption, and get 3 improved versions instantly.

How many captions can I generate per day?

Free users get 10 generations/day. Each generation gives 3 variations. Limits reset at midnight.

Does it generate hashtags?

Yes — grouped by reach (high, medium, niche). Toggle individual hashtags before copying.

Is this free?

Yes. All platforms, tones, and features included. 10 generations per day.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We do not store your captions or briefs on our servers. All processing happens in real-time and nothing is saved after you leave the page. We never use your content for training.

Does it support multiple languages?

CaptionCraft works best in English but can generate captions with mixed-language phrases common across African social media — Pidgin, Swahili, Yoruba, and more. Just mention your language preference in the brief.

Is there an API for bulk generation?

Not yet, but an API for bulk caption generation is on our roadmap. Join the waitlist on the app page to be notified when it launches.
