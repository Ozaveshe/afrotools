Nollywood Budget Rule
Standard Nollywood allocations: Above-the-line 30% (cast, director, producers), Production 45% (crew, locations, equipment, catering), Post-production 15% (editing, sound, VFX, colour), Marketing 10%. A Nollywood feature that grosses 3x its production budget is considered a hit.
