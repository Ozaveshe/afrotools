Frequently Asked Questions

What drives crop insurance premium?

Premiums usually depend on insured crop value, crop risk, district, peril covered, farm history, and subsidy support.

Is this an official quote?

No. It is a planning estimate to help compare risk and affordability before contacting an insurer or public scheme.

What should farmers check before paying?

Check covered perils, claim evidence, waiting period, excess, payout trigger, and whether weather-index data is used.
