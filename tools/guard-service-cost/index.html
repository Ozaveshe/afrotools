Important Legal Note (Nigeria)
All private security companies in Nigeria must be licensed by NSCDC (Nigeria Security and Civil Defence Corps). Armed guards must have written approval from the Inspector General of Police or State Commissioner. Unlicensed security operations attract penalties up to ₦500,000 and imprisonment. Always verify your security company's NSCDC licence number.
