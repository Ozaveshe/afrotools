Frequently Asked Questions

What currencies are supported?
All major African currencies (NGN, KES, ZAR, GHS, EGP, TZS, UGX, RWF, XOF, XAF) plus USD, EUR, GBP, and more. Over 40 currencies total.
Can I use this as a final tax invoice?
Use it as a document draft. Confirm tax registration, numbering, VAT, withholding, client wording, and recordkeeping requirements before issuing the final copy.
Is my client data private?
Treat client and payment details as sensitive. Keep drafts local unless you intentionally sign in, save, export, copy, or share them.
Can I use this for my agency?
Yes. CreatorInvoice works for solo creators and agencies that need reusable client details, clear line items, and professional exports.
How should I send the invoice?
Export the PDF or copy a client-ready summary, then send it through your own email, chat, payment link, or client portal.
Is CreatorInvoice free?
Yes, CreatorInvoice is completely free. Create unlimited invoices, quotes, manage clients, export PDFs - all at no cost.

What to check

  • Verify VAT or sales-tax treatment, registration numbers, withholding, payment links, exchange rates, and client purchase-order requirements before sending.

Limitations

  • Disclaimer: document builder only, not accounting advice, tax filing, payment collection, legal review, or official e-invoicing submission.
  • Tax rates, withholding rules, invoice format requirements, and payment-provider terms can change by country and client type.
