FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What currencies are supported?
All major African currencies (NGN, KES, ZAR, GHS, EGP, TZS, UGX, RWF, XOF, XAF) plus USD, EUR, GBP, and more. Over 40 currencies total.
Are these invoices legally valid?
Yes. Each invoice includes all required elements: unique invoice number, dates, tax details, line items, and totals. They meet standard business invoicing requirements.
Is my client data private?
Absolutely. All data is stored securely in your personal account. Shared invoice links only show the invoice itself — never your other business data.
Can I use this for my agency?
Yes. CreatorInvoice works for solo creators and agencies alike. Manage multiple clients, track outstanding payments, and maintain a professional image.
How does WhatsApp sharing work?
One tap generates a pre-formatted WhatsApp message with a professional summary and a shareable link. Your client can view and download the invoice on any device.
Is CreatorInvoice free?
Yes, CreatorInvoice is completely free. Create unlimited invoices, quotes, manage clients, export PDFs — all at no cost.
