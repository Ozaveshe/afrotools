Safety notes

    Price-check notes

      Sources & verification

      Last verified: 15 May 2026. Starter LPG prices are editable reference assumptions from the AfroTools Energy & Utilities data snapshot and country pages.

      LPG retail prices vary by city, dealer, delivery, cylinder ownership, subsidy rules, and fill weight. Confirm the refill price with a licensed dealer and relevant national energy regulator or petroleum authority before buying.

      Methodology

      1. If both scale weights are entered, delivered LPG equals filled weight minus tare weight; otherwise the rated cylinder size is used.
      2. Price per kg equals refill price divided by the delivered-LPG basis.
      3. Meal model: meals per day times cooking days times kg per meal.
      4. Hour model: burner hours times active burners times kg per burner-hour.
      5. The planning buffer is added before daily, weekly, and monthly cost are calculated.
      6. Refill forecast divides measured delivered LPG, or the rated-size fallback, by average daily LPG use.
