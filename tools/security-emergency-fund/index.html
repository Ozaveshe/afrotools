Where to Keep Your Security Emergency Fund
Keep your security emergency fund in a high-yield savings account — separate from your regular emergency fund. In Nigeria: PiggyVest, Cowrywise (10–15% p.a.). In Kenya: M-Shwari, KCB Goal. In South Africa: 32-day notice account. Avoid keeping large cash at home — that itself is a security risk.
