🤝 NEGOTIATION TIPS
  • Commission is always negotiable — most agents start high
  • For properties above a threshold, ask for 1–2% reduction
  • Offer a sole mandate (exclusivity) in exchange for a lower rate
  • Negotiate the renewal fee down to half the letting fee
  • Get the commission rate in writing in the mandate agreement
  • Ask if VAT is included in their quoted rate — always clarify
