Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are these exchange rates? ▼

Our rates are based on real market data as of 2026 and are updated regularly. However, providers may charge different rates based on the amount, payment method, and current market conditions. Always verify the exact rate with your provider before confirming a transfer.

Which provider is cheapest for my transfer? ▼

This depends on the amount and corridor. For small amounts, services like Remitly may be competitive. For larger amounts over $1000, Wise typically offers the best mid-market rates. Use our comparator to get a personalized recommendation for your specific transfer.

What's the difference between exchange rate and spread? ▼

The exchange rate is the official conversion between two currencies. The spread is the markup a provider adds to make a profit. A lower spread means you get closer to the mid-market rate. Wise is known for low spreads (0.5-1%), while traditional services like Western Union have wider spreads (3-5%).

How long do transfers take? ▼

Transfer times vary by provider and destination. Bank transfers typically take 1-3 business days, while cash pickup services (Western Union, MoneyGram) can be same-day in many corridors. Digital wallets like Chipper Cash offer instant transfers in Africa.

Is my money safe with these providers? ▼

All providers listed here are regulated and compliant with international money transfer regulations. Wise, Western Union, and MoneyGram are all established global companies. For digital services like Remitly and Sendwave, check that they're licensed in your country before use.

Can I compare providers for a specific corridor? ▼

Yes! Simply enter your send amount, select your source country and currency, choose the recipient country, and click "Compare Rates". The tool will show you all available providers for that specific corridor with their exact fees and rates.
