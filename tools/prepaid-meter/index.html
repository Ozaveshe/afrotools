Frequently Asked Questions

Why did my prepaid units drop?

Tariff change, debt recovery, VAT, fixed charges, arrears, or leaving a lifeline band can reduce units.

Is this a token generator?

No. It only estimates kWh from a purchase amount. Tokens must come from licensed vending channels.

What should I save for complaints?

Keep the receipt, token, meter number, tariff class, vending date, and a photo of the meter units before loading.
