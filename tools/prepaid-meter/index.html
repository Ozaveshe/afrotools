Frequently Asked Questions

Why did my prepaid electricity units drop?

A tariff or service-band change, VAT or levies, fixed charges, arrears, meter debt recovery, leaving a lifeline band, or a different vending channel can reduce delivered units. Compare the receipt breakdown with the current utility or regulator tariff.

Is this a prepaid electricity token generator?

No. It only estimates expected kWh and receipt variance. Buy tokens through a licensed vending channel, and never paste a token number or meter number into this calculator.

What evidence should I save for a prepaid vending complaint?

Keep the original receipt, token record, meter number, tariff or service class, vending date and channel, payment record, and a photo of the meter units before loading. Do not paste those identifiers into this calculator.
