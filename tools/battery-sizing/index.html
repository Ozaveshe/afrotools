Battery & Inverter Sizing FAQ

Why does LiFePO4 need less nameplate capacity?

This planning model uses 85% usable depth of discharge for LiFePO4 and 50% for lead-acid. The same backup energy therefore requires a larger lead-acid nameplate bank.

Why is the inverter recommendation the same for both?

The inverter is sized from peak load, not battery chemistry. The model adds 30% load headroom and rounds upward to the next 0.5 kVA; an installer must still verify exact motor and compressor surge.

What is excluded from this comparison?

It does not model a specific brand, BMS current limit, temperature derating, battery aging, cable loss, warranty terms, replacement labour, financing, or an exact installer quote.
