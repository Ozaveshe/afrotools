FAQ

Common Questions

What size are the carousel slides?
Instagram Portrait (1080x1350) is the default — takes up maximum feed space. Also supports Square (1080x1080) and LinkedIn Landscape (1280x720).
How many slides can I create?
Up to 10 slides per carousel — matching Instagram's limit. Each slide is fully customizable with text, colors, images, and branding.
Can I add my brand colors and logo?
Yes. Set your brand colors, font, and handle once — every template respects your branding across all slides automatically.
Is CarouselStudio free?
Completely free. All templates, fonts, export formats — no watermarks, no sign-up required to start creating.
How do I export my carousel?
Export all slides as a ZIP with individual PNG/JPEG images. Or export single slides. Ready to upload to Instagram or LinkedIn.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes — designed mobile-first. Swipe between slides, edit with bottom sheets, batch export. Create carousels from your phone.
