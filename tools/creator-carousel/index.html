FAQ

Common Questions

What sizes can I create carousels in?
8 formats: IG Portrait (1080x1350), Square (1080x1080), TikTok/IG Story (1080x1920), LinkedIn, LinkedIn Landscape, Facebook, X/Twitter, and Pinterest.
How many slides can I create?
Up to 10 slides per carousel — matching Instagram's limit. Each slide is fully customizable with text, colors, stickers, images, and branding.
Can I save and resume my designs?
Yes. Built-in file manager lets you save, name, duplicate, and resume any carousel. Your work is always waiting for you.
Is CarouselStudio free?
Completely free. All 14 templates, African stickers, export formats — no watermarks, no sign-up required to start creating.
What makes this different from Canva?
Purpose-built for African creators. Afrocentric templates, African stickers (flags, culture, food, music), curated palettes. Free, no subscription, works with ThumbnailForge.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes — designed mobile-first. Swipe between slides, edit with bottom sheets, add stickers, batch export. Create carousels from your phone.
