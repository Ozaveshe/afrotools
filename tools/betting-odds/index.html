Frequently Asked Questions

What is implied probability?

It is the probability suggested by the odds. Decimal odds of 2.00 imply about 50% before bookmaker margin.

Does positive edge guarantee profit?

No. It only compares your assumption with the odds. The event can still lose.

What should users avoid?

Chasing losses, borrowing to bet, betting under pressure, and treating this calculator as a prediction.
