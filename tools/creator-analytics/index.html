FAQ

Common questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your analytics stay on your device.

Which platforms does it support?

Instagram, X/Twitter, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, and Facebook. Log posts from any platform and compare performance across all six.

How is engagement rate calculated?

Engagement rate = (likes + comments + shares + saves) / reach × 100. This is the industry-standard formula used by most analytics tools.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Export all your logged posts as a CSV file at any time, including all metrics and calculated engagement rates.

Does it show benchmarks?

Yes. Industry-average engagement rate benchmarks per platform are shown so you can compare your performance to typical numbers.

Do I need an account?

No. CreatorAnalytics works instantly with no signup. All data is stored in your browser. Just open the app and start logging.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why not focus only on views?

Views can be passive. Saves, clicks, comments, watch time, and conversions show stronger intent.

How often should I review content?

Weekly is enough for patterns; daily swings can mislead unless you are testing a campaign.

What should I compare?

Topic, hook, format, platform, post time, retention, saves, clicks, and audience source.
