SARS 2027 scale

  • R0–R1,210,000: 0%.
  • R1,210,001–R1,663,800: 3% above R1,210,000.
  • R1,663,801–R2,329,300: R13,614 + 6% above R1,663,800.
  • R2,329,301–R2,994,800: R53,544 + 8% above R2,329,300.
  • R2,994,801–R13,310,000: R106,784 + 11% above R2,994,800.
  • Above R13,310,000: R1,241,456 + 13% above R13,310,000.

SARS says the 2027 table has no changes from the rates effective 1 April 2025.

Calculation boundary

This calculator covers transfer duty only. It excludes VAT, conveyancing fees, Deeds Office fees, bond costs, valuation fees, municipal clearance, penalties, interest and statutory exemptions. “Subject to VAT” is a user-supplied fact, not a decision by AfroTools.

Official sources reviewed 23 July 2026

  • SARS transfer-duty rates provides the 2027 table effective 1 April 2026.
  • SARS eFiling guide explains consideration, fair value, other consideration and rate applicability.
  • National Treasury Budget 2026 Tax Guide confirms transfer duty applies to transactions not subject to VAT.

Value used

The estimate uses the greater of declared fair market value and total consideration. Total consideration adds the purchase amount and any other amount entered. Ask the conveyancer which values belong in the SARS declaration.

Privacy

No input is stored, put in a URL or sent to analytics, AI or an API. Do not enter party names, tax numbers, property addresses or agreement documents. CSV, JSON and PDF are created locally.
