Frequently Asked Questions

Why charge separately for editing?

Editing is skilled time and often takes longer than the shoot. Hiding it makes quotes unprofitable.

What belongs in a client quote?

Deliverables, timeline, location, crew, revisions, payment milestones, usage rights, and cancellation terms.

When should I charge a rush fee?

Charge rush fees when the deadline compresses editing, staffing, transport, or other booked work.
