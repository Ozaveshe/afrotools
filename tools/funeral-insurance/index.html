Methodology and assumptions

The calculation starts with the funeral budget, adds a small family-size buffer, subtracts existing savings, then estimates a monthly premium range using broad funeral-cover rate factors by market and age band. It is a planning estimate, not an insurer quote. Real premiums depend on underwriting, maximum cover, waiting period, relationship rules, claims history, group scheme terms, and local regulation.

Savings versus insurance

If savings can close the gap within the selected period, the family may choose to keep saving and still hold a smaller policy for speed of payout. If the gap stays large, compare funeral cover quotes from licensed providers.
