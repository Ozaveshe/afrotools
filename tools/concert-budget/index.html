Frequently Asked Questions

What costs are often missed?

Security, generators, fuel, ticketing fees, artist hospitality, permits, cleanup, insurance, and refunds.

Why do sponsors matter?

Sponsors reduce break-even pressure, but deliverables and payment timing must be written down.

What should I calculate before deposits?

Break-even tickets, worst-case attendance, refund exposure, artist cancellation terms, and cash reserve.
