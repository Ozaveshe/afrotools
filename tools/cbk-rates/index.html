Common questions

Does this page fetch rates?

No. It makes no FX-data request and has no bundled, cached or fallback rate.

How is the result calculated?

Foreign amount ÷ quoted units × entered CBK Mean rate = KES reference amount. The exact values appear in the result.

Is the result a final exchange quote?

No. Banks, bureaus and transfer services can apply spreads and fees.
