EV Adoption in Africa — Cost Analysis

Electric vehicles are gaining ground in South Africa, Egypt, Morocco, and Kenya. With local electricity tariffs typically far below the per-km equivalent of petrol, EVs offer significant running cost savings. This calculator uses real local tariff data to give you an accurate cost comparison.

Disclaimer: Calculations assume standard EV efficiency of 17kWh/100km. Actual consumption varies by model, driving style, and temperature.
