Authentic Kente Tip
Authentic hand-woven Kente from Bonwire, Ghana costs GHS 250–1,500 per yard. Mass-produced "Kente-print" fabric from factories costs GHS 15–50. When buying for export or gifting, always specify "authentic hand-woven" and get a certificate of origin for customs purposes. Kente exported to the US or Europe commands 3–8x the local price.
