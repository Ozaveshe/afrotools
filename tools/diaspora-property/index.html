Diaspora Investment Considerations
  • FX risk: African currencies tend to depreciate against USD/GBP/EUR. This increases your buying power when purchasing, but reduces rental yield in foreign currency terms over time.
  • Remote management: Budget 10–15% for a property manager if you\'re based abroad. Use the Property Management Fee Calculator.
  • Title verification: Critical for diaspora buyers who can\'t visit frequently. Use the Land Title Verification Checklist.
  • Repatriation: Verify you can send rental income abroad. Nigeria has CBN restrictions on FX repatriation; Kenya and South Africa are more straightforward.
  • Tax obligations: Rental income may be taxable in BOTH your country of residence AND the property\'s country. Check for Double Taxation Agreements (DTAs).
  • Legal representation: Always have YOUR OWN lawyer in the property\'s country. Never use the seller\'s lawyer.
