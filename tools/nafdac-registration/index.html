Application evidence checklist

  • Open the exact current guideline for the product category and local/imported route.
  • Confirm whether one application is required for each product or variant.
  • Reconcile applicant/company evidence, product ownership or authority, labels/artwork, formulation or technical dossier, manufacturing/facility evidence, and certificates of analysis against that guideline.
  • Use NAPAMS for the official application and retain submission, payment, query-response and approval records.
  • Do not manufacture, import, advertise, sell or distribute a regulated product on the strength of this planning worksheet.
