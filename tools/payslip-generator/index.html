What is calculated

Gross earnings equal the entered earning lines. Cash paid adds reimbursements. Net pay subtracts every entered deduction. Employer cost adds the separate employer contribution without reducing employee pay.

What is not calculated

No PAYE band, pension rate, insurance cap, minimum wage, leave balance or legal entitlement is inferred. Confirm those values in the applicable payroll and legal process.
