Side-hustle shortlist questions

Does the highest score mean highest profit?

No. It means only that the idea fits more of the skill, time and capital bands selected. Profit depends on real demand, price, cost, execution and risk that this static tool does not know.

Why are there no income ranges?

Unsourced country ranges become stale quickly and can hide major differences in price, hours, costs and experience. Build a local revenue-and-cost test from actual customer conversations and quotes instead.

What should I test first?

Define one narrow paid offer, ask several suitable buyers, record objections, price the full cost, and stop or adjust if the evidence does not support further spending.
