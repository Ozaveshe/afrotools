What serious solar ROI calculators usually ask for
  • Electricity use or bill, location or peak sun hours, and system size.
  • Daily load, peak running load, generator size, and backup target when outages matter.
  • Efficiency losses, panel wattage, battery depth of discharge, inverter headroom, costs, maintenance, and financing.

Sources & verification before purchase
  • Use a recent electricity bill and fuel receipt, then verify with a written installer quote.
  • Check panel orientation, shade, roof condition, inverter headroom, battery depth, warranty, and maintenance terms.
