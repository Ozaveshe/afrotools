Visa Application Tracker — UK, Canada, Australia, USA, UAE FAQ

Does this show my official case status?

No. It organises your dates and the processing range you enter; only the official destination portal can show case status.

Where do the processing dates come from?

You copy the current minimum and maximum from the official portal. AfroTools does not maintain a hidden processing-time table.

Where is my tracker saved?

Only in this browser, and only after you choose Save on this device. Delete local data removes that saved copy.
