FAQ

Common questions

What types of content can I add?

5 lesson types: Video (YouTube/Vimeo embed), Text (rich formatting), PDF (link to documents), Quiz (multiple choice with pass marks), and Assignment (text prompts). Organize into modules and drag to reorder.

How do students pay?

Set your price in any supported currency (NGN, KES, ZAR, GHS, USD). Share your auto-generated sales page link. Create discount codes with percentage or fixed discounts and expiry dates.

What does the student experience look like?

Students see a professional sales page with curriculum, instructor bio, testimonials, and pricing. After enrolling, they progress through modules with optional drip scheduling.

How do certificates work?

Auto-generated on completion with student name, course name, date, and instructor name. Classic, modern, or elegant border styles. Downloadable as PDF.

Can I offer free courses?

Yes. Set price to 0. All features (quizzes, certificates, drip content, analytics) work the same for free courses.

What analytics do I get?

Enrolled count, completion rate, revenue, average quiz scores, completion time. Charts for enrollment trends and lesson completion. Individual student progress views.
