See what one selling day produced without mixing sold stock with spoilage. Enter your own currency label, prices, quantities and expenses; AfroTools adds no tax, platform fee or exchange rate.

Plan today’s stall result

Nothing is saved unless you choose Save locally. Changing an input makes the result stale until you calculate again.

Method and assumptions

  • Revenue = selling price × units sold.
  • Net daily profit = revenue − sold-stock cost − stock-loss cost − operating expenses.
  • Same-mix break-even revenue covers stock loss and expenses at the day’s contribution ratio. It is unavailable when contribution is zero or negative.
  • The monthly scenario repeats the same day for the number of market days you enter. It is a planning estimate, not accounting, tax or financial advice.

Questions traders ask

Are spoiled units counted twice?

No. Sold-stock cost uses units sold; stock-loss cost uses only units lost or spoiled.

Can I use any currency?

Yes. Enter a short display label such as KES, GHS, XOF or TZS. The planner does not convert money.

Can I export the calculation?

Yes. Copy a summary or download CSV, JSON and a text-based PDF. Print remains available if the PDF library cannot load.
