Route fare planning questions

Does this show the official fare?

No. The calculator uses the fare you enter. Confirm the current price and any luggage, transfer, peak-time or booking fees before travelling.

What counts as a ride?

Each separate paid boarding counts as one ride. A direct return commute is usually two rides per day; a trip with one paid transfer each way is usually four.

Can I make a PDF?

Yes. Calculate first, choose “Print / save PDF,” then use your browser’s PDF destination. The text download is a simpler portable alternative.

Before relying on the result

  • Confirm the fare on the day of travel.
  • Ask whether peak time, luggage or cashless payment changes the price.
  • Count every paid transfer in “rides per day.”
  • Keep emergency transport outside the normal commute budget.

Method

(Fare + extras) × rides per day × travel days, plus the contingency percentage you choose. The previous fare is used only for the change comparison.
