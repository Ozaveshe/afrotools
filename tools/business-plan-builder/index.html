Method and boundaries

  • Gross contribution is monthly revenue minus monthly variable costs.
  • Operating profit subtracts monthly fixed operating costs. It does not automatically include tax, interest, owner drawings or depreciation.
  • Same-mix break-even revenue divides fixed costs by the positive contribution ratio.
  • Simple payback divides startup plus working-capital need by positive operating profit. It is undiscounted and is not break-even.
  • Low and high scenarios change revenue by your percentage, hold the base variable-cost ratio constant and keep fixed costs fixed.

This workshop does not determine fundability or replace programme forms, market research, accounting records or professional review.

Choosing the right AfroTools workflow

Use this workshop for a concise private first draft. Use the advanced Business Plan app when you need a longer document workflow with templates and review. Use the Business Registration Planner for registration, licences and setup steps.
