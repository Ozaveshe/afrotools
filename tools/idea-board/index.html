Method and limits

What this explorer does—and does not do

Normalizes records

Rows must contain a valid idea name, country and supported sector. Invalid or duplicate rows are excluded rather than guessed.

Labels estimates

Starting cost, monthly revenue and break-even are displayed as submitted planning estimates. Missing values remain visibly missing.

Shows provenance gaps

The current reviewed public projection supplies no provenance fields, so their absence is disclosed. Optional fields appear only under a future reviewed response contract.

Keeps comparison local

The shortlist is written only after your explicit action. Export or clear it before leaving a shared device.

Questions before you compare

Are the costs and revenue figures verified?

No. They are submitted planning estimates. The current reviewed view supplies no provenance fields, so source, date and confidence are marked as not supplied.

Does the shortlist leave my browser?

No. A shortlist is stored only after an explicit add or import action, and can be cleared from this browser.

Does this tool use AI to recommend a business?

No. It filters available evidence records and does not send your search or shortlist to an AI service.
