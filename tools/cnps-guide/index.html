CNPS Côte d'Ivoire Guide 2026 — Rates, e-CNPS & Registration

What the Caisse Nationale de Prévoyance Sociale covers, the contribution rates employers and employees pay, and how the e-CNPS portal works. AfroTools is independent — official actions happen on cnps.ci.

Last reviewed: July 2026

Quick answer: CNPS Côte d'Ivoire (CNPS-CI) is the social security institution for private-sector workers, covering retirement, family benefits, maternity and work injury. Employees contribute 6.3% of gross salary for retirement; employers pay 7.7% retirement plus family, maternity and sector-based accident rates. Employers register, declare wages and pay online via e-CNPS at cnps.ci. Estimate your deductions with the free Côte d'Ivoire PAYE calculator.

What CNPS covers

  • Retirement pension — funded by the combined 14% employee + employer retirement contribution.
  • Family benefits — allowances for declared employees’ children.
  • Maternity cover — daily allowances during maternity leave for declared workers.
  • Work-injury insurance — medical care and compensation after workplace accidents or occupational illness.

How employers register and declare (e-CNPS)

  1. Register the business with CNPS to get an employer number (immatriculation employeur).
  2. Declare each employee (immatriculation travailleur) — workers get a CNPS social security number.
  3. Create an e-CNPS account via cnps.ci to file declarations and pay online.
  4. File wage declarations and pay contributions on the schedule set for your workforce size (monthly or quarterly).
  5. File the annual DISA (Déclaration Individuelle des Salaires Annuels) summarising each worker’s pay for the year.

CNPS-CI vs CNPS Cameroon

Two different countries use the same acronym. CNPS Côte d'Ivoire (cnps.ci) and CNPS Cameroon (cnps.cm) are separate institutions with different rates and rules. This guide covers Côte d'Ivoire; if you searched “CNPS” for Cameroon payroll, check Cameroon-specific sources.

CNPS Côte d'Ivoire FAQ

What is CNPS in Côte d'Ivoire?

The Caisse Nationale de Prévoyance Sociale (CNPS-CI) is Côte d'Ivoire’s social security institution for private-sector workers, managing retirement, family benefits, maternity cover and work-injury insurance.

What is e-CNPS?

e-CNPS is the online portal of CNPS Côte d'Ivoire, reachable via cnps.ci, where employers register, declare wages, file the annual DISA and pay contributions electronically.

Is the employee CNPS contribution tax-deductible?

Yes. The employee’s 6.3% retirement contribution is deducted from gross salary before the ITS salary-tax bands are applied.

Is CNPS Côte d'Ivoire the same as CNPS Cameroon?

No — Cameroon’s CNPS (cnps.cm) is a separate institution with different rates and rules.
