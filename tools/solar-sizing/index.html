Solar System Sizing for Africa — How It Works

Getting the right solar system size is critical. Too small and your batteries run out; too large and you overpay. Our calculator adds up your appliance wattage, accounts for your country's peak sun hours, and recommends the minimum viable system with 1.5 days backup storage.
