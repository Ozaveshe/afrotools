Solar Sizing Calculator FAQ

How does the Solar Sizing Calculator estimate system size?

It totals appliance watts and daily runtime, applies the selected country's peak-sun assumption, adds a 25% solar production margin, targets 1.5 days of battery energy, and adds 20% inverter headroom.

Does the calculator model appliance startup surge?

No. The inverter estimate includes general connected-load headroom but does not model the exact startup surge of pumps, compressors, air conditioners, or motors. Ask an installer to verify nameplate surge ratings.

What should I send installers for comparable solar quotes?

Calculate once, then copy the installer brief with the country assumption, appliance load list, daily energy, panel array, battery bank, inverter size, modeled cost, and verification checklist.
