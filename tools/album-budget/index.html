Method and source

Album and EP budget questions

Where should the net revenue value come from?

Use an effective value from your own distributor or label statement: divide net recording revenue received by eligible streams for the same period. It is only a scenario input, not a promised platform rate.

Does changing country convert the amounts?

No. It changes the displayed currency only. Review and replace every amount with quotes in that currency.

Does the total include tax, rights clearance or contracts?

Only if you add those amounts to an existing cost field. Confirm tax, licensing, publishing, sample clearance, union, venue and contract obligations separately before spending.
