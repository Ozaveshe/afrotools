Methodology

  • Ranks providers by estimated fee for the transfer amount and shows settlement speed context.
  • Scenario tab projects annual cost from per-transaction amount, frequency, and selected provider.
  • Provider details separate cost, speed, coverage, pros, cons, and suitability notes.

Before acting

  • Confirm live FX spread, payout corridor, settlement time, chargebacks/reversals, compliance checks, limits, and availability.
  • Check whether the provider supports your sender country, receiver country, currency, business type, and invoice size.
  • Copy or download the payment brief before requesting provider quotes or approving supplier payment.
