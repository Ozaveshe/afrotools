Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a legal baby name registry?

No. It is a planning tool for finding ideas. Always confirm spelling, pronunciation, and civil registration rules in the country where the child will be registered.

Can I filter by meaning?

Yes. Start with a meaning theme such as joy, faith, strength, or peace, then review cultural fit and family preference before choosing.

Does one name mean the same thing everywhere?

Not always. African names can change meaning by language, tone, spelling, and family context, so local confirmation matters.
