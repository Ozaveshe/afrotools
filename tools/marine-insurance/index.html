Marine / Cargo Insurance Calculator — What You Need to Know

Calculate cargo insurance premiums for African imports and exports. Estimate costs by CIF value, cargo type, and shipping route. Use this free calculator to get a quick estimate before contacting insurance providers.

Marine and cargo insurance is essential for African trade, with import values frequently exceeding millions in local currency. Rates typically range from 0.3% to 1.5% of cargo value depending on route, goods type, and coverage level. Institute Cargo Clauses A (all risks), B (named perils), and C (major casualties) are the standard frameworks used across African markets.
