What zero means here

A zero means no positive amount has been entered for that line. It does not mean the authority, professional or contract has waived the charge.

Questions before relying on the total

Why does this page not select a rate for my country?

A pan-African rate table would hide state, municipality, instrument, valuation, VAT, exemption and date differences. Only reviewed country owners calculate statutory amounts.

Can I put an estimate in a line?

You can reconcile your own scenario, but label it in your working file and replace it with a written quote before committing funds. The export records amounts, not evidence quality.

Does the total prove I have enough cash to close?

No. It is the purchase price plus the lines entered. Missing deposits, prepaid rates, insurance, finance conditions or professional disbursements remain possible.

Is any purchase information uploaded?

No. Calculation and exports run locally without storing or transmitting the amounts.
