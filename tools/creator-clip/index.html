FAQ

Common questions

What is the maximum video file size?

CreatorClip supports files up to 500MB. Your video is processed entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

How accurate are the auto-captions?

Captions use your browser's Speech Recognition API. Accuracy depends on audio clarity and language. You can edit every caption after generation.

What video formats are supported?

MP4, WebM, and MOV for import. Export is WebM, which works on all modern platforms. Convert to MP4 with any free tool if needed.

Is this really free?

Yes. 100% free. No watermarks, no trial, no subscriptions. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Will my video quality be reduced?

You choose export quality — 1080p, 720p, or 480p. The video maintains original quality up to your selected resolution.

What browser do I need?

Chrome, Edge, or Brave work best. Firefox supports most features but auto-captions need Chrome. Safari has limited MediaRecorder support.
