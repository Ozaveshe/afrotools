Method and limits

A budget boundary, not borrowing power

What belongs in recurring ownership costs?

Enter only amounts you have researched or been quoted, such as insurance, property rates, a service charge or a maintenance allowance. Leave unknown costs at zero and investigate them separately.

Why is there no DSR or country preset?

Those rules vary by lender, applicant and time. A generic preset would create false precision and could be mistaken for approval guidance.

What is the next step?

Use the mortgage planner with a rate, term, deposit and costs from a real quote or a clearly labelled scenario. Compare its total monthly cost with this boundary.
