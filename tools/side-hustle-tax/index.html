Build the reserve

Use one currency and one tax period. Do not enter taxpayer IDs, account numbers, client names or invoice details.

Fail-closed boundary

Planning profit = gross revenue - refunds - platform fees - other recorded costs. Reserve before credits = planning profit x your documented rate. The worksheet never labels a cost deductible and never selects a tax regime, country bracket or payment date.

Verify before filing

  • Legal taxpayer type and filing status
  • Whether each cost is allowable and supported
  • Current rates, bands, reliefs and thresholds
  • Whether withholding is final or creditable
  • Registration, instalment and return deadlines

Arithmetic contract

All amounts are user-entered. Credits reduce the planning reserve but never below zero. Instalments only split the reserve evenly; they are not official due dates.

Official-source context

ATAF lists African member tax administrations. Examples of current primary guidance include KRA return preparation, SARS provisional tax, and GRA personal income tax. Use the authority for your jurisdiction.

Privacy and tax boundary

No input is stored, put in a URL, sent to analytics, AI or an API. This plan is not a return, assessment, tax opinion or proof that an expense is deductible.
