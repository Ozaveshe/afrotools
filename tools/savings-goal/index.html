Before relying on it

  1. Check whether the quoted rate is effective annual or nominal.
  2. Check fees, withholding tax and withdrawal rules.
  3. Use the date and product terms actually reviewed.
  4. Stress-test with a lower rate or zero.

This worksheet does not select a bank, fund or investment. Higher assumed return is not evidence of lower risk.

Method

The effective annual rate is converted to an equivalent monthly rate. Current savings compound first; each contribution is added at month end. No fee or tax is invented.

Consumer context

The FSCA consumer education service supports financial goal-setting without selling products or offering financial advice. Verify providers and terms in your jurisdiction.

Privacy boundary

No input is stored, put in a URL, or sent to analytics, AI or an API. Exports are local.
