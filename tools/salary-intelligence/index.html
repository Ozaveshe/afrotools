Salary Intelligence verification notes

Methodology and freshness

The app combines stored benchmark rows, crowd salary reports, filters, observed dates, currencies, gross and net amounts, and optional proof metadata. Treat every result as market research that needs freshness checks, not an official salary table, payroll instruction, tax calculation, visa rule, or employment advice.

Privacy and proof handling

Keep payslips, offer letters, names, emails, phone numbers, employee IDs, employer identifiers, and profile URLs out of public proof. Submit only redacted proof links and only when you choose to send a report.

Export or copy path

Create the benchmark brief, copy it locally, then compare it with multiple recent rows before using it in negotiation, hiring, salary review, or editorial research.

Salary Intelligence FAQ

How should I use this?

Filter by country, city, role, and experience, then compare the salary brief with multiple recent reports before negotiating.

What should I verify?

Check report date, currency, gross versus net pay, benefits, contract type, taxes, bonuses, and whether proof has been safely redacted.

Is this official?

No. It is market research from stored benchmarks and reports, not an official wage schedule, tax calculation, offer guarantee, or employment advice.
