Frequently asked questions

What does the free SEO audit check?

Each audit runs 30+ checks across 7 categories: titles and metadata, crawling and indexing (robots.txt, sitemap, llms.txt, canonical, noindex), content and headings, links and mixed content, social share tags, structured data validity, and speed signals like HTML size and server response time.

Are the results real or estimated?

Every check is computed from your actual page — we fetch it live and analyze the real HTML and response headers. We do not show invented metrics like guessed traffic or fake authority scores.

How many free audits do I get?

You can run 3 free single-page audits per day. AfroTools Pro includes unlimited audits, audit history with score tracking, and schema generators in the SEO Studio workspace.

What is llms.txt and why does the audit check it?

llms.txt is a file that tells AI search tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity what your site is about and which pages matter. Sites with it are easier for AI assistants to cite, which is a growing source of traffic.

Does this work for sites outside Africa?

Yes — the audit works for any public website. The recommendations are written with African businesses in mind: WhatsApp share previews, low-bandwidth performance, and local search visibility.
