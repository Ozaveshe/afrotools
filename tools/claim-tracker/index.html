    Insurance Claim Tracker / Checklist — What You Need to Know

    Free insurance claim tracker and checklist for African policyholders. Required documents, deadlines, and follow-up tips for motor, health, life, and property claims. Use this free calculator to get a quick estimate before contacting insurance providers.

    Filing an insurance claim in Africa can be complex. Required documents typically include the policy document, incident report, photos of damage, police report (for theft/accident), and medical reports (for health claims). Processing times range from 48 hours for motor third-party to 6+ months for complex life claims. Common rejection reasons include policy exclusions, late reporting, and insufficient documentation.
