Free PDF Format Converter

The AfroTools PDF Format Converter is a free, browser-based tool that converts Word documents and Excel spreadsheets to PDF, and extracts text from PDF files. All processing happens locally in your browser — your files are never uploaded to any server.

Word to PDF Conversion

Upload any .docx file and convert it to a clean PDF document. The converter preserves headings, paragraphs, bold and italic text, lists, links, and tables from your Word document. Choose between A4 and Letter page sizes, and select normal, narrow, or wide margins to match your needs.

Excel to PDF Conversion

Transform your spreadsheets into professional PDF tables. The tool supports .xlsx, .xls, and .csv files, and handles workbooks with multiple sheets. Tables are formatted with bold headers, alternating row colours, and proper borders. Choose landscape orientation for wide spreadsheets, and use the auto-fit option to ensure columns fit within the page.

PDF Text Extraction

Extract all selectable text from any PDF document. The extracted text is displayed page by page and can be copied to clipboard or downloaded as a plain text file. This feature works with any PDF that contains selectable text (not scanned images — for that, use our OCR tool).

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a Word document to PDF? ▼
Select the "Word to PDF" tab, upload your .docx file by dragging it onto the upload area or clicking to browse. Choose your page size and margin preferences, then click "Convert to PDF". Your PDF will be generated instantly in your browser.
Can I convert Excel files with multiple sheets? ▼
Yes. When you upload a multi-sheet workbook, the tool shows tabs for each sheet. You can select which sheet to convert. Each sheet is rendered as a styled table in the PDF.
Is my data safe? ▼
Absolutely. All processing uses JavaScript libraries running entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. No data leaves your device at any point during conversion.
What formatting is preserved in Word to PDF? ▼
The converter preserves headings, paragraphs, bold, italic, underline, lists (ordered and unordered), tables, and hyperlinks. Complex formatting like custom fonts, images, and advanced layout may not be fully supported.
Why can't I extract text from my scanned PDF? ▼
The PDF to Text feature extracts selectable text embedded in the PDF. Scanned documents are images without text data. For scanned PDFs, use our PDF OCR tool which uses optical character recognition to read text from images.
