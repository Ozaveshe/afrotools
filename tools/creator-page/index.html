FAQ

Common Questions

Can I use a custom domain?
Custom domains are on our Pro tier roadmap. For now, your page lives at a clean, shareable AfroTools URL you can put in any bio.
How do payments work?
You choose which payment methods to accept: bank transfer, M-Pesa, Paystack, Flutterwave, or PayPal. For manual methods, buyers confirm and you verify. For Paystack/Flutterwave, it's automatic.
Is CreatorPage free?
Yes. The builder, all themes, and up to 3 products are completely free. Pro features include unlimited products, custom domains, and advanced analytics.
How is this different from Linktree?
Linktree gives you buttons. CreatorPage gives you a full digital storefront — sell products, accept tips, book consultations, capture emails, and showcase your work.
Can I sell digital products?
Absolutely. Upload presets, templates, ebooks, courses. Set your price in your local currency or "pay what you want." Buyers pay and get instant access.
Will my page work on mobile?
100%. Built mobile-first, loads in under 1.5s on 3G. Designed for Instagram bios, WhatsApp statuses, and Twitter profiles.
