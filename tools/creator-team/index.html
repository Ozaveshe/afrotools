FAQ

Common questions

How many team members can I add?

Up to 10 for free. Each member gets Owner, Editor, or Viewer role. Owners control everything, Editors create and move cards, Viewers can see but not edit.

Is the board real-time synced?

Data is stored locally by default. Sign in with your AfroTools account for cloud sync across devices. Changes propagate automatically when team members are online.

How does WhatsApp sharing work?

Every card has a WhatsApp button that formats task details into a clean message and opens WhatsApp with text pre-filled. Pick the chat, send. Done.

Is my team's data private?

Yes. All data stays in your browser unless you opt into cloud sync. Nothing is sent to any server without your action.

Is CreatorTeam free or paid?

Completely free for teams up to 10. All features included: board, briefs, reviews, assets, calendar, activity feed.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Local storage means full offline support. Create cards, move tasks, write briefs without internet. WhatsApp sharing needs connectivity.
