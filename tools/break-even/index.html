What is Break-Even?
Break-even is the point where your total revenue equals your total costs. At this point, you make zero profit or loss.

Formula: Fixed Costs ÷ (Selling Price - Variable Cost)
Key Metrics
Contribution Margin: Selling Price minus Variable Cost. The portion of each sale that covers fixed costs.

Margin of Safety: How much sales can drop before you hit break-even.
Why It Matters
Most Nigerian SMEs don't calculate break-even. Knowing your break-even gives you:

✓ Pricing confidence
✓ Sales targets
✓ Risk awareness
✓ Growth strategy clarity
