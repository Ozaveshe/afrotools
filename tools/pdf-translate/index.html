Translate PDFs with African Language Support

AfroTools PDF Translator is the first free online tool built specifically to support translation of PDF documents into and from African languages. Whether you need to translate legal documents from English to Swahili, educational materials from French to Hausa, or business proposals from Portuguese to Yoruba, our AI-powered translator handles it page by page with high accuracy.

Supported African Languages

  • East Africa: Swahili (Kenya, Tanzania), Amharic (Ethiopia), Somali (Somalia, Djibouti), Kinyarwanda (Rwanda)
  • West Africa: Hausa (Nigeria, Niger), Yoruba (Nigeria), Igbo (Nigeria), Twi (Ghana), Wolof (Senegal)
  • Southern Africa: Zulu (South Africa)

Global Language Support

In addition to African languages, the tool supports English, French, Arabic, Portuguese, and Spanish — the most widely spoken international languages across the African continent. This makes it ideal for translating documents between local African languages and the global languages used in business, education, and government.

How It Works

Upload your PDF, select source and target languages, and our AI translates each page individually. The translated document preserves the text content while converting it to your chosen language. You can preview both the original and translated text side by side before downloading the final translated PDF.
