About QR Codes
QR codes store data in a 2D barcode readable by smartphones and cameras.
  • ✓ Store up to 4,296 characters
  • ✓ Error correction built-in
  • ✓ Works offline
  • ✓ No data sent to servers

This QR code generator creates scannable QR codes for URLs, text, phone numbers, email addresses, WiFi credentials, and more. Customise the size, colour, and error correction level, then download your QR code as a high-resolution PNG image. The tool generates codes instantly in your browser with no server processing and no watermarks. Businesses use QR codes on menus, packaging, and marketing materials. Event organisers embed them in tickets and posters. Developers use them for app deep links and configuration sharing. Whether you need one QR code or a batch for different purposes, this generator delivers clean, reliable results every time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the QR code free to use commercially?
Yes. QR codes generated with AfroTools are completely free for both personal and commercial use. There are no watermarks, usage limits, or licensing fees. You own the QR codes you create.
What formats can I download QR codes in?
You can download your QR codes as PNG (raster image, great for web and print) or SVG (vector format, scales to any size without quality loss). SVG is recommended for large-format printing.
Does the QR code generator work offline?
Yes. The QR code generation happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Once the page has loaded, you can generate QR codes without an internet connection. No data is sent to any server.
Can I customize the colors of my QR code?
Yes. You can customize both the dark (foreground) and light (background) colors of your QR code using the color pickers. Make sure there is enough contrast between the two colors for scanners to read the code reliably.
What types of data can I encode in a QR code?
AfroTools QR Code Generator supports four types: plain text, URLs (website links), WiFi credentials (network name, password, and security type), and vCards (contact information including name, phone, email, and organization).
