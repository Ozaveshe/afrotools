Free Contract Generator for African Businesses

The AfroTools Contract Generator helps individuals and businesses across Africa create professional legal documents without hiring an expensive lawyer for routine agreements. Whether you need a tenancy agreement in Lagos, an employment contract in Nairobi, or a freelance agreement in Cape Town, our templates are adapted to local legal requirements.

Country-Specific Templates

Legal requirements vary significantly across African countries. Nigerian tenancy agreements must comply with the Lagos State Tenancy Law. South African employment contracts must meet the Basic Conditions of Employment Act. Kenyan agreements reference the Employment Act 2007. Our generator includes the correct terminology, statutory references, and mandatory clauses for each jurisdiction.

Five Essential Templates

We cover the five most commonly needed legal documents: residential tenancy agreements for landlords and tenants, employment contracts for hiring staff, non-disclosure agreements for protecting business secrets, freelance/contractor agreements for independent workers, and loan agreements for personal or business lending.

Fill-in-the-Blank Simplicity

No legal knowledge required. Each template presents clear form fields for party names, dates, amounts, and terms. Guidance tooltips explain what each field means and suggest appropriate values. The result is a complete, professional document ready for review and signatures.

Important Disclaimer

This tool generates template documents for common transactions. It is not a substitute for professional legal advice. For complex agreements, significant financial transactions, or disputed situations, always consult a qualified legal practitioner in your jurisdiction.
