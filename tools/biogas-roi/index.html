Biogas Digesters in Africa — A Sustainable Energy Solution

Biogas digesters convert animal waste into cooking gas and a nutrient-bearing digestate. Household programs in African markets use manure-fed systems for cooking, but output and economics depend on collected feedstock, water, temperature, operation, installed cost, and the fuel actually displaced.

Method boundary: The protected livestock-yield model remains a planning screen. FAO and World Bank technical references show that gas production varies with feedstock and operating conditions, so this page caps fuel savings by modeled coverage and requires local evidence for financial benefits.
