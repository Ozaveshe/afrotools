Method and evidence questions

Does “inside the range” mean compliant?

No. It means only that the arithmetic indicator is between the user-entered low and high values. A reliable conclusion depends on the controlled transaction and comparable circumstances.

Why are there no African industry benchmarks?

A generic range without transaction, year, functions, risks, geography, screening and domestic-law context could mislead. This worksheet requires a source note instead.

Does the tool apply Amount B?

No. Amount B is an optional simplified approach for in-scope baseline distributors and depends on jurisdiction adoption and detailed scoping. This page does not automate it.

Method arithmetic

TNMM: (sales − operating costs) ÷ sales.

Cost plus: (remuneration − cost base) ÷ cost base.

Resale: (resale revenue − controlled purchase price) ÷ resale revenue.

CUP: controlled unit price compared directly with your price range.

Loan: entered annual rate compared with your rate range.
