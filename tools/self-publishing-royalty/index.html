KDP 70% Royalty Tip
To qualify for Amazon KDP's 70% royalty tier, price your ebook between $2.99–$9.99. Outside this range you earn only 35%. Most successful indie authors price at $2.99, $3.99, or $4.99 — balancing royalty rate vs volume of sales.
