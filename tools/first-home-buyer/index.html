Method and limits

What this planner does—and does not do

Why do I enter the deposit as an amount?

Deposit rules vary by lender and product. Entering a documented amount prevents this page from inventing a bank rule or repeating the mortgage tools.

Does the timeline include interest?

No. It divides the remaining gap by your monthly contribution and rounds up. Use the savings tools separately if you need an interest-bearing projection.

Can I treat the printout as approval?

No. It is a private preparation note. A lender, lawyer, valuer and relevant authority must verify their own part of the purchase.
