Methodology and safety

Understand risk without doomscrolling.

AfroConflict is an information dashboard. It summarizes data types, update cadence, known limitations, and source labels so readers can orient themselves without graphic, inflammatory, or sensational framing.

Not security advice, not travel advice, not legal advice, not tactical guidance, and not emergency instruction. Do not use it to choose routes, evade checkpoints, handle weapons, or make field decisions.

Static snapshot last reviewed 14 April 2026. Live figures refresh from the source feeds above, but displacement and event data can lag real conditions — always verify against the original ACLED, UNHCR, IDMC, World Bank, or SIPRI release before quoting.
