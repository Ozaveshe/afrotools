Generator Fuel Cost Calculator FAQ

Methodology and sources

The estimate uses the bundled energy country data, selected generator kVA, fuel type, daily run hours, typical load assumptions, and grid tariff comparison. Replace fuel price and consumption assumptions with a recent receipt, maintenance log, or technician measurement when available.

What should I verify?

Check fuel price, generator age, actual consumption curve, load percentage, maintenance, ventilation, noise, transfer switch, carbon-monoxide safety, and local technician advice.

Is this official?

No. It is an operating-cost estimate, not a fuel quote, safety certificate, emissions assessment, installation instruction, or equipment warranty. Inputs stay local unless you copy or download the estimate.
