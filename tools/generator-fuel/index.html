Generator Fuel Efficiency Tips

  • Avoid running at 100% load; 50-75% is optimal for fuel efficiency
  • Regular maintenance (oil changes, filter cleaning) improves efficiency by 5-15%
  • Quality fuel extends engine life and reduces consumption
  • Modern inverter generators consume 20-30% less fuel than conventional models
  • Right-sizing your generator prevents wasted fuel on oversized units
