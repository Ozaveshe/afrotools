Solar Mini-Grid Feasibility in Africa — Key Considerations

This calculator provides a first-pass demand, system-size, CAPEX, revenue, and simple-payback screen. Its result uses a fixed USD 0.40/kWh sales tariff and 5% of CAPEX as annual operating cost; it does not establish demand, collections, permits, grant eligibility, or project finance.

Disclaimer: Treat VIABLE, MARGINAL, or CHALLENGING as a model label only. Pre-feasibility still requires seasonal load and willingness-to-pay surveys, anchor-customer checks, site and solar assessment, regulatory review, and detailed engineering.

A screen is not a feasibility study

Keep model output separate from field evidence

The protected engine sizes a concept and labels simple payback. The readiness gate records whether the minimum demand, payment, anchor, site, and regulatory evidence exists to justify deeper work.

  • Model assumption: sales revenue is fixed at USD 0.40/kWh and annual OPEX at 5% of CAPEX.
  • Field evidence: measured loads and willingness to pay must replace household and business-count proxies.
  • Approval path: an illustrative grant cap is not grant eligibility, funding availability, or a DFI decision.

Outside this screen
Collection efficiency, connection ramp-up, outages, battery replacement, degradation, taxes, debt service, inflation, currency risk, land, licensing, environmental safeguards, distribution design, and verified grant programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the mini-grid screening result mean?

VIABLE, MARGINAL, or CHALLENGING is a protected-model label based on simple payback thresholds. It is not a feasibility study, bankability opinion, permit, grant decision, or investment recommendation.

Which tariff and operating-cost assumptions drive payback?

The protected model fixes sales revenue at USD 0.40 per kWh converted to local currency and subtracts annual operating cost equal to 5% of modeled CAPEX. Collection loss, downtime, financing, tax, and replacements are not modeled.

Which evidence should be checked before pre-feasibility?

Confirm a seasonal load survey, willingness and ability to pay at the model tariff, anchor-customer offtake, and the site, land, regulatory, environmental, and interconnection route.
