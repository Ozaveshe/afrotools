Solar Mini-Grid Feasibility in Africa — Key Considerations

Rural Africa has over 600 million people without reliable electricity access. Solar mini-grids offer a faster and often cheaper path to electrification than grid extension. This calculator provides a first-pass feasibility assessment including CAPEX, revenue projections, and payback period — the starting point for any DFI or investor proposal.

Disclaimer: These are screening-level estimates. Full feasibility studies require site assessment, load surveys, and detailed engineering design.
