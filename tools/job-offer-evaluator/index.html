How the comparison works

Source: your written offers and your estimates. This tool does not infer PAYE, market pay, promotion rates, equity outcomes or negotiation success. Use a dedicated PAYE calculator first if you need a net-pay input.

Annual value = 12 × (monthly pay + monthly cash allowances + monthly benefit estimate − monthly work costs) + expected annual bonus − one-off costs. The higher positive annual value anchors the financial score at 100; other ratings are your 0–10 entries. The final score is their normalized weighted average, not career advice.
