Good vs Bad

+
Good rent report

"1-bed flat, Lekki Phase 1, Lagos, NGN 3,800,000 annual rent, NGN 500,000 legal/agency, listed April 16 at 4:10 PM, link attached."

-
Bad rent report

"Lagos apartment is expensive, around 4 million or so."

+
Good fare report

"Kasarani to CBD by matatu, KES 120 at 7:45 AM, route 17B, cash fare, Nairobi."

-
Bad fare report

"Transport in Nairobi is about KES 100 sometimes."

+
Good FX report

"USDT to NGN, Binance P2P, buy 1,585, sell 1,600, Lagos market check, screenshot attached."

-
Bad FX report

"Dollar rate in Nigeria is high today."

Examples By Category

Staple basket

Rice 1kg, tomatoes basket, eggs crate, cooking oil 1L, exact market name, observed price, and unit.

Salary

Role, industry, city, experience level, monthly gross, monthly net, sector, and company size.

School fee

Institution name, level, annual tuition, extras total, and whether the quote is annual, term, or semester based.

Clinic cost

Facility name, service, public or private type, observed cost, wait time, and proof if safe to share.

Backup power

Fuel type, quantity, cost, location, seller, and runtime hours if you know them.

Wholesale spread

Wholesale price, retail price, product brand, market, and pack size in the same city and timeframe.

Gold Data Checklist

Gold data is hard to scrape, changes often, affects real money decisions, and can be trusted enough that somebody will pay for it.
