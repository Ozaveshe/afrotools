What Good Contributors Do

Own a lane

Pick a few categories and a few cities or neighborhoods you can actually cover well.

Report on a rhythm

Weekly consistency is more valuable than random bursts.

Use proof when possible

Proof-backed submissions tend to clear faster and create stronger buyer trust.

Stay local

The strongest data is the data you can verify from real movement in your own environment.
