Important Default

Crypto payouts are requests, not instant on-chain sends. Reviewers approve the request before any payout is completed.

This protects contributors from wrong-wallet mistakes and protects AfroPoints from payout fraud or duplicate claims.

Before You Submit A Wallet

1
Use a wallet you control

Do not paste an exchange memo-only destination unless you fully understand the requirements.

2
Check the asset and network twice

A correct wallet on the wrong network can still lose funds.

3
Keep a backup fiat method

If a crypto payout needs clarification, mobile money or bank transfer can be faster.

4
Use your real contributor identity

Manual review works best when the payout request matches your AfroPoints account.

Good Reasons To Use Crypto

  • You contribute from multiple countries and want one payout rail.
  • Your local payout rails are unreliable or expensive.
  • You already manage stablecoin or major-asset wallets safely.

When Not To Use Crypto

  • You are unsure how to verify a wallet address or network.
  • You need the simplest possible payout path and mobile money is available.
  • You are borrowing someone else's wallet or sending to a friend.
