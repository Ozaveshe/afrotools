How To Cash Out Well

  1. Set your payout preference in the contributor playbook.
  2. Keep your trust score healthy by submitting proof-backed, confirmable records.
  3. Use consistent identity details so payout review is not delayed.
  4. Request cashout only when your data history looks normal and clean.

What Slows Payouts Down

1
Conflicting identity details

If names or destination details do not line up, reviewers will pause the request.

2
Low-trust submission history

Lots of weak or rejected records can slow approval.

3
Missing wallet or bank details

Crypto and bank payouts are not guessable. Fill them in completely.

4
Trying to game the system

Duplicate submissions and low-quality spam are the fastest way to lose payout trust.
