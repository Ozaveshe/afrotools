Method

Payments use reducing-balance amortization with a nominal annual rate divided by 12. Any balloon is discounted and remains due after the last regular payment. Contributions and costs are not guessed.

Credit-cost context

Central Bank of Kenya guidance highlights total cost, fees, APR, repayment schedules and borrower due diligence. Apply your jurisdiction's disclosures.

Privacy and boundary

No input is stored, put in a URL, or sent to analytics, AI or an API. Outlay excludes depreciation, resale, tax changes and costs you did not enter.
