📊 VIABILITY BENCHMARKS
  • >25% margin: Excellent — proceed with confidence
  • 20–25% margin: Good — viable for experienced developers
  • 15–20% margin: Marginal — risk mitigation needed
  • <15% margin: Danger zone — review assumptions
  • Nigerian market benchmark: 25%+ required given construction risk
  • SA benchmark: 15–20% acceptable given lower risk environment
