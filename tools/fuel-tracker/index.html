Method and limits

Are these official or live fuel prices?

No. The source file is a third-party snapshot and currently contains no rows marked officially verified. Display checks improve honesty; they do not turn a third-party source into an official quote.

Why are some countries withheld?

A row is withheld for the selected fuel if it lacks a positive local or USD value, lacks a row-level source link, or is older than 45 days. You can still calculate from a local price you verify yourself.

How does the generator formula work?

Daily litres = litres per hour × hours per day. Monthly litres = daily litres × operating days. Monthly cost = monthly litres × your price per litre.

What leaves my browser?

The page sends one same-origin GET request for the public JSON snapshot. Your country choice, price and generator inputs are not posted to an API, sent to AI or saved to browser storage.
