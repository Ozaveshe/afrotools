Handoff

What this draft can support later

  • Reviewable payroll draft with company, pay period, pay date, language lane, row-level countries, and per-currency totals.
  • Full-pack rows can show engine-backed previews when the local PAYE engine is loaded. They still need human review before client sign-off.
  • Estimate and next-pack rows use arithmetic only and mark statutory deductions as estimate-only or needing a country engine.
  • Payment handoff files are instruction drafts only; AfroPayroll does not move money, submit bank files, or confirm recipient payout.
  • No salary disbursement, payroll filing, statutory remittance, or compliance guarantee is performed from this workspace.
