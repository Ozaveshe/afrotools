Use the presets carefully

City values are rough living-cost references, not official price indexes. Use them to start faster, then replace them with your actual rent, transport, and food pattern before trusting the output.

  • School-fee reserve is optional and helps when annual fees are the real monthly pressure.
  • Support mode is practical: it captures scholarship stipends, part-time income, or loan-backed top-ups.
  • Saved scenarios stay on this device for repeat planning.
