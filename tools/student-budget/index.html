Reality check

Use the presets carefully

City values are rough living-cost references, not official price indexes. Replace them with your actual rent, transport, and food pattern before trusting the verdict, especially if the fee case is still estimated rather than confirmed.

  • School-fee reserve is optional and helps when annual fees are the real monthly pressure.
  • Support mode is practical: it captures scholarship stipends, part-time income, or loan-backed top-ups.
  • Upfront burden still matters even when the month looks survivable, so treat monthly comfort as one test, not the whole route.
  • Saved scenarios stay on this device for repeat planning.
