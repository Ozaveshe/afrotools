Image Crop Studio: How It Works

The AfroTools Image Crop Studio gives you practical control over cropping, rotating, flipping, and exporting images directly in the browser. Upload an image, choose a target like square, portrait, story, landscape, passport, photo, banner, or free crop, then drag or resize the crop box on the canvas. You can also type exact crop X, Y, width, and height values for pixel-level work. The live preview updates as you adjust the frame, rotate in 90-degree steps, flip horizontally or vertically, nudge the selection, and enter exact export dimensions. Download as JPG, PNG, or WebP, with a quality slider for compressed formats and a background color for JPG exports with transparent areas. Settings and recent export recipes are stored locally so repeated crop jobs feel faster. All processing uses browser canvas APIs, so your photos stay private and never leave your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What aspect ratio presets are available?

The studio includes free crop, square 1:1, portrait 4:5, story 9:16, landscape 16:9, photo 3:2, passport 35:45, and banner 4:1 presets.

Can I rotate and flip images?

Yes. You can rotate the image in 90-degree increments (clockwise or counter-clockwise) and flip it both horizontally and vertically before or after cropping.

What image formats can I upload?

You can upload JPG, PNG, WebP, and most standard browser image formats. The tool outputs your cropped image as JPG, PNG, WebP, or a sensible default based on the original file.

Does cropping reduce image quality?

The crop keeps the selected pixels from the transformed image. PNG output is lossless, while JPG and WebP exports use the quality value you choose.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All image processing happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your images are never transmitted to any server, ensuring complete privacy.
