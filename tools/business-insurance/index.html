Frequently Asked Questions

What cover does a small shop usually compare?

Fire, theft, stock, equipment, public liability, goods in transit, and business interruption are common starting points.

What documents help with a quote?

Stock list, equipment invoices, lease, photos, security details, annual revenue, and claim history.

Can one policy cover every risk?

Usually no. Compare policy sections, exclusions, limits, excess, and whether professional liability is separate.
