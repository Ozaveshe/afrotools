Frequently Asked Questions

Why do royalty rates vary?

Platform, country, subscription type, ad-supported listening, rights split, and distributor terms affect payouts.

Do streams pay the artist directly?

Usually no. Money passes through labels, distributors, publishers, and rights administrators before artists are paid.

What should artists reconcile?

Streams, territory, ISRC, distributor fee, publishing split, label split, exchange rate, and payment date.
