Frequently asked questions

Short answers for the questions people usually ask before trusting a CV tool with career data.

Is the AfroTools CV Builder free?

Yes. You can create, preview, and export a PDF CV without a watermark. AfroTools does not show paid partner placements in the CV Builder while no sponsorships are active.

Does it work for Nigeria, Kenya, and South Africa?

Yes. The builder includes country-aware guidance for all 54 African countries and an international or diaspora format. It gives suggestions and warnings rather than forcing sensitive personal fields.

Can I use it as an ATS CV builder?

The builder includes ATS readiness checks, job-description matching, and plain-format export options. Helps readability; does not guarantee interviews or rankings.

Can graduates use the CV templates?

Yes. Graduate and no-experience users can start with skills-first templates, project sections, education details, and editable examples.

Where is my CV saved?

Draft CV data is saved in your browser unless you choose to sign in. Avoid using shared devices for private job-search documents.
