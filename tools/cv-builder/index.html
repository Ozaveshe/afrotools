Country-Specific Formatting

African job markets have distinct expectations. Nigerian employers typically expect to see your date of birth, marital status, state of origin, and a passport photograph. Kenyan CVs include national ID numbers and personal details. South African CVs, by contrast, omit all demographic information to comply with employment equity legislation. The AfroTools CV Builder automatically adjusts which fields appear based on your selected country.

Tips for African Job Applications

  • Keep your CV to 2 pages unless you have 10+ years of experience.
  • Include a professional summary at the top with your key achievements and career goals.
  • Quantify your accomplishments where possible: revenue generated, team sizes managed, projects delivered.
  • List your most recent experience first and work backwards chronologically.
  • Include references or state "Available upon request" as expected in most African markets.
