Free CV and Resume Builder for African Job Markets

AfroTools CV Builder helps students, graduates and professionals prepare a country-aware CV for African and international applications. It combines a guided editor, live A4 preview, ATS readiness checks, job-description matching, a cover letter generator, LinkedIn profile helpers and a local job application tracker. PDF export is free and does not add a watermark.

Use it as a free CV builder for Africa, a free resume builder for diaspora applications, or a focused ATS CV builder when a job portal asks for a simple, readable document. The guidance is educational and editable. It does not force sensitive personal data or promise interviews.

CV Guides by Country

Country guidance is not legal advice. It highlights common employer expectations and safer choices so you can decide what to include. Sensitive fields stay optional.

Nigeria CV Format

For Nigerian applications, include clear contact details, education, NYSC status where relevant, skills and evidence-led experience bullets. Treat personal fields as optional unless the employer process asks for them.

Nigeria tools

Kenya CV Format

For Kenya, keep the CV concise, show relevant qualifications, list technical and soft skills, and avoid sharing national ID details unless a trusted hiring process requires them.

Kenya tools

South Africa CV Format

For South Africa, use a no-photo, achievement-led format by default and avoid unnecessary demographic information unless an employer lawfully requests it.

South Africa tools

Ghana CV Format

For Ghana, lead with profile, education, work experience, national service details where useful, certifications, languages and references on request.

Ghana tools

Egypt CV Format

For Egypt, bilingual or formal templates may be useful for some roles. Include language ability and only add photo or military-service information when it fits the target employer process.

Egypt tools

Morocco CV Format

For Morocco, prepare French, Arabic or bilingual content when relevant, make language levels visible, and keep sensitive details optional.

Morocco tools

Ethiopia CV Format

For Ethiopia, keep education, professional training, language ability and practical project evidence easy to scan. Use a simple ATS-friendly template for online applications.

How the ATS and Job-Match Checks Help

The ATS score reviews contact details, summary strength, bullet quality, quantified achievements, skills relevance, education completeness, keyword coverage, formatting safety, country-specific completeness, length and readability. The job-description matcher helps you find missing skills and weak sections, but it encourages evidence and proof instead of keyword stuffing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the AfroTools CV Builder free?

Yes. You can create, preview and export a PDF CV without a watermark. Optional partner offers may appear outside the active editor, but the CV builder remains usable without them.

Does the CV Builder work for Nigeria, Kenya and South Africa?

Yes. The builder includes country-aware guidance for all 54 African countries and an international or diaspora format. It gives suggestions and warnings rather than forcing sensitive personal fields.

Can I use it as an ATS CV builder?

The builder includes ATS readiness checks, job-description matching and plain-format export options. It does not guarantee interviews or employer ranking outcomes.

Can graduates use the CV templates?

Yes. Graduate and no-experience users can start with skills-first templates, project sections, education details and editable examples.

Where is my CV saved?

Draft CV data is saved in your browser unless you choose to sign in. Avoid using shared devices for private job-search documents.
