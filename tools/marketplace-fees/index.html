Frequently Asked Questions

Which marketplace has the lowest fees?
The calculator does not embed or rank current platform rates. Enter dated seller terms for the same product and service level; the lowest total deduction in those inputs produces the highest mathematical net payout.
Can rates differ by product category?
They may differ by platform, country, category, fulfilment method, campaign and seller agreement. Use the exact terms shown in your current portal or written quote.
What's the difference between listing fee, commission, and payment fee?
Commission and payment fees are percentage deductions. Fixed per-sale and withdrawal charges are amounts deducted from one payout. Allocate a recurring or one-time cost across the number of sales you reasonably expect, then enter the per-sale allocation.
Should I sell on multiple platforms at once?
Net payout is only one factor. Compare logistics, returns, fraud controls, advertising, service obligations and payout timing from current terms before choosing channels.
