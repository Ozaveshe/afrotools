Social Media Card Generator: How It Works

The AfroTools Social Media Card Generator creates eye-catching share cards and Open Graph images for Twitter, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Instagram, and other social platforms. Enter your headline, description, and optional branding, then choose from platform-specific templates that are pre-sized for optimal display on each network. Customise the background colour, gradient, text colour, font size, and layout to match your brand identity. The live preview shows exactly how your card will appear when shared, so you can fine-tune every detail before downloading. Export your finished card as a high-quality PNG image ready to upload as an Open Graph image for your website, blog post, or social media update. Creating consistent, professional share graphics is essential for increasing click-through rates and engagement on social media — posts with custom images receive significantly more interaction than those with generic or auto-generated previews. This tool is invaluable for content creators, bloggers, marketers, developers, and anyone who shares links on social media and wants them to stand out. No design skills required — the templates handle the layout while you focus on the message.

Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms are supported?

The tool includes templates sized for Twitter (1200x675), LinkedIn (1200x627), WhatsApp, and Instagram. Each template uses the optimal dimensions recommended by that platform for link preview images.

What is an Open Graph image?

An Open Graph (OG) image is the preview image that appears when your link is shared on social media. Setting a custom OG image in your website's meta tags ensures your content looks professional and clickable when shared.

Can I customise the colours and fonts?

Yes. You can customise background colours, gradients, text colour, and font size. This lets you match the card design to your brand identity for a consistent visual presence across social platforms.

What format is the card downloaded in?

Cards are downloaded as PNG images, which is the recommended format for social media preview images. PNG provides crisp text rendering and supports all the colour variations in your design.

How do I add the card to my website as an OG image?

Upload the downloaded image to your website, then add a meta tag in your HTML head: <meta property="og:image" content="https://yoursite.com/path-to-image.png">. Social platforms will display this image when your link is shared.
