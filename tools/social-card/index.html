    How to use Social Card Studio

    Start with the platform where the graphic will appear, then choose a campaign starter. The canvas renders at the exact pixel size shown in the export panel, so the file you download is ready for website meta tags, creator posts, ad drafts, WhatsApp broadcasts, event flyers, and founder updates.

    For websites and blogs

    Use the Open Graph size, keep the main headline inside the safe zone, export PNG or WebP, then paste the generated metadata snippet into your page head after uploading the image.

    For social teams

    Save your brand kit, export the platform set, and copy the handoff brief so another person knows the copy, colors, dimensions, and intended placement.

    For creators and local businesses

    Use the WhatsApp or story sizes for launches, market days, training cohorts, church events, hiring posts, and community announcements without sending files to a server.

    Social Card Studio FAQ

    Does this upload my image or logo?

    No. Rendering happens in your browser with Canvas. Uploaded images and logos are only used locally on this device.

    Which sizes are included?

    The studio includes Open Graph and X, LinkedIn, Instagram square, portrait feed, story or WhatsApp status, and YouTube community sizes. The exported canvas uses the exact selected dimensions.

    Can I reuse my brand settings?

    Yes. Save your brand kit to local storage, then load it later on the same browser. The editor also autosaves the current design state.

    What should I export for a website preview image?

    Use the Open Graph option at 1200 x 630 and export PNG or WebP. After uploading the file to your website, copy the OG snippet and update the image URL.
