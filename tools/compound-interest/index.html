Method and assumptions

This simpler calculator uses the same reviewed AfroTools Investment Return engine as the full investment projection. It converts the entered nominal annual rate into the selected periodic rate, then into an equivalent monthly growth rate so regular monthly contributions can be applied consistently. Contributions are added either before or after each month’s growth, as selected.

Calculations keep full floating-point precision and round only displayed values. Fees, taxes, inflation, withdrawals, defaults, rate changes and currency movement are outside this view. The full Investment Return Calculator adds inflation-adjusted value and sensitivity scenarios.

Checked worked example

With an initial 100,000, an end-of-month contribution of 10,000, an 8% nominal annual rate compounded monthly and a five-year term, total contributions are 700,000 and the projected balance is 883,753.13, including 183,753.13 projected interest. Reviewed 23 July 2026 using the shared engine.

Frequently asked questions

Is the entered rate nominal or effective?

Enter a nominal annual rate. The calculator derives the periodic rate and shows the corresponding effective annual rate.

When are monthly contributions added?

Choose beginning or end of month. A beginning contribution receives that month’s growth; an end contribution does not.

Does this calculator use live savings rates?

No. It uses only your entries. Verify current product rates, fees, tax and withdrawal conditions with the provider.
