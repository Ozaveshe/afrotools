Logo Maker: How It Works

The AfroTools Logo Maker helps you create clean, professional text-based logos in minutes. Enter your brand name or business initials, then customise the font, colour, size, letter spacing, and styling to match your brand identity. Choose from a curated selection of fonts that range from bold and modern to elegant and refined. Add optional icons from a built-in library to complement your text logo. The live preview updates as you adjust settings, so you can experiment freely until you find the perfect combination. When you are ready, download your logo in both SVG (scalable vector format for print and professional use) and PNG (for web and social media). The SVG format ensures your logo stays crisp and sharp at any size, whether it appears on a business card or a billboard. This tool is ideal for startups, freelancers, small businesses, and side projects that need a polished logo without the cost of hiring a graphic designer. No design experience is needed — the tool does the heavy lifting while you focus on your creative vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

What formats can I download the logo in?

You can download your logo as SVG (vector format, infinitely scalable, ideal for print) and PNG (raster format, ideal for websites, social media, and digital use).

Can I use the logo commercially?

Yes. Logos created with this tool are yours to use for any purpose, including commercial use on websites, business cards, packaging, and marketing materials.

Can I add icons to my text logo?

Yes. The tool includes a library of icons you can pair with your text. Choose an icon that represents your industry or brand, and position it alongside your logo text.

What is the difference between SVG and PNG?

SVG is a vector format that stays sharp at any size and can be edited in design software. PNG is a raster (pixel) image best for digital use. For professional printing, always use the SVG file.

Do I need design skills to create a logo?

Not at all. The tool provides pre-styled fonts, colour pickers, and layout options. Simply type your brand name, adjust the settings, and download. The interface is intuitive enough for anyone to create a professional-looking logo.
