How this calculator models the result

Each contribution is added at the end of the month. The entered annual dividend and bank rates are converted to equivalent monthly rates, then compounded over the selected term.

  • SACCO balance: the contribution schedule plus modelled dividends.
  • Bank comparison: the same schedule using your entered bank savings rate.
  • Loan capacity: projected SACCO balance multiplied by your entered factor; it is not a lending decision.

Dividend allocation, eligible deposits, taxes, fees, guarantor rules and loan limits vary. Check the society's current member terms and the official register for your jurisdiction before committing funds.

What does SACCO mean here?
This calculator uses SACCO as a general label for a member-owned savings and credit cooperative. Legal names, products and member rights differ by jurisdiction and society.
Is the projected loan capacity guaranteed?
No. It is only the projected savings balance multiplied by the factor you enter. Approval can depend on eligible deposits, membership duration, guarantors, affordability, existing loans, fees and current lending policy.
How should I verify a society?
Check the current official register or regulator for the jurisdiction, then compare the society's member terms, audited information, withdrawal rules, fees and complaint process. This calculator does not verify or endorse a provider.
