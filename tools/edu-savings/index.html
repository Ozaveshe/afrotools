Method and checks

This projection estimates future school fees using country and institution assumptions, then compares that target with the future value of your monthly savings. It is a planning aid, not advice.

  • Update the target institution type and expected return to match your family plan before trusting the gap figure.
  • Check official fee schedules, living costs, exchange rates, inflation, product fees, tax rules, and withdrawal restrictions.
  • Export the plan before speaking with a school, scholarship adviser, regulated financial adviser, or family decision-maker.
