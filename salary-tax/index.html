Frequently Asked Questions

How do your PAYE calculators work?

Each calculator estimates salary tax using the published bands, thresholds, deduction rules, and visible assumptions available for the selected country. Check each calculator's source panel and confirm final filing rules with the relevant authority or a qualified adviser.

What country-specific deductions are included?

Country-specific pension (Nigeria NHF/NSITF, Kenya NSSF/SHIF, South Africa UIF/SDL), medical aid tax credits, housing allowances, bonus tax, and multi-currency support. Supported calculators produce a payslip-style breakdown or source-note summary.

Is my salary data safe?

Core calculations run in your browser, and your figures stay on this device unless a page clearly asks for consent to send data. Some exports or workspace features may ask for an account.

Do you cover VAT and other taxes?

Yes. You can also use VAT calculators, capital gains tax estimators, and withholding tax tools for contractors and freelancers.

How often are tax tables updated?

We review official updates and refresh calculators when the source rules change. Important tax pages should show the assumptions and source note used for the estimate.
