Accurate Salary & Tax Calculators for Every African Country

Understanding your take-home pay shouldn't require a chartered accountant. AfroTools provides free, accurate PAYE (Pay As You Earn) calculators for all 54 African countries, built with real tax tables from each country's revenue authority. Whether you're a salaried employee in Lagos checking your FIRS deductions, a Nairobi professional verifying KRA calculations, or an HR manager in Johannesburg running SARS payroll compliance checks, our tools give you instant, reliable results.

We track every budget amendment and tax table change across the continent. When Kenya's Finance Act amends PAYE bands, when Nigeria's NTA introduces new reliefs, when Ghana's GRA updates its consolidated tax tables, our calculators are updated within days. This commitment to accuracy sets AfroTools apart from generic salary calculators that use outdated rates or approximate tax brackets.

How Our PAYE Calculators Work

Each AfroTools PAYE calculator applies the exact tax bands, thresholds, and deduction rules published by the respective country's revenue authority. For Nigeria, that means the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS) rates under both the Personal Income Tax Act (PITA) 2025 and the new Nigeria Tax Act (NTA) 2026. For Kenya, we use the Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) bands including the updated SHIF contributions that replaced NHIF in 2024. For Ghana, our calculators follow the Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA) consolidated tax table with SSNIT Tier 1 and Tier 2 deductions. South Africa calculations use the South African Revenue Service (SARS) 2025/26 tax year brackets including UIF and SDL contributions.

Country-Specific Deductions and Benefits

Key features across all calculators: Country-specific pension deductions (Nigeria NHF/NSITF, Kenya NSSF/SHIF 2.75%, South Africa UIF/SDL), medical aid tax credits, housing allowances, bonus tax calculations, and multi-currency support. Every calculation produces a full payslip-style breakdown showing gross pay, each deduction line item, taxable income, tax payable, and net take-home pay — so you understand exactly where your money goes.

Beyond the core PAYE calculators, our salary and tax suite includes VAT calculators for all 54 countries (with rate variations for exempt, zero-rated, and standard goods), capital gains tax estimators, and withholding tax tools for contract workers and freelancers. Each tool is tailored to the specific legislation of the country it serves — not a generic template with a flag swapped in.
