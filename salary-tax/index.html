Frequently Asked Questions

How do your PAYE calculators work?

Each calculator applies the exact tax bands, thresholds, and deduction rules published by the respective country's revenue authority. For Nigeria, that means FIRS rates under PITA 2025 and NTA 2026. For Kenya, KRA bands including SHIF contributions. For Ghana, GRA consolidated tax tables with SSNIT Tier 1 and 2.

What country-specific deductions are included?

Country-specific pension (Nigeria NHF/NSITF, Kenya NSSF/SHIF, South Africa UIF/SDL), medical aid tax credits, housing allowances, bonus tax, and multi-currency support. Every calculation produces a full payslip-style breakdown.

Is my salary data safe?

Completely. All calculations run in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers. No registration required. Your figures stay on your device.

Do you cover VAT and other taxes?

Yes. Beyond PAYE, our suite includes VAT calculators for all 54 countries (with rate variations for exempt, zero-rated, and standard goods), capital gains tax estimators, and withholding tax tools for contractors and freelancers.

How often are tax tables updated?

Within days of any budget amendment or tax table change. When Kenya's Finance Act amends PAYE bands, when Nigeria's NTA introduces new reliefs, when Ghana's GRA updates rates — we update before other tools even notice they changed.
