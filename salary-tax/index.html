Frequently Asked Questions

How do your PAYE calculators work?

Each calculator estimates salary tax using the published bands, thresholds, deduction rules, and visible assumptions available for the selected country. Check each calculator's source panel and confirm final filing rules with the relevant authority or a qualified adviser.

What country-specific deductions are included?

Country-specific pension, social security, medical aid, housing allowance, bonus tax, and employer-cost items appear where the relevant route supports them. Check each page's source panel before treating an estimate as payroll-ready.

Is my salary data safe?

Most salary calculations run in your browser. Your figures are not sent unless you choose a feature that clearly says it will save, share, or sync the result.

Do you cover VAT and other taxes?

Yes. You can also use VAT calculators, capital gains tax estimators, and withholding tax tools for contractors and freelancers.

How often are tax tables updated?

We review official updates and refresh calculators when the source rules change. Each important tax page should show the assumptions and source note used for the estimate.
