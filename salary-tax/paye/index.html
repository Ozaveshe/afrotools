Frequently Asked Questions

How do PAYE calculators work?

Each calculator estimates salary tax using the published bands, thresholds, deduction rules, and visible assumptions available for the selected country. Check each calculator's source panel and confirm final filing rules with the relevant authority or a qualified adviser.

What country-specific deductions are included?

Country-specific pension, social security, medical aid, housing allowance, bonus tax, and employer-cost items appear where the relevant calculator supports them. Check each page's source panel before payroll use.

Is my salary data safe?

Completely. Core calculator operations run in your browser; labelled network features are disclosed separately. Nothing is sent to our servers. No registration required. Your figures stay on your device.

How often are tax tables updated?

When Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Tanzania, Malawi, or Lesotho changes payroll-tax rules, we update the relevant calculator route and keep official filing links visible.
