## Recommended Optimization Moves

- Test shorter, higher-clarity titles on pages with strong impressions but soft CTR.
- Add more quick-answer blocks to pages where users likely need the answer before they commit to the tool.
- Expand FAQs only where they target real search intent, not filler.
- Keep tool CTAs primary and singular on high-intent pages.
- Add more country-specific examples before expanding into broader comparison content.
