What the 2026 federal evidence supports

The FY2026 Budget Policy Framework lists named sales taxes for hotels, telecommunications, electricity, airline tickets and imported goods. It does not establish one nationwide VAT rate.

Separate 2023 turnover-tax publication

The federal small-business turnover-tax table uses USD. It is not VAT, and current 2026 liability was not established by the evidence reviewed.

Calculation methodology

Fail-closed federal evidence reference. The FY2026 budget's named sector sales taxes are kept separate from the 2023 USD-denominated turnover-tax table. No nationwide VAT rate, liability, currency, filing, exemption or Federal Member State treatment is calculated or inferred.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Federal budget collection lines and proposals do not prove enacted transaction rates.
  • The 2023 turnover-tax table is historical evidence; current 2026 applicability was not established.
  • Federal evidence does not determine Federal Member State or Somaliland treatment.
  • Confirm filing, registration, and remittance duties with the official authority or a qualified adviser before submission.
