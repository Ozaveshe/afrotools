Calculation methodology

The calculator applies the federal monthly USD schedule to combined chargeable employment income. Resident adults use marginal bands of 0%, 6%, 12%, and 18%; non-residents use 18% flat and residents under 18 use 20% flat. No employment deductions are modeled. Annual figures repeat the monthly case twelve times.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Federal member-state, Somaliland, benefit, treaty, sector and special-regime rules are outside the modeled result.
  • Social-security and other payroll contributions are not modeled and are not asserted to be zero.
  • Confirm filing, registration, and remittance duties with the official authority or a qualified adviser before submission.
