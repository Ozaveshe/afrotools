Rwanda VAT FAQ

Common VAT Questions

What is Rwanda's VAT rate?

Rwanda's standard VAT rate is 18%. Exports are zero-rated. Medical, pharmaceutical, and educational services are exempt.

How do I calculate reverse VAT in Rwanda?

Divide the total by 1.18 to get net, subtract net from total to get VAT. This calculator does this automatically.

What is the VAT registration threshold?

Businesses with annual turnover exceeding RWF 50 million must register for VAT with RRA.

How often are VAT returns filed?

Monthly VAT returns must be filed with RRA by the 20th of the following month.
