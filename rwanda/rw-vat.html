What the tool will not guess

  • 18% applies to taxable supplies not lawfully zero-rated or exempt.
  • Article 7 defines zero-rated supplies; exports are an RRA example, not an automatic label for every cross-border transaction.
  • Article 8 exemptions changed in May 2025 and several entries depend on ministerial lists or licensing conditions.
  • VAT withholding depends on appointment and transaction facts, so no generic percentage calculator is presented here.

Calculation methodology

The shared engine adds or extracts Rwanda VAT using the Article 4 standard rate. Zero-rated and exempt treatment is available only after the user confirms an exact current Article 7 or Article 8 basis. Registration screening uses strict above-threshold tests for prior-fiscal-year turnover and preceding-quarter turnover; it does not register, file or determine treatment for the user.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Zero-rating, exemption, input-tax recovery, public-procurement withholding and special treatment require exact current legal classification and are not inferred.
  • The 2025 amendment removed or changed exemptions; old category summaries must not be used as current classification advice.
  • Confirm filing, registration, and remittance duties with the official authority or a qualified adviser before submission.
