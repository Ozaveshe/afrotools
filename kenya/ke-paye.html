PAYE Band-by-Band Computation

Legal Basis — Kenya PAYE 2025/26

  • Income Tax Act Cap 470 — PAYE bands: 10% on first KES 24,000/mo, 25% to KES 32,333, 30% to KES 500,000, 32.5% to KES 800,000, 35% above KES 800,000/mo
  • Personal Relief: KES 2,400/month (KES 28,800/year) — statutory, no conditions
  • NSSF Act 2013 — Tier I: 6% on first KES 8,000 (= KES 480); Tier II: 6% on KES 8,001–72,000 (max KES 3,840). Effective Feb 2025.
  • Social Health Insurance Act 2023 — SHIF replaces NHIF from 1 Oct 2024. Rate: 2.75% gross, min KES 300, no cap. Deductible for PAYE per Tax Laws (Amendment) Act 2024.
  • Affordable Housing Act 2024 — 1.5% employee contribution (matched by employer). AHL tax relief repealed by Tax Laws (Amendment) Act 2024 (effective Dec 2024) — deducted from net pay, no longer reduces taxable income. Most calculators get this wrong.
  • Remittance deadline: PAYE + SHIF + AHL by 9th of following month via KRA iTax (Form P10)
  • NSSF remittance: 9th of following month via NSSF i-portal. Late penalty: 5% + 1% monthly interest.
  • SHIF late penalty: 2% per month on outstanding. AHL late penalty: 3% per month.
