Statutory Deductions Summary

📊 NSSF (Feb 2025 rates)

  • Tier I: 6% on first KES 8,000 = KES 480/month employee
  • Tier II: 6% on KES 8,001–72,000 = max KES 3,840/month
  • Total max employee contribution: KES 4,320/month
  • Employer matches employee contribution exactly
  • Deducted before PAYE taxable income calculation
  • Remit by 9th of following month via NSSF i-portal

🏥 SHIF (replaced NHIF Oct 2024)

  • 2.75% of gross monthly salary — no upper cap
  • Minimum KES 300/month contribution
  • Replaced NHIF flat-rate bands from 1 October 2024
  • Managed by Social Health Authority (SHA)
  • Deductible against PAYE (Tax Laws Amendment Act 2024)
  • Remit by 9th of following month — late: 2%/month penalty

🏠 Affordable Housing Levy

  • 1.5% of gross monthly salary (employee)
  • 1.5% matched by employer (total 3%)
  • Effective March 2024 — Affordable Housing Act 2024
  • Deductible against PAYE since Tax Laws Amendment Act 2024
  • Declared on Form P10 Sheet M via KRA iTax
  • Late penalty: 3% per month on outstanding amount

✅ Reliefs Available

  • Personal Relief: KES 2,400/month (KES 28,800/year) — automatic
  • Insurance Relief: 15% of premium paid, max KES 5,000/month
  • Pension deduction: actual contrib, max KES 30,000/month
  • Post-Retirement Medical Fund: max KES 15,000/month
  • Home Ownership Savings Plan (HOSP) contributions
  • SHIF deductible (new) — Housing Levy deductible (new)
