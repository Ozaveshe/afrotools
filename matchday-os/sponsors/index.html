Sponsor influence that is not allowed

Commercial inventory works only if fans can trust the product. These rules apply before any placement goes live.

No editorial impersonationSponsored blocks are labeled and cannot look like independent match analysis.
No official affiliation claimsNo sponsor may imply FIFA, tournament, federation, team, or official broadcast partnership through Matchday OS.
No product logic controlSponsors cannot change fixtures, standings, Prediction Points, Fan Points, winner verification, prize eligibility, or legal rules.
No utility blockingPlacements cannot block teams, fixtures, predictions, share-card export, country rooms, or newsletter signup.
No heavy creativeAssets must stay lightweight and approved before launch so mobile fans on slower networks are not punished.
No invented proofNo fake sponsors, fake traffic, fake winners, fake leaderboard rows, or fake audience numbers.
