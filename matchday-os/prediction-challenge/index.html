The campaign is designed around football knowledge, transparent scoring, locked deadlines, and manual winner checks. The live prize version must wait until server-side enforcement is complete.

Anti-cheat and fairness

The campaign must protect honest fans and sponsors. The current page is not a live contest engine; it is a public draft with a clear launch gate.

Eligibility checks

One account per person, verified email, terms acceptance timestamp, and age/country eligibility must be enforced before leaderboard eligibility.

Abuse signals

Device/session, IP, and user-agent signals must be stored as hashes or derived review signals, never as raw frontend data.

Review queue

Duplicate accounts, late attempts, unusual IP clusters, and identical-prediction patterns must route to admin review.
