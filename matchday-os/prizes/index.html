Prediction Points vs Fan Points

The cash prize uses only Prediction Points

Cash prizes are based only on Prediction Points. Prediction Points come from your football picks. Fan Points from sharing the app help you climb the Top Fans board, but they do not count toward the cash prize.

Prediction Points

Prediction Points decide cash prizes. They come only from football picks such as match outcomes, exact scores, qualifiers, and tournament picks.

Fan Points

Fan Points are for sharing, badges, shoutouts, sponsor perks, and Top Fans recognition.

Referrals

Referrals do not improve cash-prize ranking. A person can top the Fan Points board and still not win cash if their Prediction Points are not in the final verified Top 10.

Tie-break rules

Tie-breaks happen only after Prediction Points

If two or more verified players finish with the same Prediction Points, AfroTools uses this order.

  1. Higher number of exact scores
  2. Higher number of correct match outcomes
  3. Higher number of correct African-team predictions
  4. Earlier submitted locked predictions across the tournament
  5. Manual review if still tied
