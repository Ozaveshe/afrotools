Points system

One Matchday Leaderboard

Total Matchday Points combine Prediction Points and eligible Fan Points. Winners may be manually verified before prize payment. Fake accounts, self-referrals, duplicate signups, or suspicious activity may be removed.

Prediction Points

Prediction Points come from football picks such as match outcomes, exact scores, qualifiers, and tournament picks.

Fan Points

Fan Points come from valid referrals, follows, sharing, profile actions, and other approved fan tasks.

Total Matchday Points

Total Matchday Points equals Prediction Points plus eligible Fan Points. This is the public leaderboard score.

Tie-break rules

Tie-breaks happen after Total Matchday Points

If two or more verified players finish with the same Total Matchday Points, AfroTools uses this order.

  1. Higher number of exact scores
  2. Higher number of correct match outcomes
  3. Higher number of correct African-team predictions
  4. Earlier submitted locked predictions across the tournament
  5. Manual review if still tied
