Sudan VAT calculator

Calculate the official fixed 17% standard VAT locally in Sudanese pounds. The current-rate evidence is the Tax Authority's 28 March 2026 clarification under Article 22 of the 2001 VAT Law.

Standard-rate formula

Add VAT: VAT = net amount × 17%; gross = net amount + VAT.

Extract VAT: net amount = VAT-inclusive amount ÷ 1.17; VAT = gross − net.

Calculation methodology

The calculator applies only the official fixed 17% standard rate. Add mode computes VAT as net × 17% and gross as net + VAT. Extract mode computes net as VAT-inclusive amount ÷ 1.17 and VAT as gross − net. Results round to SDG 0.01.

Known limitations

  • Standard-rate planning estimate only; it is not professional tax, legal or filing advice.
  • Exemption and zero-rating are outside the calculator and are never inferred from a broad product label.
  • Registration, filing, invoice compliance, input-tax eligibility and transaction classification are not modeled.
  • Confirm the exact current treatment with the Sudan Tax Authority or a qualified adviser before invoicing or filing.
