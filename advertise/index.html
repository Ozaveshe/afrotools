Sponsorship rules

User trust stays independent of the sponsor.

The sponsor funds a labeled placement or distribution package. The calculator, estimate, source label, and ranking continue to follow AfroTools product rules.

  • Every sponsor or partner placement is visibly labeled.
  • Sponsors cannot buy changes to formulas, eligibility checks, rankings, or source notes.
  • Lead handoff requires an explicit user action and consent.
  • Campaign claims and destination pages are reviewed before launch.
