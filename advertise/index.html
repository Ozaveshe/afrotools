Why Advertise on AfroTools?

AfroTools reaches African professionals, entrepreneurs, and students actively researching financial, tax, and career decisions — high-intent audiences who are in research mode and ready to act. Our tools cover salary tax calculators, VAT calculators, import duty, currency conversion, and more across all 54 African nations.

Our audience includes employees calculating take-home pay, small business owners managing VAT compliance, students checking WAEC grades and university eligibility, and diaspora Africans comparing remittance services. These are readers who trust the data they see and engage deeply with financial content.

Advertising formats include sponsored tool integrations, banner placements, newsletter sponsorships, and custom country or tool-specific campaigns. All placements are non-intrusive, clearly marked, and designed to match AfroTools' clean, professional aesthetic. Contact us to discuss rates and availability.
