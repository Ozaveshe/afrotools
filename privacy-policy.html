Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · AfroTools.com

1. What we collect

AfroTools collects only what is necessary to operate the platform:

  • Email address — only when you voluntarily subscribe to updates or download a PDF report. We never require an account.
  • Usage data — anonymised page views via analytics (no personal identifiers).
  • Calculator inputs — salary and tax inputs are processed entirely in your browser. We do not store or transmit your financial data to our servers.

2. How we use your email

If you submit your email address, we use it to:

  • Send you notifications when new tools launch for your country
  • Alert you when tax law changes affect a calculator you've used

We do not sell, rent, or share your email with third parties. Ever.

3. Cookies

AfroTools uses no tracking cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party analytics that identify individual users. Basic analytics (page views, country, device type) are collected anonymously.

4. Your calculator data

All calculations happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your salary figures, tax inputs, and results are never sent to our servers. When you download a PDF, it is generated in your browser and saved directly to your device.

5. Third-party services

  • Netlify — hosting and form submissions. Netlify's privacy policy applies to form data.
  • Anthropic Claude API — the AI Tax Advisor feature sends your anonymised tax summary (not personal identifiers) to Anthropic's API to generate advice. No name or email is transmitted.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google's privacy policy applies.

6. Data retention

Email addresses submitted via forms are retained until you unsubscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly at hello@afrotools.com.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Contact us at hello@afrotools.com and we will respond within 7 days.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the platform grows. Material changes will be noted at the top of this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions? Email hello@afrotools.com.
