Finance Act 2025 in effect (1 July 2025). PAYE bands remain progressive from 0% (up to TSh 270,000/month) to 30%. NSSF contributions are 20% of gross salary (10% employee + 10% employer) with no upper cap. Skills Development Levy (SDL) is 3.5% for employers with 10+ employees. Fiscal year runs July–June.

Key 2025/26 Facts

📋 For Employees

  • First TSh 270,000/month is tax-free (0% band)
  • NSSF (10% employee) deducted before PAYE
  • Top PAYE rate: 30% above TSh 1,000,000/month
  • Housing benefit: taxed at higher of market value, 15% of income, or employer cost
  • Non-residents taxed at flat 30% on Tanzania-sourced income
  • Capital gains on shares: 10% for residents

🏢 For Employers

  • NSSF employer: 10% of gross salary (no cap)
  • SDL: 3.5% of gross payroll (10+ employees)
  • WCF: 0.5% of gross payroll
  • PAYE/SDL filing: 7th of following month
  • NSSF/WCF: end of following month
  • Succession planning required for expat roles (2–5 year transition)

⚠️ Compliance Deadlines

  • PAYE + SDL: 7th of following month
  • VAT returns: 20th of following month
  • NSSF / WCF: end of following month
  • Provisional tax: end of each quarter
  • Annual returns: 30 June
  • Fiscal year: 1 July – 30 June
