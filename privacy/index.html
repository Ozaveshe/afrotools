Section 01

Introduction

AfroTools ("we", "us", "our") operates the website afrotools.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website and use our tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

By accessing or using AfroTools, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please discontinue use of the platform immediately.

This policy applies to visitors, users, and subscribers of AfroTools. Feature-specific disclosures, consent controls, and request channels are intended to support applicable privacy obligations; this policy is not a legal compliance certification.

Section 02

Data We Collect

AfroTools uses feature-specific data flows. The following list describes the main personal and non-personal data classes evidenced by current public workflows:

  • Email address — collected only when you voluntarily subscribe to updates, create an account, or use a labelled email-delivery workflow. Core public calculators remain available to guests without a paid subscription.
  • Account information — if you create an account, Supabase Auth manages the credential flow; AfroTools account services can store your email, profile, subscription status, preferences, and supported saved items.
  • Usage analytics — bounded page, device, country, session, and product-event metadata may be collected by configured analytics services after analytics consent. Raw sensitive workflow content is excluded; provider request metadata and identifiers can still exist.
  • Calculator inputs — local-only tools process salary, tax, health, and financial inputs in your browser. AI-assisted tools send a calculation summary and your question only after you continue from the AI notice. Saved scenarios are stored only when you use account sync.
  • Payment information — if you start a paid checkout, payment details are entered and processed on the configured provider flow. AfroTools receives the transaction and subscription metadata required to confirm entitlement, not the full card number or CVV entered with the provider.
  • Contact form submissions — when you use our contact form, your name, email, and message are stored by our form provider for the purpose of responding to your enquiry.

Section 04

Cookies & Local Storage

AfroTools does not enable advertising features in its intended analytics configuration. Functional storage and consented analytics can still create the identifiers described below:

  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics 4 can use _ga and _ga_* identifiers for bounded usage measurement. Microsoft Clarity may set analytics identifiers for session diagnostics after consent when configured. AfroTools excludes raw sensitive workflow content, but does not describe provider request metadata as anonymous. You can reject or withdraw analytics consent.
  • Supabase authentication cookies — if you sign in to an AfroTools Pro account, Supabase stores a session token to keep you logged in. These are strictly functional and are cleared when you log out.
  • localStorage preferences — we use browser localStorage to remember settings such as preferred country, dark/light mode, cookie consent choice, and recently used tools. Browser-local preferences stay on your device unless you choose a tool or feature that uses account sync, analytics, forms, or AI assist.

You can clear browser-local AfroTools data by clearing cookies and site data for afrotools.com. Account, provider, email, payment, or vault records require their corresponding deletion or account control.

For the full list of cookies, analytics identifiers, and how to change your choices, see our dedicated Cookie Policy.

AI Notice

AfroTools AI Privacy And Source Limits

AfroTools AI is a router and planning assistant. It helps you find the right AfroTools calculator, planner, document tool, or export workflow, but it is not a tax, legal, immigration, engineering, customs, medical, financial, admissions, employment, or government authority.

  • Deterministic routing first — core routing, tool search, manual launch, and supported prefill handoffs can work without model consent.
  • Deterministic routing works locally; optional AI may send the disclosed prompt or selected context to AfroTools servers and the configured provider after consent.
  • Sensitive details need care — career, financial, education, document, property, trade, legal, health, immigration, payroll, invoice, and business details should not be sent to optional AI help unless you understand what will be sent.
  • Saved AI projects are sanitized by default — saved AI summaries should not store raw CVs, resumes, invoices, PDFs, transcripts, salaries, payroll rows, private identifiers, client names, or raw prompts unless a future feature explicitly adds consent and retention controls.
  • Sources can go stale — rates, fees, deadlines, fuel prices, scholarships, visa rules, tax rules, customs rules, and construction assumptions need source and freshness checks before you rely on them.
  • Verify before high-stakes action — use official sources or qualified professionals before filing, applying, importing, building, hiring, paying, submitting documents, or making compliance decisions.
ℹ️

AfroTools AI should route, clarify, prefill, explain, and export around existing workflows. It should not invent official sources, make guarantees, or turn planning estimates into professional advice.

Section 06

Third-Party Services

AfroTools uses the following service classes in current public workflows. Each feature should send only the fields disclosed for that action:

  • Supabase — provides configured authentication, account database, workspace, and private-vault services. Supabase processes account identifiers and the records a signed-in user explicitly saves. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics 4 — can process bounded usage and request metadata after analytics consent when the property is configured. Raw sensitive workflow content is excluded from intended product events. Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity — consented product-session diagnostics such as page interactions, scroll behaviour, and heatmap-style UX signals. AfroTools does not intentionally send form values or sensitive calculator inputs to Clarity. Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • Paystack — can provide the configured checkout and payment-status flow. Card details are entered with the provider; AfroTools receives transaction and subscription metadata needed to confirm status. Paystack Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — can provide the configured checkout and payment-status flow. Card details are entered with the provider; AfroTools receives transaction and subscription metadata needed to confirm status. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Netlify — hosts AfroTools and processes form submissions. May log basic request metadata. Netlify Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI assist may send your prompt and selected workflow context only after you continue from the AI notice. Do not include personal identifiers or sensitive documents unless the workflow explicitly asks and you choose to continue. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log the request IP address. Google Privacy Policy

AfroTools does not sell personal data. Configured processors can receive the bounded data and request metadata described for their workflow above.

Section 07

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Newsletter subscriptions — email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe
  • Accounts and saved workspace items — retained while the account or saved item exists, subject to the account deletion and service-retention process
  • Contact form submissions — retained for the disclosed response purpose and the configured form or email service process
  • Analytics data — retained according to the configured analytics property and provider settings
  • Local calculator inputs — not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save a scenario to your account

You may request deletion of eligible account or contact data by emailing privacy@afrotools.com. We will acknowledge the request and explain any service-controlled or legally required retention.

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection legislation, including POPIA and NDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you

Section 10

Data Security

We implement a range of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS)
  • Supabase Auth manages account credentials; AfroTools application code is not intended to receive plaintext passwords
  • Full payment credentials are entered and processed on the configured payment-provider flow
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • Named local calculator and document operations avoid server upload; AI, sync, email, payment, analytics, TTS, lookup, and vault actions are separate disclosed network flows

No internet transmission or electronic storage method is completely secure. Security controls reduce risk but do not guarantee an outcome.
