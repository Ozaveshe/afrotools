Section 01

Introduction

AfroTools ("we", "us", "our") operates the website afrotools.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website and use our tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

By accessing or using AfroTools, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please discontinue use of the platform immediately.

This policy applies to all visitors, users, and subscribers of AfroTools, regardless of location. Section 02

Data We Collect

AfroTools follows a strict minimal data policy. We only collect what is necessary to operate and improve the platform. Here is a comprehensive list of the personal and non-personal data we may process:

  • Email address — collected only when you voluntarily subscribe to updates, create an AfroTools Pro account, or request a PDF report via email. We never require an account to use any free tool.
  • Account information — if you create an AfroTools Pro account, we store your email, hashed password (via Supabase authentication), subscription status, and account preferences.
  • Usage analytics — anonymised page views, device type, country, and session duration collected via Google Analytics 4 (GA4). No personal identifiers are linked to browsing behaviour. IP anonymisation is enabled.
  • Calculator inputs — local-only tools process salary, tax, health, and financial inputs in your browser. AI-assisted tools send a calculation summary and your question only after you continue from the AI notice. Saved scenarios are stored only when you use account sync.
  • Payment information — if you subscribe to AfroTools Pro, payment details are processed directly by our payment processors (Paystack and/or Stripe). We never see, store, or have access to your full card number, CVV, or banking details.
  • Contact form submissions — when you use our contact form, your name, email, and message are stored securely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry.

Section 04

Cookies & Local Storage

AfroTools uses no advertising cookies and no third-party tracking cookies that identify individual users. We use only the following:

  • Google Analytics 4 cookies — _ga and _ga_* cookies collect anonymous page view data (country, device type, page visited). No personal identifiers are linked. These cookies expire after 14 months. You can opt out via our cookie consent banner.
  • Supabase authentication cookies — if you sign in to an AfroTools Pro account, Supabase stores a session token to keep you logged in. These are strictly functional and are cleared when you log out.
  • localStorage preferences — we use browser localStorage to remember your settings such as preferred country, dark/light mode, cookie consent choice, and recently used tools. This data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to any server.

You can clear all AfroTools data at any time by clearing your browser's cookies and site data for afrotools.com.

Section 06

Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services to operate AfroTools. Each service only receives the minimum data necessary for its function:

  • Supabase — provides authentication and database services for AfroTools Pro accounts. Supabase processes your email and hashed password. Data is stored in compliance with SOC 2 Type II standards. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymised usage statistics with IP anonymisation enabled. No cross-site tracking. We do not enable Google Signals or advertising features. Google Privacy Policy
  • Paystack — processes payments for AfroTools Pro subscriptions in African currencies. Paystack is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant. We never receive or store your card details. Paystack Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — processes international payments for AfroTools Pro. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. We never receive or store your card details. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Netlify — hosts AfroTools and processes form submissions. May log basic request metadata. Netlify Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — the AI Advisor features send anonymised calculation summaries and your question (not your name, email, or personal identifiers) only after you continue from the AI notice. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log the request IP address. Google Privacy Policy

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any parties other than those listed above, and only to the extent necessary for the stated purposes.

Section 07

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Newsletter subscriptions — email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe
  • Pro accounts — account data is retained while your subscription is active and for 30 days after cancellation to allow reactivation
  • Contact form submissions — retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Analytics data — Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months per our GA4 configuration
  • Local calculator inputs — not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save a scenario to your account

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@afrotools.com. We will confirm deletion within 7 business days.

Section 08

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection legislation, including POPIA and NDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to deletion — request that we delete your personal data from our systems
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to the processing of your personal data for specific purposes
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw your consent to data processing at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal
  • Right to export — Pro account holders can export all saved data from their dashboard at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@afrotools.com. We respond to all requests within 7 business days. No fee is charged for exercising your rights.

Section 09

POPIA & NDPR Compliance

South Africa — Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA): AfroTools processes personal information in accordance with POPIA. We process personal information only with your consent or where necessary to provide our services. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or destruction. South African users may lodge complaints with the Information Regulator if they believe their rights under POPIA have been infringed.

Nigeria — Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR): AfroTools complies with the NDPR and the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023. We obtain consent before collecting personal data, process data only for stated purposes, and provide mechanisms for data subjects to exercise their rights. Nigerian users may direct complaints to the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) if they believe their data protection rights have been violated.

For users in other African jurisdictions, we apply the highest applicable data protection standard across all territories we serve. We are committed to respecting the privacy laws of every country from which our users access AfroTools.

Section 10

Data Security

We implement a range of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS)
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt algorithms via Supabase; we never store plaintext passwords
  • Payment information is handled exclusively by PCI DSS Level 1 compliant processors (Paystack and Stripe)
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • Client-side processing ensures that sensitive calculator and PDF data never reaches our infrastructure

While we take every reasonable precaution to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information to the highest practical standard.
