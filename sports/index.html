Frequently Asked Questions

Most tools cover the top 15 African economies: Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, Egypt, Ethiopia, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Côte d'Ivoire, Cameroon, Senegal, Morocco, Tunisia, and Angola. The Betting Tax and Match Ticket tools cover all 54 African countries.
Our betting tax rates are based on current legislation: Nigeria 10% WHT on net winnings (since 2023), Kenya 20% excise duty, Ghana 10%, Tanzania 18% VAT on winnings, South Africa income tax applies. Always verify with your local tax authority as rates may change.
Royalty rates are based on published industry data: Spotify $0.003–0.005/stream, Apple Music $0.005–0.007, YouTube Music $0.001–0.002, Boomplay $0.0025, Audiomack $0.002, Deezer $0.003–0.005. Actual rates vary by territory, label deal, and platform tier.
The estimator uses data from the Nigerian film industry market, which generates approximately ₦35–45 billion annually. Calculations factor in cinema split (50%), average ticket prices (₦3,000–6,000), opening weekend percentage (40–50% of total run), and streaming deal multipliers.
