Bayani mai muhimmanci

Kayan aikin nan suna bayar da bayanai ne kawai. Ba diagnosis ba ne, ba shawarar magani ba ne, kuma ba su maye gurbin likita, pharmacist, lab, hospital ko emergency care ba. Idan akwai ciwo mai tsanani, jini, zazzabi mai tsawo, matsalar numfashi, seizure, pregnancy emergency ko wata alama mai hatsari, a nemi taimakon lafiya kai tsaye.
