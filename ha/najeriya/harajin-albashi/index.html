Wane tsarin haraji ya shafe ni?

PITA (tsoho): Yi amfani da shi don shekarar haraji ta 2025 ko idan mai daukar aikinka bai canza ba tukuna. CRA (Consolidated Relief Allowance) yana rage kudin shiga da haraji zai shafa. NTA 2026 (sabo): Nigeria Tax Act ya fara aiki 1 Jan 2026. An soke CRA, an maye gurbinsa da iyakar ₦800K mara haraji da saukin haya. Idan ba ka da tabbas, lissafa duka ka kwatanta. Maballan da ke kasa suna bari ka canza nan take.

An zabi NTA 2026: An soke CRA. Iyakar ₦800,000 ba ya da haraji. Saukin haya ya maye gurbin CRA. Pension yana kan Basic+Housing+Transport kawai.

Fahimtar harajin Najeriya

Yadda PAYE ke aiki a karkashin NTA 2026

Muhimman abubuwa game da sabon tsarin harajin Najeriya, daga iyakar ₦800K zuwa saukin haya.

Me ya canza?

Nigeria Tax Act (NTA) ya maye gurbin PITA daga January 1, 2026. Babban canji: an soke CRA. A maimakonsa, kowa yana da iyakar ₦800,000 mara haraji. Idan kudin shigarka bai kai ₦800K a shekara ba, ba ka biyan harajin kudin shiga.

Saukin haya

Ya maye gurbin CRA a matsayin babban saukin haraji bayan pension. Ana lissafa shi a matsayin mafi karanci tsakanin 20% na hayar da aka biya ko ₦500,000 a shekara. Idan gidan naka ne kuma ba ka biyan haya, ba za ka iya neman wannan sauki ba.

Pension da NHF

Fansho na ma'aikaci yana nan 8%, amma yanzu yana aiki ne kan albashin asali + alawus din gida + alawus din sufuri, ba jimillar albashi ba. Masu daukar aiki suna bada 10%. NHF a Hausa 2.5% ne na albashin asali kuma har yanzu yana rage kudin shiga da haraji zai shafa.

Tambayoyin harajin Najeriya

Tambayoyi na PAYE da aka fi yi

Menene sabbin matakan PAYE na 2026?

A karkashin NTA 2026: 0% a kan ₦800,000 na farko; 15% daga ₦800,001 zuwa ₦3M; 18% daga ₦3M zuwa ₦12M; 21% daga ₦12M zuwa ₦25M; 23% daga ₦25M zuwa ₦50M; 25% sama da ₦50M.

Me ya faru da CRA a sabon dokar haraji?

An soke CRA a karkashin NTA 2026. Saukin haya ya maye gurbinsa: mafi karanci tsakanin 20% na hayar shekara da aka biya ko ₦500,000. Idan gidan naka ne, ba za ka iya neman saukin haya ba.

Yaushe Nigeria Tax Act zai fara aiki?

An sanya hannu June 26 2025. Zai fara aiki January 1 2026. Payslip na 2025 har yanzu yana amfani da PITA tare da CRA. Zabi “Tsohon tsari — PITA” a sama don lissafin 2025.

Yaya ake lissafa pension a karkashin NTA 2026?

A karkashin NTA 2026, pension na ma'aikaci (8%) yana aiki ne kan Basic Salary + Housing + Transport, ba jimillar gross ba. Shigar da kudin da ake lissafin pension a kai daban.

Zan iya yin lissafin net-to-gross?

Eh. Canza zuwa yanayin “Albashin hannu → Gross” a saman katin shigarwa. Shigar da albashin hannu da kake so, mu kiyasta albashin gross da ake bukata a PITA ko NTA.

Menene kudin mai daukar aiki idan ana daukar aiki a Najeriya?

Masu daukar aiki suna biyan 10% na gudummawar pension tare da NHF (2.5%) da NHIS idan sun shafi kamfani. Bangaren kudin mai daukar aiki a kasa yana nuna jimillar kudin kamfani da wajibai.

Menene bambanci tsakanin kason haraji na gaskiya da kason haraji na gaba?

Kason haraji na gaskiya = jimillar haraji / kudin shiga na gross. Kason haraji na gaba = kason haraji a kan Naira ta gaba da ka samu. Mai babban albashi na iya samun kason gaba mai yawa amma kason gaskiya ya kasance matsakaici saboda matakai masu hawa.

Akwai mafi karancin haraji a Najeriya?

A PITA, mafi karancin haraji 1% ne na kudin shiga na gross idan PAYE da aka lissafa ya fi kasa. A NTA 2026, iyakar ₦800,000 mara haraji ya maye gurbin ra'ayin mafi karancin haraji ga mutane.

Menene CRA a karkashin PITA?

Consolidated Relief Allowance = 20% na kudin shiga na gross tare da mafi girma tsakanin ₦200,000 ko 1% na kudin shiga na gross. Ana cire shi kafin a yi amfani da matakai shida (7% zuwa 24%). An soke shi a NTA 2026.

NHF wajibi ne?

National Housing Fund yana bukatar ma'aikata masu samun ₦3,000+ a shekara su bada 2.5% na basic salary. Gudummawar tana rage kudin shiga da haraji zai shafa. Aiki da shi na bambanta da mai daukar aiki, amma a dokance yana da wajibci karkashin NHF Act.

An tabbatar karshe: Maris 2026 · Tushe: NTA 2025, FIRS. Duba kuma: Kalkuleta VAT Najeriya, yadda ake lissafa PAYE a Najeriya, da jagorar kudin shigo da kaya Najeriya.

Hanyar lissafi

Kalkuleta yana mayar da albashi zuwa shekara idan ya dace, yana saka cire-ciren doka na ma'aikaci, yana bi da kudin shiga da haraji zai shafa ta matakan PAYE na kasa, sannan yana samo albashin hannu daga gross pay bayan PAYE da cire-cire. Layukan kudin mai daukar aiki bayani ne idan shafin ya nuna su.

Iyakokin da aka sani

  • Kiyasin bayani ne kawai. Ba shawarar kwararre ta haraji, doka, payroll ko filing ba ce.
  • Ka'idojin bangare, yanki, yarjejeniya, saukin haraji da tsari na musamman na iya zama ba a cika saka su ba.
  • Tabbatar da filing, rajista da wajibin biyan haraji da hukumar hukuma ko kwararren mai ba da shawara kafin turawa.

Misalan test-case

Abin da aka shigar: Albashin shekara kafin cire-cire: 0.
Abin da ake sa ran gani: PAYE da cire-ciren doka su zama 0 kuma albashin hannu kada ya zama kasa da sifili.
Dalili: Yana tabbatar da tushe na kudin shiga sifili kuma yana kama matsalar koma baya ta bashin haraji mara kyau.

Abin da aka shigar: An shigar da albashin gross na shekara a kudin shafin.
Abin da ake sa ran gani: Ana sarrafa kudin shiga da haraji zai shafa ta matakan da aka nuna, kuma albashin hannu ya zama gross pay bayan cire abubuwan da aka tsara.
Dalili: Yana tabbatar da kalkuleta yana amfani da hanyar da aka rubuta, ba kima madaidaiciya marar bayani ba.

Yi amfani da Kalkuleta PAYE Najeriya a matsayin kiyasin payroll, ba madadin filing ba

Wannan shafi na duba payroll, tattaunawar albashi, kiyasin HR da bitar akawunta ne. Ya kamata ya taimaka wa mai amfani ya fahimci hanyar lissafi kafin dogaro da filing na hukuma.

Amfanin kai tsaye

Mai kamfani, akawunta ko HR zai iya shigar da albashin gross na wata, alawus da zato na saukin haraji don kiyasta PAYE da albashin hannu kafin a kammala payroll.

Misalin aiki

Ga ma'aikaci da NGN 500,000 albashin gross na wata, yi amfani da kalkuleta don raba kudin shiga da haraji zai shafa, zato na saukin haraji, kimar PAYE da albashin hannu. Dauki sakamakon a matsayin adadin tsari har sai kwararren payroll ya tabbatar da cikakken bayani.
