FAQ

Tambayoyi

Me yasa ake bukatar counselling?

Genotype probabilities na iya shafar family planning. Counsellor ko doctor zai iya bayyana options, testing, antenatal follow-up da support ba tare da judgement ba.

Shin wannan tool yana bada treatment?

A'a. Yana nuna educational probabilities ne kawai. Duk treatment ko care plan ya fito daga qualified health professional.
