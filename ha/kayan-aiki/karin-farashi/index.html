Kalkuletan ƙarin farashi da farashin sayarwa

Lissafa daga ƙarin farashi ko tazarar riba ba tare da zato na haraji, canji, masana'antu ko shawarar farashi ba.

Ka'idojin lissafi

Daga ƙarin farashi: farashin sayarwa = farashi × (1 + ƙarin farashi ÷ 100).

Daga tazarar riba: farashin sayarwa = farashi ÷ (1 − tazarar riba ÷ 100).

Riba: farashin sayarwa − farashi. Tazara: riba ÷ farashin sayarwa × 100. Ƙarin farashi: riba ÷ farashi × 100.

Misali: farashi 100 da ƙarin farashi 50% suna bada farashin sayarwa 150, riba 50 da tazara 33.33%.
