Kasafin haihuwa - bayani ne kawai

Kiyasta kudin haihuwa ba tare da yin ikirarin shawarar likita ba.

Shafin nan yana lissafa ziyarar antenatal, haihuwa, kulawar bayan haihuwa da ajiyar kudin magani daga bayanan shafin tushe. Tabbatar da komai da antenatal clinic, likita, ungozoma ko kwararren ma'aikacin lafiya.

Gargadi

Ba ya maye gurbin antenatal care

Wannan kayan aiki na kasafi ne kawai. Ba ya gaya miki irin haihuwa da ya dace, ba ya gano matsala, kuma ba ya bada magani.

Tuntubi likita, midwife ko kwararren ma'aikacin lafiya. Idan akwai jini, ciwon ciki mai tsanani, ciwon kai mai tsanani, kumburi, zazzabi, motsin jariri ya ragu ko gaggawa, nemi kulawa nan take.
Sakamako

Kiyasin jimilla

Kiyasi ne kawai. Farashi da abin da inshora ke rufewa suna canzawa bisa asibiti ko clinic, kamfanin inshora, matsaloli, magunguna, gwajin lab da gari. Nemi farashi a rubuce da shawarar likita kafin yanke hukunci.

Tambayoyi

Shin kayan aikin na iya fada ko C-section ya kamata?

A'a. Wannan lissafin kudi ne kawai. Irin haihuwa shawarar likita ce da likita ko midwife zai tantance.

Me yasa aka kara ajiyar kudin magani?

Lissafin tushe yana amfani da kusan 15% na kudin haihuwa domin magunguna. Wannan ba takardar kudi ta gaskiya ba ce, kuma asibiti ko clinic na iya bambanta.
