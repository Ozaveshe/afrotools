Tambayoyi

Shin wannan ya isa kafin aure ko pregnancy?

A'a. Wannan lissafi ne na inheritance kawai. A bukaci lab confirmation da genetic counselling idan akwai genotype S ko C.

Me ake nufi da percentage?

Percentage yana nuna yiwuwar pattern a kowace pregnancy bisa Punnett square, ba tabbacin abin da zai faru a kowane haihuwa ba.
