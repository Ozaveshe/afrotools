Tace bukata

Gaskiyar neman tallafi

AfroTools ba ya bada tallafi kai tsaye. Shafin nan yana taimaka maka shirya takardu, tambayoyin cancanta da mataki na gaba. Kafin ka aika bayanan ka, tabbatar da asalin hukumar ko makarantar da ke bayar da tallafin.

Idan kana son karin tacewa da jerin dama, bude shafin Turanci da aka fi cika da bayanai.

Takardun farko

  • Sakamakon makaranta ko takardar shaidar karatu.
  • Shaidar mutum kamar NIN ko fasfo.
  • Wasikar bayyana buri.
  • Shaidar kudi idan ana bukata.
Fitarwa a cikin burauza

Ajiye shirin ko sakamako

Rubuta matakin tallafin karatu, fanni, kasa, takardu, ranar karshe da jerin tabbatarwa. Ana gina takaitawar a burauzarka domin ka kwafa ko sauke ta.

Tushe, sabuntawa da hanyar aiki

Verification, freshness and limits

Tushe: Based on the AfroTools workflow at /tools/scholarship-finder/.

Tabbacin hukuma: Ka tabbatar da matsayin tallafin, cancanta, ranar karshe, caji, takardu da hanyar mikawa daga mai bayar da tallafin ko makaranta ta hukuma.

Hanyar aiki: Shafin yana tattara matakin karatu, fanni da kasar da ake nema, sannan ya shirya jerin takardu da matakan tabbatarwa.

Iyaka: Wannan taimakon shirya neman tallafi ne, ba bayar da tallafi ba ne, ba jerin hukuma ba ne, ba tabbacin cancanta ko neman biyan kudi ba ne.
