Yadda ake karanta ribar jari

Ribar jari = ribar karshe ÷ jimillar kudi x 100. Idan ribar jari ta yi ƙasa ko ta zama asara, duba farashin kasuwa, asarar bayan girbi, sufuri, kudin aiki da kudin taki.

Tushen bayanai: FAOSTAT, ILO, World Food Programme, APHLIS da ma'aikatun noma na kasa. Kiyasi ne kawai; sakamako na gaskiya na iya bambanta bisa kaka, tsarin aiki da yanayin kasuwa. Sabuntawa ta karshe: 2026.
Ajiye sakamako

Fitar da tsarin noma

Bayan ka yi lissafi, kwafi ko sauke takaitaccen sakamakon domin raba shi da mai ba da shawarar noma, abokin kasuwanci ko mai saye.

A shirye don fitarwa.

Sirri: Bayanan da ka shigar suna aiki a browser dinka. Ba a aika farashin gona, girman gona ko kudin aiki zuwa sabar AfroTools daga wannan panel.

Tushen bayanai da iyaka

SourceBayanan FAOSTAT, ILO, WFP, APHLIS da bayanan noma na kasa a cikin AfroTools.

FreshnessAn tsara wannan a matsayin kiyasin 2026; farashin kasuwa, yanayi, kwari, kudi da dokokin gida na iya canzawa.

MethodologyInjin lissafi yana hada shigar da mai amfani da bayanan gona da aka ajiye a AfroTools. Sakamako yana taimakawa wajen tsara aiki, ba wai shawarar hukuma ba.

LimitationsTabbatar da farashi, iri, taki, ruwa, kasar gona, yanayi da shawarar jamiin fadakar da manoma kafin kashe kudi mai yawa.
