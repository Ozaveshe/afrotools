FAQ

Tambayoyi

Shin wannan sakamakon bill ne?

A'a. Estimate ne kawai. Bill na asibiti zai iya samun karin abubuwa kamar consultation, tests, drugs, admission, theatre, nursing, supplies ko follow-up.

Me ya sa private hospital ya fi tsada?

Yawanci saboda equipment, specialist access, waiting time, staffing da facility overhead. Duk da haka, farashi ya bambanta sosai daga city zuwa city.

Ina zan tabbatar?

Ka tambayi facility, insurer, doctor ko admin desk. Ka nemi abin da bill ya kunsa da abin da bai kunsa ba.
