Bayanan kiwon kifi

Abin da ke iya sauya riba

Mutuwar kifi, tsadar abinci, yanayin ruwa, farashin kasuwa da lokacin sayarwa na iya canza sakamako sosai. Yi amfani da wannan a matsayin tsarin tambaya kafin fara jari.

Shafin Turanci yana da karin cikakken tsarin kiwon kifi ga Najeriya.

Tuntubi kwararren kiwon kifi ko likitan dabbobi idan kana ganin cuta, rashin cin abinci ko matsalar ruwa.

Takaitaccen tabbaci

Kiwon kifi yana bukatar farashin 2026

Hanyar lissafi: Ana hada kudin fingerlings, abinci, ruwa, magani, asara da farashin sayarwa domin samun riba.

Tushen bayani: Farashin abinci da kifi daga kasuwa, bayanan gonarka, vet ko jami'in fadakarwa. Duba sabunta bayanai na 2026 kafin amfani.

Bayani mai muhimmanci: Wannan kiyasi ne; kada a dauka a matsayin shawarar kudi, haraji, lafiya ko doka.
