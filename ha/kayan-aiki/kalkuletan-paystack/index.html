Tambayoyi kan kuɗin Paystack

Biyan da bai kai NGN 2,500 ba kyauta ne?

A'a. A cinikin gida na Najeriya da bai kai NGN 2,500 ba, Paystack tana cire tsayayyen NGN 100 ne kawai. Har yanzu kuɗin kaso 1.5% yana aiki.

Mai tsara kuɗin yana ƙara haraji kan kuɗin Paystack?

Yana ƙara VAT 15% ga kuɗin Afirka ta Kudu kawai domin Paystack ta wallafa farashin a matsayin wanda bai haɗa VAT ba. Ba ya hasashen haraji ga Najeriya, Ghana ko Kenya.

An haɗa kuɗin payout da stamp duty?

A'a. Kuɗin payout transfer, stamp duty na Najeriya, refunds, chargebacks, canjin kuɗi, farashi na musamman da lokacin settlement ba sa cikin lissafin.
