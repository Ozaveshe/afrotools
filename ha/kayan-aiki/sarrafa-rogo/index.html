Me ya sa ake sarrafa rogo?

A Najeriya, garri shi ne mafi sanannen processed cassava product. Source data ya nuna farashin raw cassava, labour, packaging, transport da output conversion na Nigeria.

  • Fresh cassava na lalacewa da sauri, don haka processing yana rage asarar bayan girbi.
  • Garri da fufu flour suna da familiar market a Najeriya, amma quality da farashi suna bambanta da wuri.
  • HQCF da starch na bukatar equipment da quality control sosai. Tabbatar da buyer kafin saka jari mai yawa.
Price data for Nigeria: FAO, IITA cassava post-harvest reports and regional market surveys 2024-2025 kamar yadda source data ya nuna. Wannan planning ne kawai - tabbatar da farashin gida kafin saka jari.
