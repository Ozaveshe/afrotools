Tabbatarwa 2026

Duba takardar albashi kafin rabawa

Hanyar lissafi: tattara albashi na asali, alawus, cire-cire, PAYE, fansho, NHF ko wasu kudin kamfani, sannan bude cikakken kayan aikin idan kana bukatar PDF ko takardar da za a buga.

Tushen bayani: tabbatar da sabon adadin PAYE, fansho, NHF, social security, kudin kamfani da tsarin HR daga hukumar haraji, kamfani, akawu ko payroll advisor. An duba a 2026.

Iyaka: ba shafin hukuma ba ne, ba shawarar haraji ba, ba shawarar doka ba, kuma ba ya maye gurbin tsarin biyan albashin kamfani. Sirri: takaitawa tana cikin burauza; kada ka rubuta BVN, lambar asusu, lambar shaidar kasa ko bayanan maikaci masu sirri idan za ka raba su.

Mataki na gaba: kwafi takaitawa, bude cikakken mai gina takardar albashi na Turanci, ko tambayi AfroTools game da widget na biyan albashi da kalkuleta na musamman.
