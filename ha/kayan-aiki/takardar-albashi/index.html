Rahoto kuskuren lissafi

Abin da ake lissafawa

Gross earnings jimillar layukan kuɗin shiga ne. Kuɗin da aka biya yana ƙara reimbursement. Net pay yana cire duk cire-cire. Employer contribution yana ƙara kuɗin mai aiki ba tare da rage net ba.

Abin da ba a lissafawa

Babu PAYE band, ƙimar fansho, iyakar inshora, minimum wage, hutu ko haƙƙin doka da ake hasashe.
