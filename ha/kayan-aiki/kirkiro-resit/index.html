Resitoci na kasuwanci a Najeriya da Afirka

Yi amfani da wannan kayan aiki don resit na siyarwa, tax receipt, deposit, refund, da slip na tabbatar da biyan kudi. Yana tattara abubuwan da shago, makaranta, coci, masallaci ko freelancer ke bukata: customer, lambar resit, rana, kaya ko aiki, haraji, rangwame, delivery, hanyar biya da reference.

Country preset yana taimaka maka farawa da common VAT rates da local payment methods, amma ba ya maye gurbin shawarar haraji. Saka TIN, VAT, PIN ko lambar rijista da ta dace, sannan ka tabbatar da dokoki daga accountant ko hukumar haraji.

Zan iya ajiye resitocin baya?

Eh. Resitocin da aka ajiye suna browser dinka ta AfroTools saved-state storage. Za ka iya export JSON backup.

Zan iya amfani da shi don mobile money receipts?

Eh. Saka provider, mobile money ko payment reference, approval code da QR/payment link. Preview din resit zai nuna su a sashin biyan kudi.

Zan iya export resit don bookkeeping?

Eh. Export PDF don customer, CSV don layukan kaya, TXT don saukin record, ko JSON don backup da re-import.
