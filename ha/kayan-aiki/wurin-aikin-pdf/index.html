Tambayoyi

Shin fayilolin PDF suna fita daga burauza?

Wannan shafin yana karanta suna, adadi da girman fayil kawai domin tsara aiki. Aikin PDF da aka bude daga nan yana bin ka'idar local-first idan shafin kayan aikin ya bayyana haka.

Wane kayan aiki zan bude idan ina da PDF da yawa?

Idan kana son hada, sake tsara ko cire shafuka, bude Hada da Raba PDF. Idan matsalar girma ce, bude Matsa PDF.

Me yasa wasu hanyoyi suke kaiwa shafin Turanci?

Wasu manyan editoci ba su gama da Hausa ba tukuna. Shafin yana nuna hakan a fili kuma yana kai ka zuwa asalin kayan aikin da ya fi kammala.

Tushe da tabbatarwa - an duba a 2026

Hanyar aiki: Mai shirin yana karanta yawan fayil, jimillar girma, aikin PDF da ka zaba da bayaninka, sannan ya nuna hanyar AfroTools mafi kusa don hadawa/rabawa, matsawa, lambar shafi ko cikakken wurin aikin PDF.

Tushe da sabuntawa: Shawarwarin hanya suna bin taswirar aikin PDF ta AfroTools. Wasu manyan kayan PDF na iya bude shafin Turanci har sai cikakkiyar fuskar Hausa ta shirya.

Iyaka da sirri: Wannan shafin ba ya loda PDF. Yana amfani da sunan fayil da girmansa kawai domin shirya takaitaccen aiki; takardunka suna zama a naurarka sai idan shafin da ka bude ya bayyana wani abu dabam.

Bude cibiyar Takardu da PDF or nemi widget na aikin PDF.

Takaitaccen shirin aiki yana zama a na'urarka. Kada ka raba takardun banki, asibiti ko shaida da wani shafi idan ba ka tabbatar da sirri da manufar amfani ba.
