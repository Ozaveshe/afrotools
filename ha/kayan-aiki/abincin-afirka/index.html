Tsarin abinci - bayani ne kawai

Gina tsarin abinci na kwanaki 7 da abincin Afirka.

Shigar da shekaru, nauyi, tsayi, motsa jiki da burin ka domin lissafa BMR/TDEE da tsarin abinci. Idan kana da diabetes, ciki, matsalar koda, ulcer, rashin lafiyar abinci ko wata matsalar lafiya, tattauna da likita ko masanin abinci.

Tsaro

Ba tsarin abincin likita ba ne

Tsarin abinci na taimakawa wajen tunani da jerin siyayya. Ba ya maye gurbin masanin abinci ko tsarin abinci na likita.

Bayani ne kawai. Idan kana da wata matsalar lafiya, ciki, shayarwa, diabetes, hawan jini, matsalar koda ko rashin lafiyar abinci, nemi kwararren ma'aikacin lafiya kafin bin tsarin.
