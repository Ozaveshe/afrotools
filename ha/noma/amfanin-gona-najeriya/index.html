    Kalandar kaka

    Kore yana nuna watannin ruwa daga bayanan tushe na Najeriya; rawaya yana nuna watannin rani.

    Tushen bayanai: FAOSTAT, HarvestStat Africa, Nigeria National Bureau of Statistics, World Bank, CGIAR. Wannan kiyasi ne kawai; yanayin gona, farashin kasuwa da tsarin aiki suna iya canzawa. Sabuntawa ta karshe: 2026.

    Takaitaccen tabbaci

    Rubuta tushen amfanin gona da girbi

    Hanyar lissafi: Ana kwatanta girman gona, yawan iri, ruwan sama, taki da asarar girbi domin samun kiyasi.

    Tushen bayani: Tushen bayani ya kamata ya fito daga bayanan gonarka, jami'in fadakarwa, ko rahoton kasuwar yankinka. Duba sabunta bayanai na 2026 kafin amfani.

    Bayani mai muhimmanci: Wannan kiyasi ne na shiri; kada a dauka a matsayin shawarar kudi, haraji, lafiya ko doka. Ka duba kwararre ko hukuma kafin yanke hukunci.
