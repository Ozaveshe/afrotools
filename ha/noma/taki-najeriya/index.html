Soil test ya fi kiyasi kyau

Soil test yana nuna abin da ƙasa ta riga ta samu. Kiyasin nan na taimaka wajen planning ne, amma kada ka dogara da shi kaɗai wajen yawan taki, musamman idan farashin taki ya yi tsada ko ƙasa tana da matsala.

  • Kada a zuba urea a saman ƙasa mai zafi da bushewa.
  • Guji zuba taki lokacin ruwa mai ƙarfi ko waterlogging.
  • Adana taki a busasshen wuri kuma ka nemi shawarar extension officer idan ba ka da tabbaci.
Tushen data: FAOSTAT, Nigeria National Bureau of Statistics, World Bank, CGIAR. Fertilizer recommendation kiyasi ne; tabbatar da shi da extension officer, ADP ko qualified agronomist. Sabuntawa ta karshe: 2026.
