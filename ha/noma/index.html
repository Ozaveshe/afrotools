Bude

Hanyar lissafi da zato

Hanyar lissafin ita ce: fili a hekta x kiyasin amfanin gona x farashin tan = kudin shiga. Sannan kayan aiki, aiki, sufuri da kariyar canjin farashi suna rage kudin shiga. Wannan ba farashin hukuma ba ne; yana taimaka maka ka san inda za ka tambayi karin bayani.

Tushe da sabunta bayanai

  • FAO kalandar amfanin gona domin tunatarwa kan lokacin shuka.
  • NBS Najeriya domin rahotannin farashin abinci da kididdiga.
  • Ma'aikatar Tarayya ta Noma da Tsaron Abinci domin tsarin noma da sanarwa.
  • ARCN domin bincike da fadakarwa ga manoma.

Takaita hadari

An sabunta wannan shafin a 17 Mayu 2026. Farashin kasuwa a kalkuletan nan na mai amfani ne ya shigar, ba ciyarwar farashi kai tsaye ba. Tabbatar da farashi da mai saya, kasuwa, kungiyar manoma, ADP ko jami'in fadakarwa kafin kashe kudi.

Abin da za a tuna

Wannan shafi kayan taimako ne, ba shawarar noma ta hukuma ba. Yanayi, kwari, ruwan sama, bashin kaka, sufuri, samun taki, ingancin iri da lokacin sayarwa na iya sauya sakamako. Idan shirin yana da babban jari, nemi shawarar kwararren mai fadakarwa ko mai ba da shawarar gona a yankinka.
