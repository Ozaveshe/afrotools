Gida/Noma
Layin noma na Najeriya da farko

Kayan aikin noma da kasuwa a Hausa.

Fara da abin da manomi ko dan kasuwa a Najeriya ke bukata yau: kiyasta amfanin gona, buhunan taki, riba da asara, sarrafa rogo, da kwandon farashin abinci. Manyan ka'idojin lissafi da bayanai sun fito daga shafukan asali na AfroTools.

Shafukan Hausa da suke aiki

Wadannan shafuka suna aiki a Hausa kuma suna amfani da bayanan Najeriya ko feed na farashi iri daya da shafin asali na Turanci.

Amfanin gona

Amfanin gona na Najeriya

Kiyasta tonnes/ha, jimillar amfanin gona, zangon kudin shiga da ratar amfanin gona ga rogo, masara, shinkafa, doya da sauran crops.

Bude Taki da NPK

Taki Najeriya

Kiyasta N, P, K, buhunan NPK/Urea, jadawalin amfani da kudin kasuwa ko tallafi idan bayanan tushe sun bayar.

Bude Riba da asara

Ribar gona

Shigar da farashin kasuwa, kudin aiki, taki, sufuri, ajiya da bashi domin ganin ribar net, ROI da inda kudi zai rufe kansa.

Bude Rogo

Sarrafa rogo

Kwatanta garri, fufu flour, HQCF, chips da starch ta hanyar riba a kowane zagaye, lokacin mayar da kudi da matakin kayan aiki.

Bude Kasuwa

Kwandon kasuwa

Duba farashin kayan abinci da aka tabbatar, gina kiyasin kwandon gida, kuma aika rahoton farashi idan kana da bayanai.

Bude Turanci tukuna

Ban ruwa Nigeria

Kalkuletan ban ruwa bai samu shafin Hausa ba tukuna, don haka an bar shi a Turanci har yanzu.

Bude shafin Turanci

Abin da wannan batch bai taba ba

Ba a mayar da dukkan shafukan noma na kasashe 54 zuwa Hausa ba. Yawan iri, ban ruwa, kiwon kaji, kiwon kifi, greenhouse, farashin kayan kasuwa da takardun fitarwa sun rage a Turanci har sai an yi batch nasu.

Bayani da hankali

Kalkuletan nan kayan tsara aiki ne. Farashin kasuwa, tallafi, dokokin gwamnati, rates da samuwa suna canzawa. Tabbatar da bayanai da extension officer, ADP, cooperative, mai saya ko tushe na hukuma kafin kashe kudi.
