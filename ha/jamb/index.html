Hanyar karatu

Yadda za ka yi amfani da AfroJAMB ba tare da rudewa ba.

Matakai masu sauki

  • 1. Fara da quick mock.Ka ga karfinka da raunin subjects ba tare da bata lokaci ba.
  • 2. Duba kuskurenka.Ka rubuta topics da suka fi fitowa a mistake list ko tutor response.
  • 3. Yi revision a matakai.Raba karatu zuwa English, core subjects da electives. Ka hada WAEC/NECO idan admission yana bukata.
  • 4. Kiyasta aggregate.Yi amfani da JAMB Aggregate calculator, amma ka tabbatar da cutoff na hukuma daga makaranta.

Ka tuna

  • Ba portal na hukuma ba.AfroJAMB kayan taimakon karatu ne, ba ya maye gurbin JAMB portal ko school portal.
  • Shafukan cikin AfroJAMB suna Turanci yanzu.CBT, tutor, archive da shafukan tambayoyi ba a fassara su a wannan pass ba.
  • Tambayoyi ba a canza su ba.Mun bar archive da engines kamar yadda suke domin kada logic ko data ya lalace.
AfroTools ba shi da alaka ta hukuma da JAMB, WAEC, NECO ko wata jami'a. Yi amfani da wannan shafi a matsayin taimakon shiri, sannan ka tabbatar da subject combination, ranar exam, cutoff, rajista da ka'idojin admission daga portal din JAMB na hukuma, school portal ko mai ba da shawarar karatu.
