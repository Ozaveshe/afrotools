JAMB Kimiyya

Shirya tsarin atom, ma'aunin sinadarai, acid da alkali, sinadaran carbon, gas da tambayoyin gwaji. Wannan jagora yana taimakawa a Hausa kafin bude tarin tambayoyi.

Fara nan

Sanya bayanan da suka fi muhimmanci, sannan ka bude kayan aikin da ya dace. Takaitaccen shirin yana zama a browser.

Bude cikakken kayan aiki
Aiki: Hanya: yi amfani da wannan a matsayin tsarin farko. Tabbatar da farashi, dokoki, jadawali, fayil ko bayanan hukuma kafin yanke shawara.

Ajiye shirin ko sakamako

Rubuta batutuwan kimiyya, wuraren rauni, atisayen tsoffin tambayoyi da shirin bita mai lokaci. Ana gina takaitawar a burauzarka domin ka kwafa ko sauke ta.

Tushe, sabuntawa da hanyar aiki

Verification, freshness and limits

Tushe: Based on the AfroTools workflow at /jamb/chemistry/.

Tabbacin hukuma: Ka tabbatar da sabuwar manhaja, ranar jarrabawa, bukatun darasi da kaidar admission daga JAMB ko makaranta.

Hanyar aiki: Shafin yana tattara abin da dalibi yake son karantawa sannan ya kai shi atisayen Kimiyya na AfroJAMB da ya dace.

Iyaka: Wannan taimakon bita ne, ba ajiyar tambayoyin hukuma ba ne, ba makin jarrabawa ko alkawarin admission ba ne, kuma ba kayan magudi ba ne.

Hanyar aiki da tabbaci

Hanyar aiki: Wannan shafi yana amfani da tsarin AfroJAMB, don haka ana kwatanta shirin karatu iri daya da shafin tushe. Ba a canza tambayoyi ko lissafin maki ba.

Tabbaci: Wannan ba shafin hukuma ba ne; ka duba sabbin bayanai kan ranar jarrabawa da dokokin JAMB daga hukumar da ta dace kafin yanke shawara.
