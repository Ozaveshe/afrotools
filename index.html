Ask AfroTools AI what you want to do. It detects the likely workflow, asks for missing details when needed, and routes you to the right tool without hiding the underlying calculator or source assumptions.

FAQ

AfroTools AI, in plain terms.

A simple front door to African tools, not a replacement for calculators, source checks, or professional judgment.

What is AfroTools AI?

AfroTools AI is a routing and planning layer for AfroTools. You describe a task, and it helps you find the right African calculator, document workflow, country page, checklist, export, widget, or API-backed tool.

Is AfroTools free?

Core calculators, document tools, country pages, and directory browsing are free. Some advanced documents, saved projects, richer exports, higher API usage, or partner workflows may have limits or paid plans.

Is my data private?

Many tools run locally in your browser where possible, especially document and planning workflows. If a workflow uses optional AI assistance, account sync, email, payments, analytics, forms, or a partner handoff, AfroTools labels that separately before private content is sent or stored.

Can I use it for tax, legal, visa, or customs decisions?

Use AfroTools for planning estimates, checklists, source links, and next steps. For tax, legal, immigration, customs, medical, financial, engineering, or other high-stakes decisions, verify with the official authority or a qualified professional.

Does AfroTools AI replace professionals?

No. AfroTools AI can organize a question, route you to a tool, summarize assumptions, and prepare a checklist. It does not replace accountants, lawyers, immigration advisers, engineers, doctors, customs brokers, or other qualified professionals.
