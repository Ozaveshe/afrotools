Our Standards

Not Generic Calculators
With African Flags.

01
Accuracy First

Every rate is pulled from the official revenue authority — FIRS, KRA, GRA, SARS. When rates change, we update. Not estimates. Not approximations.

02
Country Context

Nigeria's PAYE has different bands than Kenya's. Ghana's SSNIT is not the same as South Africa's UIF. We never copy-paste logic between countries.

03
Results Tell a Story

Tier 1 tools include an AI Financial Advisor. Not just numbers — context. What your effective rate means. What you could do differently.

04
Mobile First

Over 60% of Africa's internet users are on mobile. Every tool is designed for the phone first, then the desktop. Never the other way around.

05
Always Free

No paywalls. No sign-up walls. No "premium" tier. Every calculator, every country, every result — free. Supported by advertising and sponsors.

06
Never Broken

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks to break. No APIs to go down. If the internet works, AfroTools works. Built to last 10 years.
