About AfroTools

Why AfroTools exists

AfroTools is a free financial platform serving all 54 African countries with 2,594+ tools including PAYE tax calculators, currency converters, budget planners, PDF workspace, invoice generators, and an AI-powered financial advisor. Founded in 2025, AfroTools sources tax logic directly from national revenue authorities — FIRS (Nigeria), KRA (Kenya), GRA (Ghana), SARS (South Africa), TRA (Tanzania), and more — and operates in English, French, and Swahili. Local calculators run in the browser by default; AI Advisor requests send a calculation summary to Anthropic only after you invoke AI, and signed-in save/sync stores selected data in your account.

Our Standards

Not Generic Tools
With African Flags.

Every tool passes 6 rules before it ships.

01
Local Accuracy First

Every rate pulled from FIRS, KRA, GRA, SARS. When Kenya repealed SHIF relief in Dec 2024, we updated same day. When Ethiopia restructured to 6 bands in July 2025, same day.

02
African Context

Nigerian plots. South African morgen. M-Pesa till QR codes. WAEC grade scales. Generator fuel vs solar ROI. No tool is built for London and relabelled for Lagos.

03
AI That Explains

Select tools include an AI Advisor — not just numbers, but context. What your effective tax rate actually means. Powered by Claude Sonnet.

04
Clear Privacy States

Local tools stay in your browser by default. AI, account sync, email, forms, analytics, and payments are labeled separately before data moves.

05
Mobile First, Bandwidth Aware

Over 60% of Africa's internet users are on mobile. Image compressor defaults to minimal output. Every tool under 100KB uncompressed. Works on 2G.

06
Built to Last

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No React, no frameworks, no CDN dependencies for core logic. Every tool will still work in 10 years.
