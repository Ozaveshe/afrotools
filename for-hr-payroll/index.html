HR and payroll pain points

Payroll buyers need clarity before they talk to sales. Calculators turn vague questions into qualified briefs.

Salary questions repeat every month

Net pay, statutory deductions, employer cost, and payslip questions can be answered with guided estimates.

Demo leads lack context

Calculator inputs can qualify country, workforce size, role type, salary range, and support needs.

Multi-country payroll is hard to explain

Start with one market or a small country set before promising broad coverage.

Compliance needs clear boundaries

Pilot copy stays estimate-first, source-backed, and reviewed before any payroll filing, tax compliance, or statutory approval language appears.
