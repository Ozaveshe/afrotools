Buying or importing a car in Ghana

Ghana buyers need to see official taxes separately from third-party port and clearing costs because the market often blends them together.

Hidden costs to budget for: ICUMS valuation, age penalties, local shipping-line fees, safe bond/rent, trade plates, and agent charges can move the final number.

Age and compliance risk: Right-hand-steering restrictions and age penalties should be checked before paying for a car abroad.
