Common Questions

Uniquely African Tools - FAQ

What is the Japa calculator?+
'Japa' is Nigerian slang for emigrating. The Japa Cost Calculator estimates total relocation costs: visa fees, flight, shipping, housing deposit, and first-month expenses - for 12 African origin countries and 7 destinations.
What is an Ajo/Chama calculator?+
Ajo (Nigeria), Chama (Kenya), and Tontine (West Africa) are rotating savings groups. Our tool tracks contributions, rotation schedules, and interest on late payments.
Do you have a generator cost calculator?+
Yes - compare monthly generator fuel spend against a solar panel system investment, with Nigerian and Kenyan fuel and electricity prices baked in.
How many African tools are available?+
We currently have 26 uniquely African tools live and free to use. These cover savings groups, remittances, cultural calculators, cost of living, fuel costs, mobile money fees, and much more.
Are these tools really free?+
Yes, all 26 African tools are completely free. Some include optional AI advisors powered by Claude AI for deeper insights, but the core calculators are always free.
