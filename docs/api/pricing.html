Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, the new limits take effect immediately. When downgrading, the change applies at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my daily limit?

Requests beyond your daily limit receive a 429 status code. Your limit resets at midnight UTC. We never charge overage fees — upgrade to a higher plan if you need more calls.

Do test keys count against my quota?

No. Sandbox keys (afro_test_*) return sample data and do not count against your production quota. They share the Free plan rate limits.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

The Free plan is available indefinitely with 100 calls/day. This lets you fully test the API before committing to a paid plan. No credit card required to start.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly. Contact api@afrotools.com for annual pricing.
