# AfroTools Pro App Product Briefs

Updated: 2026-06-30

## Scope

This is the sellable-product brief for the 20 AfroTools Pro apps. It combines the current app registries, `docs/PRO-APP-READINESS.md`, and a representative scan of official competitor/product pages. It is not a full pricing teardown, and it must not be used to claim features that are still shell, local-only, or unverified.

Current product truth: Payroll is the only fully active Pro app. The other Pro apps can become sellable earlier if they are framed as practical local workspaces, export packet builders, and review-ready operating systems instead of pretending to be bank-sync, official-filing, payment, marketplace, clinical, or legal platforms.

## Global Positioning

AfroTools Pro should not try to out-enterprise every incumbent. The wedge is narrower and stronger:

- Africa-first defaults: country, currency, language, document names, tax/source confidence, and practical work habits.
- Mobile-first operations: usable on a phone by an owner, clerk, accountant, stylist, technician, pastor, farm manager, or family admin.
- WhatsApp-native handoff: copy blocks, follow-up text, reminders, receipts, and status updates that users can paste into the channels they already use.
- Packet-first value: PDFs, CSVs, evidence folders, checklists, review notes, payslips, invoices, handoff packs, and audit trails.
- Local-first trust: device save by default where account save is not verified, with clear labels for local, account, draft, review, and official-source confidence.
- Source confidence: every changing rate, deadline, filing, fee, and compliance claim needs source date, confidence, and "planning estimate" language.

## Paid Packaging

Sell the current Pro universe as a set of operating packets and saved workspaces:

| Package | Buyer promise | Product requirement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pro Starter | Save work locally, produce clean packets, and stop losing decisions in chats and notebooks. | Strong local state, exports, clear labels, no fake sync. |
| Pro Workspace | Account-backed records, Vault, reminders, team roles, and repeatable work queues. | Verified account save, permissions, activity history, and upgrade boundaries. |
| Pro Review | Expert-ready payroll, tax, legal, tender, property, or compliance handoff packs. | Review checklist, source labels, packet exports, and explicit human review boundary. |
| Partner Lane | Accountant, consultant, agency, school, clinic, or community admin manages multiple clients. | Client list, role labels, packet history, and safe invitations only after team flow is real. |

## App Briefs

### 1. AfroPayroll

- Status: Active Pro app.
- Best buyers: SMEs, accountants, NGOs, distributed African teams, payroll admins, and businesses that already need payslips and statutory records.
- Market products: [Workpay](https://www.myworkpay.com/), [SeamlessHR](https://seamlesshr.com/), [PaySpace](https://www.payspace.com/), [Gusto](https://gusto.com/).
- Market pattern: Payroll competitors sell automation, HRIS, statutory compliance, salary disbursement, employee self-service, and multi-country support.
- AfroTools USP: "Payroll packets you can understand and defend." AfroPayroll can lean into country-pack confidence, source review status, payslip packets, manual approval trails, and accountant-ready exports instead of overclaiming full disbursement or statutory remittance.
- Make it better now: Add a month-close checklist, country-pack freshness banner, employer summary PDF, employee change log, "ready for accountant" packet, and clearer warnings for unsigned/device-only runs.
- Sellable product shape: Per-company payroll workspace with saved runs, payslip packets, review notes, export history, and optional payroll-country-pack review.
- Guardrails: Do not claim salary disbursement, statutory payment, or official filing unless the flow is live, permissioned, and tested.

### 2. Tax Compliance

- Status: Shell/local review packet.
- Best buyers: Small business owners, accountants, consultants, startups entering a new country, and operators tracking tax tasks manually.
- Market products: [Avalara](https://www.avalara.com/), [TaxJar](https://www.taxjar.com/), [TaxTim](https://www.taxtim.com/za/), [SimplePay](https://www.simplepay.co.za/).
- Market pattern: Competitors sell tax automation, sales/VAT calculations, filing workflows, practitioner review, and deadline guidance.
- AfroTools USP: "African tax obligations organized into a defensible packet." The value is not automated filing yet; it is knowing what to collect, when to review, what source was used, and what to hand to an accountant.
- Make it better now: Add country obligation templates, source review cards, deadline confidence labels, import checklist, evidence pack builder, and accountant-facing export.
- Sellable product shape: Tax calendar plus evidence packet subscription for businesses with recurring VAT, payroll, withholding, or annual return tasks.
- Guardrails: No filing, payment, approval, or official deadline guarantee without source-led validation and a live submission integration.

## Cross-App Improvements

These upgrades make almost every Pro app more sellable without overbuilding:

1. Setup score: Every app should show "profile complete", "records added", "packet ready", and "account save status".
2. Packet builder: Every app should export one clean PDF/CSV/JSON packet with a visible local/account/source label.
3. WhatsApp copy drawer: Reminder, receipt, handoff, follow-up, and status text should be copyable, never auto-sent until messaging integrations exist.
4. Review mode: Mark fields as "missing", "needs source", "ready for accountant/lawyer/client", or "not official".
5. Source and freshness cards: Any changing rule, fee, rate, deadline, or market price needs source date, review status, and confidence.
6. Sample business templates: Each app should have realistic African sample templates, not empty enterprise dashboards.
7. Device/account clarity: All local-only routes must show device-save labels and account-save limitations.
8. Pro Vault integration: Exported packets should appear in Vault only when real account or session storage exists.
9. Client/partner lane: Accountants, consultants, agencies, and admins should eventually manage multiple client workspaces.
10. "Do not claim" tests: Add copy checks for filing, legal advice, medical advice, payment collection, bank sync, private intelligence, and official approvals.

## Next Build Bets

The fastest path to sellable Pro is not all 20 apps at once:

1. Harden Payroll as the flagship proof of Pro value.
2. Turn Books, HR, and Tax into an "SME back-office packet" bundle.
3. Turn Seller and Beauty into the first daily-operator bundle because their pain is frequent, visible, and WhatsApp-heavy.
4. Add Field Service and Food once packet export and local records are strong.
5. Keep Legal, Clinic, Trade, Grants, and Stream conservative until source, privacy, and advice boundaries are hardened.
