ABOUT THESE TOOLS

Built for daily SME decisions

The hub is organised around decisions operators actually make: whether a stall is profitable, whether a shipment still has margin, whether a promotion damages profit, or whether a cash-flow dip needs action before payroll and rent are due.

Inputs and saved briefs stay in the browser unless a specific tool clearly asks for consent. Rates, fees, taxes, bank terms, freight, supplier quotes, and marketplace rules can change, so treat outputs as planning estimates and verify before paying, filing, borrowing, or publishing a quote.

Use the planner above to choose a lane, copy a brief, and open the first tool with a clearer task in mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between the POS Agent Calculator and the POS Transaction Fee Calculator?

The POS Agent Business Calculator models the operator's revenue, provider charges, cash costs, operating costs, and return on startup capital. It does not quote a provider's current customer transaction fee; enter and verify the fee assumptions that apply to your outlet.

Is the Mini-Importation Calculator accurate for 1688.com sourcing?

Use it as a planning estimate for landed cost, FX sensitivity, duty, freight, clearing, and margin. Consolidator rates, FX, customs treatment, and local charges can change, so verify with your supplier, clearing agent, bank, and customs source before paying or quoting.

Can I use these tools for a formal business plan or investor pitch?

Yes, for initial estimates and sanity checks. Tools like the Startup Runway Calculator, TAM/SAM/SOM Calculator, and Cash Flow Forecast Tool produce outputs you can use as a starting point for a pitch deck or business plan. For a formal investor presentation, you should validate the numbers with actual market research and have an accountant review financial projections.

Are all these tools free?

Core calculation flows are available without an account. Some optional save, cloud, branding, PDF, or AI-assisted paths may ask for consent, sign-in, or a Pro plan where the tool clearly says so.
