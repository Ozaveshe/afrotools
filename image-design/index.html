Frequently asked questions

The image category is meant to be practical and fast, so these are the common questions people ask before jumping in.

Are AfroTools image tools really free?

Yes. The tools in this directory are free to open and use, with browser-based processing as the default approach so you can get in, finish the job, and export quickly.

Do my images get uploaded to a server?

No. These tools are designed to process your work locally in the browser, which is better for privacy and especially helpful on slower or metered connections.

Can I use these tools on my phone?

Yes. The directory and the linked tools are mobile-friendly, so you can search, open, edit, and export directly from Android or iPhone browsers.

What kind of work fits this category?

This section covers optimization, document prep, identity photos, creator graphics, and quick business design jobs like flyers, cards, QR codes, and simple branding assets.
