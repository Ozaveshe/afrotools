Whether you're a social media manager in Lagos, a photographer in Nairobi, or a student in Accra preparing documents, image tools are essential. AfroTools provides professional-grade image processing that runs entirely in your browser, no expensive software subscriptions, no slow cloud uploads eating your data bundle, no watermarks on your exports.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are AfroTools image tools really free?

Yes. All image tools — compressor, resizer, QR generator, background remover, and more — are 100% free with no watermarks, no sign-up, and no usage limits. Processing happens in your browser, so your files never leave your device.

Do my images get uploaded to a server?

No. AfroTools processes images entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy. This also means the tools work offline once loaded.

What image formats are supported?

Most tools support JPG, PNG, WebP, and GIF. Some tools like the Image Format Converter also handle BMP. The Image Compressor can output in WebP for maximum file size reduction.

Can I use these tools on my phone?

Yes. Every tool is mobile-responsive and works on Android and iOS browsers. Touch gestures work in image preview areas, and all buttons meet the 44x44px minimum tap target size.

What makes AfroTools different from iLoveIMG or TinyPNG?

AfroTools is built for African users. The Passport Photo Tool knows specifications for all 54 African immigration offices. The QR Code Generator has presets for M-Pesa and MTN MoMo. All processing is browser-based — critical for users on metered data connections where uploading to servers is expensive.
