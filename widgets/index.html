Partner terms

These terms protect the free distribution model and keep the commercial upgrade clean.

Sponsor a tool Build a custom calculator Use AfroTools API

Attribution and backlink

Free embeds must keep the visible AfroTools badge and backlink. Do not hide it, remove it, style it to be invisible, block it in a noindex wrapper, or present the free widget as your own product.

No deceptive use

Do not use widgets in scam, misleading financial advice, deceptive lead capture, illegal offers, or pages that imply AfroTools endorses a sponsor without an agreement.

White-label needs approval

Changing attribution, adding partner branding, collecting leads, using custom formulas, or routing analytics to a partner requires Widget Pro or a custom widget agreement.

Calculation trust stays intact

Partners can sponsor placement or request a custom build, but they cannot buy misleading formula changes or hide source and disclaimer requirements.
