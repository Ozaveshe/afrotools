Operational notes

        

          
Media
Store metadata and preview uploads here first. For production, point `imageUrl` to the approved storage path or checked-in asset.

          
Financing
Keep offers country-specific. These are partner estimates unless a verified lender feed is wired.

          
Risk
Risk and liquidity are runtime scores driven by `scoringModels`, freshness, confidence, and landed-cost sensitivity.

          
Publish
Export the full pack after review, then update the repo data file or database publish job.
