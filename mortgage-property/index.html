Mortgage & Property — FAQ

Which mortgage types do you support?↓
Nigeria NHF (National Housing Fund), Kenya Housing Finance, South Africa bond — plus standard fixed/variable rate mortgages for any country.
Do you include stamp duty and transfer costs?↓
Yes. We calculate all-in purchase costs: stamp duty, legal fees, agent commission, survey fees, and transfer duty — per country.
Can I compare rent vs buy?↓
A rent vs buy analyser for Nigerian, Kenyan and South African markets is in our pipeline.
