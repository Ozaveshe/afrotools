Maswali ya mara kwa mara

Small projects can look profitable before MRV, validation and verification costs. Net revenue is the useful view.
Many nature projects withhold a shiriki of credits to protect against reversal risk such as fire, drought or illegal harvesting.
Usually aggregation is more realistic. The tool flags small scale as a cooperative or program candidate.
