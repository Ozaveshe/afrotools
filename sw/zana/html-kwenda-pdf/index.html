Badilisha HTML kuwa PDF mtandaoni

AfroTools HTML kwenda PDF hubadilisha HTML ghafi au rich text kuwa hati za PDF kwa browser-local rendering. Tumia templates za ankara, ripoti, barua na vyeti, au bandika HTML yako yenye inline styles, majedwali, orodha na picha. Chagua ukubwa wa ukurasa, mwelekeo, margins, render quality, CSS, headers, footers, namba za kurasa na tarehe kabla ya export.

Kila kitu hufanyika client-side kwa kutumia html2canvas na jsPDF assets za ndani. Maudhui yako hayatoki kwenye kifaa chako, hivyo yanafaa kwa ankara, mikataba, ripoti, risiti, barua za shule na nyaraka za ndani. Kwa kunasa live URLs kwa usahihi wa pixel, tumia server-side renderer; kwa HTML au rich text uliyobandika kwa faragha, zana hii inatoa kasi na udhibiti.

Maswali ya mara kwa mara

Je, HTML yangu inapakiwa kwenye seva?
Hapana. Ubadilishaji wote hufanyika kwenye browser yako kwa html2canvas na jsPDF files za ndani. HTML yako haitoki kwenye kifaa chako.
What page sizes and orientations are supported?
The converter supports A4, A3, Letter, and Legal page sizes in portrait or landscape. You can choose normal, narrow, wide, no-margin, or custom margin settings.
Naweza kubadilisha rich text yenye formatting kuwa PDF?
Yes. Switch to the Hariri rich text tab to use a visual editor with bold, italic, underline, headings, lists, links, and text alignment. The formatted content converts directly to PDF.
Does the converter handle multi-page content?
Yes. Long content is split across multiple PDF pages based on your chosen page size, margins, and render scale. Headers, footers, page numbers, and dates are applied to every page.
Je, CSS styles huhifadhiwa kwenye PDF?
Inline styles na field ya CSS maalum hu-render kwenye PDF. Picha za nje zinaweza kuhitaji CORS, hivyo picha zilizopachikwa au same-origin ndizo za kuaminika zaidi.
