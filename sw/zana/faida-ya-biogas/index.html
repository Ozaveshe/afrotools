Biogas Digesters in Africa A Sustainable Energy Solution

Biogas digesters convert animal waste into cooking gas and organic fertilizer (bioslurry). In Kenya, Ethiopia, Rwanda, and Nigeria, household biogas has replaced LPG and firewood for millions of rural families. This kikokotoo helps you assess whether a biogas digester makes financial sense for your livestock holding.

Disclaimer: Gas yield figures are averages based on livestock type. Actual matokeo depends on feed quality, temperature, and digester design.
