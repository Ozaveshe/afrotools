Likizo za umma Afrika

African countries observe a mix of national, religious, and cultural holidays. Nigeria has approximately 13 public holidays, while South Africa has 12 national holidays. Kenya observes 12 gazetted public holidays, and Ghana has 14. Islamic holidays (Eid al-Fitr, Eid al-Adha, Maulid) shift by about 10-11 days each year due to the lunar calendar.

How many public holidays does Nigeria have?

Nigeria has approximately 13 official public holidays, including New Mwaka, Workers' Day, Democracy Day (June 12), Independence Day (October 1), Christmas, and Islamic holidays which vary by lunar calendar.

Nini hutokea likizo ikiangukia weekend?

Kanuni hutofautiana. South Africa mara nyingi huhamishia Jumatatu likizo iliyoangukia Jumapili. Nigeria inaweza kutangaza Jumatatu kuwa likizo, na Kenya ina utaratibu unaofanana katika baadhi ya matukio.

Kwa nini tarehe za likizo za Kiislamu ni makadirio?

Likizo za Kiislamu hufuata kalenda ya mwezi, hivyo tarehe kamili hutegemea kuonekana kwa mwezi. Tarehe zilizoonyeshwa ni makadirio na zinaweza kusogea kwa siku 1-2.
