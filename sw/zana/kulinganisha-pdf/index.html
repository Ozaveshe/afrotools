Linganisha PDF mtandaoni bila malipo

Zana ya AfroTools Kulinganisha PDF hukuruhusu kulinganisha PDF mbili pembeni kwa pembeni moja kwa moja kwenye browser. Kama unakagua mabadiliko ya mkataba, kulinganisha drafts au kuhakiki edits, this tool highlights zilizoongezwa, zilizofutwa, changed pages, zinafananaity, and visual layout changes.

Kila kitu hufanyika kwenye browser yako. PDF zako hazipakwi kwenye seva yoyote, jambo linalosaidia kulinda faragha ya nyaraka nyeti.

Maswali ya mara kwa mara

Maandishi diff hufanya kazi vipi?
The tool extracts text from each page of both PDFs, compares it at word, sentence, or line level, and highlights zilizoongezwa and zilizofutwa in side-by-side review panes.
What does the visual diff show?
Visual diff renders both PDF pages as images, highlights changed pixels, reports a visual-change percentage, and supports slider or side-by-side review modes.
Is my PDF uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your PDFs never leave your device.
Naweza ku-export ulinganisho?
Yes. You can copy the summary or download a text report with document names, page counts, zinafananaity, and per-page changes.
