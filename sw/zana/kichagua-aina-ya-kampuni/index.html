      Chanzo cha tabia ya zana

      Ukurasa huu umeandikwa kwa Kiswahili lakini umeoanishwa na kichagua aina ya kampuni ya Kiingereza kwa assumptions, scripts na disclaimer posture.

      Kanusho: Zana hii ni ya kupanga. Haitoi ushauri wa kisheria, haithibitishi registration approval, name availability, filing acceptance, fee accuracy au compliance. Thibitisha na registrar, company secretary, lawyer, accountant, tax authority au official portal.

      Maswali ya mara kwa mara

      Je, hii ni filing rasmi?
      Hapana. Hii ni zana ya kupanga, kukadiria au kuandaa rasimu. Tumia official portal au mtaalamu kabla ya filing yoyote.
      CAC na CIPC ni nini?
      CAC ni Corporate Affairs Commission ya Nigeria. CIPC ni Companies and Intellectual Property Commission ya Afrika Kusini. Majina haya yanabaki kwa sababu hutumika kwenye portals na forms rasmi.
      Kwa nini annual returns na compliance calendar zina disclaimer kali?
      Deadlines, penalties, fees na forms hubadilika. Kampuni inaweza kupata penalties au strike-off ikiwa filing itachelewa, kwa hiyo thibitisha kila tarehe na registrar au tax authority.
