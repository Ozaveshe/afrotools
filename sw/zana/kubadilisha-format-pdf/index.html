Free Faragha PDF Badilishaer

The AfroTools PDF Badilishaer is a browser-based toolkit for everyday document conversion. Badilisha DOCX files to PDF, turn XLSX, XLS, and CSV sheets into PDF tables, extract selectable PDF text, or export PDF pages as PNG or JPG images. All processing happens locally in your browser, so your files are never uploaded to a server.

Word to PDF Conversion

Pakia a .docx file and convert it to a clean PDF document. The converter preserves common document structure such as headings, paragraphs, bold and italic text, lists, links, and tables. Choose between A4 and Letter page sizes, then set normal, narrow, or wide margins. Complex Word layouts, custom fonts, floating images, and exact print fidelity may vary because the conversion runs fully in the browser.

Excel to PDF Conversion

Transform spreadsheets into professional PDF tables. The tool supports .xlsx, .xls, and .csv files, and handles workbooks with multiple sheets. Tables are formatted with bold headers, alternating row colours, and borders. Choose landscape orientation for wide spreadsheets, and use auto-fit to keep columns inside the page.

PDF Maandishi Toaion

Toa selectable text from PDF documents page by page. The extracted text can be copied to the clipboard or downloaded as a plain text file. This works with PDFs that contain real text. Scanned documents are images and need OCR before text can be extracted.

PDF to Picha Hamisha

Hamisha all pages or a selected range of pages as PNG or JPG images. The converter renders pages in your browser, packages the output into a ZIP file, and keeps predictable filenames so the images are ready for sharing, archiving, or uploading into another workflow.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a Word document to PDF? ▼
Select the "Word to PDF" tab, upload your .docx file by dragging it onto the upload area or clicking to browse. Choose your page size and margin preferences, then click "Badilisha to PDF". Your PDF will be generated instantly in your browser.
Can I convert Excel files with multiple sheets? ▼
Yes. When you upload a multi-sheet workbook, the tool shows tabs for each sheet. You can select which sheet to convert. Each sheet is rendered as a styled table in the PDF.
Can I turn PDF pages into images? ▼
Yes. Select "PDF to Pichas", upload your PDF, choose PNG or JPG, set the render scale, and optionally enter a page range such as 1, 3-5. The tool creates a ZIP file with one image per selected page.
Is my data safe? ▼
Absolutely. All processing uses JavaScript libraries running entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. No data leaves your device at any point during conversion.
What formatting is preserved in Word to PDF? ▼
The converter preserves headings, paragraphs, bold, italic, underline, lists (ordered and unordered), tables, and hyperlinks. Complex formatting like custom fonts, images, and advanced layout may not be fully supported.
Why can't I extract text from my scanned PDF? ▼
The PDF to Maandishi feature extracts selectable text embedded in the PDF. Scanned documents are images without text data. For scanned PDFs, use our OCR ya PDF tool which uses optical character recognition to read text from images.
