Free Online Nafasi ya Kazi ya PDF

The AfroTools Nafasi ya Kazi ya PDF is a complete suite of PDF tools that runs entirely in your browser. Unganisha multiple PDFs into one document, split large files into smaller ones, compress files to reduce size, add watermarks, insert page numbers, sign documents, and convert between formats. Hakuna kupakias to external servers, no file size limits, and no sign-up required.

Available Tools

  • Unganisha PDF — Combine two or more PDF files into a single document. Drag to reorder pages before merging.
  • Gawanya PDF — Toa specific pages or split a large document into smaller files by page range.
  • Bana PDF — Reduce PDF file size while maintaining quality, ideal for email attachments.
  • Add Watermark — Overlay text or image watermarks on every page of your document.
  • Ukurasa Numbers — Add page numbers in various positions and formats to any PDF.
  • Nenosiri Protect — Encrypt PDFs with AES-256 in your browser.
  • Saini PDF — Chora or type your signature and place it on any page of a document.

Privacy & Security

All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to any server. This means your sensitive documents stay completely private, and the tool works even without an internet connection after the page loads.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a file size limit?

There is no hard file size limit. Since processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's available memory. Most devices can handle files up to 100MB without issues.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. All PDF operations run locally in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your documents never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Does this work on mobile devices?

Yes. The Nafasi ya Kazi ya PDF is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets, though complex operations like merging many files may be faster on a desktop or laptop.
