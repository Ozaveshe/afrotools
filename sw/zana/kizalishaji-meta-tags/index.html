Kwa nini meta tags ni muhimu kwa SEO

Meta tags ni elementi za HTML zinazoeleza ukurasa kwa search engines na mitandao ya kijamii. Hazibadili ranking peke yake kama ubora wa maudhui na backlinks, lakini huathiri jinsi matokeo yanavyoonekana na kama mtu atabofya. Tumia title na description zilizo wazi, halafu hakiki matokeo kwenye Search Console.

Meta tags muhimu kwa kila ukurasa

  • Title tag: The most important on-page SEO element. Appears in search results, browser tabs, and social shares.
  • Meta description: The snippet shown below your title in search results. Not a ranking factor but crucial for CTR.
  • Canonical URL: Huonyesha search engines URL inayopendelewa kwa ukurasa na kupunguza duplicate content issues.
  • Open Graph tags: Dhibiti jinsi ukurasa wako unavyoonekana ukishirikiwa kwenye Facebook, LinkedIn, WhatsApp na majukwaa mengine.
  • Twitter Card tags: Control the appearance of shared links on Twitter/X.
  • Robots meta: Instructs search engines whether to index and follow links on the page.

Maswali ya mara kwa mara

Urefu mzuri wa title tag ni upi?
Google huonyesha takribani herufi 50-60. Title ndefu inaweza kukatwa. Weka keywords muhimu mwanzoni na jina la brand mwishoni kwa separator kama | au -.
Does meta description affect rankings?
Not directly. Google has confirmed that meta descriptions are not a ranking factor. However, a compelling description increases click-through rate, which can indirectly improve rankings. Google may also bold matching search terms in the description.
Picha za OG zinapaswa kuwa ukubwa gani?
The recommended size is 1200x630 pixels (1.91:1 ratio). This works well on Facebook, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, and Twitter. Use JPEG or PNG format. Keep file size under 1MB for fast loading.
