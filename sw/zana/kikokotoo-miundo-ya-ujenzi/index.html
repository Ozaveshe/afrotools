Jinsi ya kutumia matokeo

  • Chukua matokeo kama range ya awali, si quote ya supplier au mkandarasi.
  • Hifadhi assumptions: eneo, rates, waste, contingency, VAT na scope.
  • Linganishia na drawings, site visit, bei za eneo na professional advice.
  • Kwa structural, electrical, septic, generator na borehole, tumia mtaalamu mwenye leseni kabla ya kununua au kujenga.

structural, beam, column, slab, load, engineer. Terms hizi zimehifadhiwa kwa sababu hutumika kwenye drawings, supplier quotes, software, forms na search. Maelezo yanabaki kwa Kiswahili ili user aelewe matumizi yake.

FAQ

Je, Kikokotoo Miundo ya Ujenzi inatoa idhini rasmi?

Hapana. Ni msaada wa kupanga. Approval, design, fees na standards hutegemea nchi, mji, local authority, professional registration na project scope.

Nitumie lini mtaalamu?

Kabla ya construction, wiring, foundation, septic works, borehole drilling, pool works, generator installation au submission ya drawings. Matokeo ya zana ni mwanzo wa mazungumzo, si mwisho wa uamuzi.
