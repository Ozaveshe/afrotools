Mambo muhimu ya mini-grid za solar Afrika

Mini-grid za solar zinaweza kusaidia jamii, shule, kliniki na biashara kupata umeme pale grid kuu inapochelewa au haina uhakika. Kikokotoo hiki hutoa tathmini ya kwanza ya CAPEX, mapato na muda wa kurudisha gharama kabla ya proposal ya DFI au investor.

Kanusho: Haya ni makadirio tu ya uchunguzi wa awali. Feasibility study kamili inahitaji site assessment, load survey, vibali, uchambuzi wa demand na engineering design ya kina.

Mbinu ya kukokotoa

Tunatumia idadi ya kaya, biashara, matumizi ya kWh, system size, CAPEX, tariff na mapato ili kutoa makisio ya uwezo, revenue na payback.

Makadirio tu

Si engineering design, idhini ya regulator, quote ya installer au ahadi ya grant. Hakiki demand, ardhi, permits, tariff, O&M na grid-extension risk kabla ya kuwekeza.

Chanzo na uhakiki

Vyanzo ni load survey, utility/regulator, installer, engineer, community records na site assessment. Tarehe ya ukaguzi: 2026-06-19.

Uwezekano wa Mini-grid kwa Kiswahili. Tumia kuchunguza viability ya mini-grid kabla ya feasibility study. Miradi ya kweli huhitaji engineer, permits na demand survey.

Matokeo ni makadirio ya planning. Hayabadilishi tariff rasmi, quote ya mtoa huduma, usanifu wa engineer, taarifa ya hali ya hewa, medical advice, insurance quote, certificate ya carbon au maamuzi ya mamlaka. Hakiki namba na authority, supplier, installer, engineer au professional anayefaa.
