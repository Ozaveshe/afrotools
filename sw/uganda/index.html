Viwango vya Kodi ya Mshahara (PAYE) 2024/25

Uganda ina vipimo vinne vya kodi ya mshahara. Kiwango cha kwanza (hadi UGX 235,000) hakina kodi.

Kipimo Mshahara wa Kila Mwezi (UGX) Kiwango cha Kodi
Kipimo 1 UGX 0 – 235,000 0% — Hakuna kodi
Kipimo 2 UGX 235,001 – 335,000 10%
Kipimo 3 UGX 335,001 – 10,000,000 20%
Kipimo 4 UGX 10,000,001 na zaidi 40%
