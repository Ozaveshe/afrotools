Ask AfroTools AI FAQ

What is AfroTools AI?

AfroTools AI helps you find the right AfroTools page. Type what you need, then continue in the calculator, planner, document tool, or export screen.

Does AfroTools AI replace the tools?

No. It helps choose a tool, asks for missing details, and can prepare a handoff. The calculator or tool page still does the actual work.

Can AfroTools AI prefill tools?

For supported low-risk workflows, AfroTools can carry a short browser handoff into the tool. You can review, edit, or ignore the details before using the output.

Does AfroTools AI work with French and Swahili?

The English command page is the main AI launcher today, and it now points into French and Swahili product surfaces. Localized tools keep their own language, source notes, and workflows as they are upgraded.

Is private content sent to AI by default?

No. You can find and open tools without model consent. Optional AI help asks before sending sensitive details.

Are high-stakes outputs official decisions?

No. Tax, visa, scholarship, customs, fuel, health, legal, and finance workflows are planning support. Check current official sources or qualified professionals before acting.
