FAQ

Will the Career Agent fabricate experience for me?

No. It should not invent employers, degrees, dates, certifications, metrics, or achievements. It can suggest structure and wording for details you provide.

Can I continue without AI-generated text?

Yes. Manual templates, ATS guidance, and local exports remain available without model assistance.

Does the CV handoff expose my private data in the URL?

No. The Ask AfroTools AI handoff uses short-lived browser storage for supported setup fields instead of putting private CV content into query strings.
