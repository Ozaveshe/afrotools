FAQ

Can AfroTools AI file my taxes or payroll?

No. It points you to calculators and planning checklists. Final filings should be verified with the revenue authority or a qualified accountant.

Does sponsor placement affect the formulas?

No. Sponsor-safe surfaces must be clearly labeled and cannot change formulas, rankings, or compliance warnings.

Which SME tasks are supported first?

PAYE, payroll planning, VAT, invoices, cash flow, TIN, and business-registration checklist help are supported first.
