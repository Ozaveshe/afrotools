FAQ

Can AfroTools AI produce approved building drawings?

No. It can open floor-planning and cost tools, but approvals and professional drawings require qualified local professionals.

Does it estimate materials?

It can open BOQ, paint, tiles, roofing, renovation, and construction-budget tools that provide planning estimates.

What should I verify before building?

Confirm land title, permits, setbacks, structural design, contractor scope, material specs, and current local prices.
