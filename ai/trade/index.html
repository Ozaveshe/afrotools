FAQ

Can the Trade Advisor guarantee the final duty?

No. It produces a planning estimate and a verification checklist. Customs valuation and classification remain the official decision points.

Does it support cars and non-vehicle products?

Yes. Vehicle prompts can open car import tools, while electronics, clothing, machinery, and other goods can open import-duty, landed-cost, and HS-code tools.

What inputs improve the estimate?

Destination country, product category or vehicle details, purchase value, currency, freight, insurance, port, origin country, and any user FX rate.
