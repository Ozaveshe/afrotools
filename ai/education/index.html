FAQ

Does AfroTools AI apply for scholarships for me?

No. It helps organize your profile, likely tools, document checklist, and source links. Applications still happen on the official provider or university site.

Can I use it without sending my academic details to AI?

Yes. Local matching and tool links work without model consent. Optional AI text should require consent before profile details are sent.

Which countries does the planner support?

The planner is designed for African origin countries and common destinations such as Canada, the UK, Germany, Australia, the US, and regional African study options where data exists.
