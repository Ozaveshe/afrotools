FAQ

Is the relocation planner immigration advice?

No. It helps organize costs, documents, and next steps. Immigration and visa decisions must be checked with official sources or qualified advisers.

Can I compare two African cities?

Yes. Use city, budget, household size, rent preference, and transport assumptions to route into cost-of-living and rent tools.

Does AfroTools store my salary or family details?

The default Ask AfroTools AI project model should save only sanitized summaries and structured inputs, not raw sensitive personal details.
