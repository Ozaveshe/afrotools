What We Can Help With

Our team reviews every message personally. Here are the types of enquiries we handle most often:

  • Bug Reports & Calculation ErrorsIf a calculator gives incorrect results, tell us the inputs you used and what the correct figure should be. We typically fix reported bugs within 48 hours.
  • Feature Requests & Tool SuggestionsWant a new tool or country added? We prioritise based on community demand. High-request tools move to the front of our roadmap.
  • Partnership & CollaborationWe work with African fintech companies, NGOs, government agencies, and educational institutions to build custom tools and integrations.
  • Advertising & SponsorshipReach African professionals making real financial decisions. We offer banner placements, sponsored tools, and newsletter sponsorships.

Response times: We aim to respond within 1-2 business days. Bug reports and calculation errors are treated as high priority and often resolved same-day.

AfroTools is built by a small, Africa-focused team passionate about creating world-class tools for the continent. Every message matters to us because our users shape what we build next.
