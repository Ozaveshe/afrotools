Buyer questions

Fast answers for teams deciding whether this should be a widget, sponsored placement, custom build, or API pilot.

What fintech use cases fit AfroTools best?

Fee explainers, loan comparison, remittance education, FX impact, savings goals, merchant fees, affordability checks, and product education workflows are the best first fit.

Can a fintech sponsor a tool?

Yes, if the placement is clearly labeled and the calculator logic, ranking, and source notes remain independent of sponsorship.

Can AfroTools provide live quotes or approvals?

Not by default. Live quotes, credit decisions, regulated advice, or approval claims require a separate reviewed integration and source contract.

How should an API pilot be scoped?

Pick one user decision, one country or country set, one endpoint family, and a clear boundary for what data or calculations AfroTools will provide.
