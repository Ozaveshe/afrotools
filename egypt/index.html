What Changed
Key 2025 Facts

📋 For Employees

  • Personal exemption: E£20,000/year (reduces taxable income)
  • Top marginal rate: 27.5% above E£1,200,000/year
  • Social insurance: 11% of insurable salary (employee share)
  • Min wage: E£7,000/month (private sector, March 2025)
  • Housing allowance exempt up to 30% of total salary for SI
  • End-of-service gratuity and pensions — generally exempt

🏢 For Employers

  • Employer social insurance: 18.75% of insurable salary
  • Max insurable salary: E£16,700/month (Jan 2026)
  • Withholding: monthly, remitted within 15 days
  • New employees: register with ETA and NOSI within 15 days
  • Annual reconciliation: by 31 January of following year
  • e-Tax system: mandatory for designated companies (Law 386/2023)

⚠️ Compliance Deadlines

  • Monthly payroll tax: 15th of following month
  • Annual personal tax return: 31 March
  • Annual employer reconciliation: 31 January
  • VAT returns: end of following month
  • Social insurance: 15th of following month
  • Late payment: penalties + interest per ETA regulations
