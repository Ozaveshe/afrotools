Date: 2026-05-17

Prompt: 122 - Swahili Beta Definition Lock

## Definition

Swahili beta complete means the public Swahili product surface is stable enough to use and validate:

- `852` Swahili HTML routes exist and `850` are indexable.
- `700/700` Swahili registry hrefs resolve.
- `469/469` `/sw/zana/` registry hrefs resolve, including nested TIN routes.
- `415/465` direct `/sw/zana/<slug>/` routes are registry-covered.
- Hreflang proof is clean with `0` warnings.
- `check-links` proof reports `0` broken internal links.
- Search is healthy for the core query buckets.
- Core lanes are usable: salary/PAYE, country hubs, PDF, business, construction, language/translation, communications, careers, blog bridge, API bridge, and developer hub.
- Bridge-only blog/API routes explain what remains English-only.
- Specialist-risk tools are labeled as planning or educational aids and are not promoted blindly.

## Non-Goals

Swahili beta complete does not mean all English routes are translated, all specialist pages are expert-reviewed, all blog/API content is localized, source-layer migration is complete, or unrelated French/Hausa/cars/generated dirty-tree work is resolved.

## Operating Rule

Future Swahili registry promotion should come only from verified ready pools. Do not chase coverage by exposing pages that are still bridge-only, specialist-risk, source-owner unclear, or visibly under-polished.
