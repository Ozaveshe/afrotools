# AfroTools 500-Backlink Campaign

Started: 2026-07-12

## Goal

Earn and verify 500 legitimate backlinks to AfroTools. The campaign counts a link only after a public placement is live and its source URL has been recorded. The secondary goal is at least 250 unique referring domains.

## Quality Rules

- No automated forum posting, fake personas, account farms, comment spam, paid-link schemes, or reciprocal-link rings.
- Community links must answer a real question or share a genuinely useful AfroTools resource.
- Directory submissions must be relevant, maintained, and visibly reviewed or curated.
- Outreach must be personalized to a real page, audience, or workflow.
- Record `dofollow`, `nofollow`, or `unknown` only after the live placement is inspected.
- Do not count pending submissions, profiles without a public link, duplicate URLs, or links that cannot be independently opened.
