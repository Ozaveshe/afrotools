# AfroTools Games Asset Prompts

Use these prompts when generating the first visual asset pack for AfroTools Games, starting with the daily puzzle world and the new fun-games catalog. Keep outputs clean, practical, Africa-relevant, and suitable for a static educational product. Prefer WebP for final site assets, with PNG only when transparency is needed.

## 1. AfroTools Games Tool Card

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: AfroTools tool card image, 1200x900
Primary request: Create a clean editorial illustration for "Daily Brain Teaser" on AfroTools.
Scene/backdrop: A calm study desk with a notebook, pencil, small market price slip, map outline shapes, and puzzle tiles arranged neatly.
Subject: Practical puzzle learning, not medical brain imagery.
Style/medium: Premium flat-3D editorial illustration, polished web product asset.
Composition/framing: Centered object cluster, generous negative space, readable at small card size.
Lighting/mood: Bright, focused, trustworthy, modern.
Color palette: AfroTools blue, neutral white and slate, tiny gold accent.
Text: No text inside the image.
Constraints: No flags, no stereotypes, no cartoon characters, no visible brand logos, no watermark.
Avoid: Literal human brain, gambling visuals, dark arcade mood, clutter.
```
