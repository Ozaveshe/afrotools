AfroTools — Built for Every African Nation

AfroTools provides country-specific financial tools for all 54 African nations. Each country page contains PAYE salary tax calculators, VAT calculators, and financial reference tools built around the specific tax legislation, social security rates, and financial regulations of that country. Tools are updated when governments amend tax laws.

From the Nigerian FIRS (Federal Inland Revenue Service) PAYE tax bands to Kenya's KRA income tax brackets, Ghana's GRA SSNIT contributions, South Africa's SARS UIF rates, Tanzania's TRA deductions, and Egypt's ETA income tax bands — every calculation is verified against official government sources.

Whether you are in North Africa (Egypt, Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Libya), West Africa (Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Cameroon), East Africa (Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Ethiopia), Southern Africa (South Africa, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Botswana, Namibia), or Central Africa (DR Congo, Congo, Gabon, Cameroon, CAR), AfroTools has the financial calculators your country needs.
