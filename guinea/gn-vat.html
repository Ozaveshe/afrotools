Calculation methodology

The shared local engine adds or extracts the 18% standard rate under CGI Article 373 and rounds monetary outputs to whole GNF. A 0% path requires explicit confirmation of an exact Article 373 export or international-transport case. Article 362 exemptions remain separate. GNF 1 billion and GNF 500 million are review boundaries, not automatic registration verdicts.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Article 362 exemption and Article 373 zero-rate classification require transaction-level legal and invoice evidence.
  • Sector-specific, regional, treaty, relief, and special-regime rules may not be fully modeled.
  • Confirm filing, registration, and remittance duties with the official authority or a qualified adviser before submission.
