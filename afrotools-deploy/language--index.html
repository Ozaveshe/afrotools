Common Questions

Language & Translation — FAQ

Which languages do you support?↓
Swahili, Yoruba, Hausa, Amharic, Igbo, Zulu, and Somali are in our first phase, covering over 400 million speakers.
Do you do machine translation or phrasebook?↓
Both. Phrasebooks for travel and business, plus AI-assisted translation for common African language pairs.
Can I type in Amharic or Arabic script?↓
Yes. Script input tools for Amharic (Ge'ez), Arabic (for North African languages), and Tifinagh (Tamazight) are in development.
