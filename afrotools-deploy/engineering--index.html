Common Questions

Engineering & CAD — FAQ

Which standards do you implement?↓
Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS), South African Bureau of Standards (SABS), Standards Organisation of Nigeria (SON), and Egypt's EOS for structural and electrical calculations.
Can I size a solar system for my building?↓
Yes. Our solar calculator takes your load profile, location, roof orientation, and battery backup requirement to size an off-grid or hybrid system.
Do you have a Bill of Quantities (BOQ) generator?↓
Yes — a basic BOQ generator for Nigerian and Kenyan construction rates is in our pipeline.
