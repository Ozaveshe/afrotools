Common Questions

VAT & Business Tax — FAQ

What is the VAT rate in Nigeria?↓
Nigeria's VAT rate is 7.5%, as set by the Finance Act 2020 (increased from 5%). Some goods are zero-rated or exempt under the VAT Act.
How do you handle VAT-exempt goods?↓
Our calculators flag exempt categories per country — basic food items, medical supplies, educational materials — and exclude them from VAT.
Can I calculate VAT for cross-border African trade?↓
Yes. Destination-based VAT for AfCFTA trade is in our pipeline — the only calculator on the continent to handle this.
