FAQ

Floor planner questions

Is this floor planner free to use?

Yes. It runs in your browser and lets you sketch plans, load templates, estimate materials, and export planning files for free.

Can I use it for African house plans?

Yes. The gallery includes self-contained rooms, bungalows, family houses, rental compounds, shops, salons, classrooms, clinics, and small offices.

Does the planner create a construction BOQ?

It creates a planning BOQ estimate from your rooms, walls, openings, rates, allowances, country, and currency.

Can this replace an architect or permit drawing?

No. Use it for early budgeting and discussion, then confirm structure, drainage, approvals, and final rates with qualified local professionals.
