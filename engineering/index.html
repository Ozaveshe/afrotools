Free Engineering Calculators for African Construction

Africa is building. From Lagos high-rises to Nairobi apartment blocks to Johannesburg industrial parks, construction is booming across the continent. But most engineering software is expensive, designed for Western standards, and assumes materials and labour costs from Europe or North America. AfroTools builds engineering calculators calibrated for African construction realities.

The BOQ Builder generates professional bills of quantities with 11 QS categories, 5 pre-built templates, and 9 currencies. The Concrete Mix Calculator covers M5 to M30 grades with Dangote, BUA, PPC, and Bamburi cement prices. The Electrical Load Calculator sizes wiring, breakers, and generators using NEMSA, KEBS, and SANS standards. The Rebar Calculator generates bar bending schedules with cutting lengths and lap lengths.

The Paint Calculator includes 15+ African brands (Dulux, Berger, Plascon, Crown) with real coverage rates. The Roof Calculator handles long-span aluminium, step tiles, and truss timber quantities. The Water Tank Calculator sizes tanks with pump and pipe recommendations. The Borehole Cost Estimator covers drilling costs across 4 countries with geology types and pump comparisons.

The Generator Fuel Calculator compares generator vs grid vs solar costs with maintenance schedules and carbon estimates. The Building Cost Estimator provides per-m² costs across 10 African cities. The Land Size Converter handles Nigerian plots, SA morgen, Egyptian feddan, and Kenyan eighth-acres. The Unit Converter covers 20 categories including engineering units.
