FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AfroDraft really free?
Yes, completely free. All 155+ features are available without signing up, downloading software, or paying anything. We believe every African student and professional deserves access to quality CAD tools.
Can I open AfroDraft files in AutoCAD?
Yes. Export your drawing as DXF and open it in AutoCAD, BricsCAD, FreeCAD, or any other CAD software that supports the DXF format.
Does it work offline?
Once loaded, AfroDraft runs entirely in your browser. Your drawings are saved locally on your device. An internet connection is only needed for the initial page load.
Can I use it on mobile?
AfroDraft is touch-enabled and works on tablets and phones. For the best experience, we recommend a device with at least a 10-inch screen and a stylus.
How does it compare to AutoCAD?
AfroDraft covers the core 2D drafting workflow: drawing, modifying, dimensioning, layers, and exporting. It won't replace AutoCAD for 3D modelling or complex BIM workflows, but for 2D technical drawings it's a solid free alternative.
Where are my drawings stored?
All drawings are stored in your browser's local storage on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. You can also export files to save them permanently on your computer.

AfroDraft: Free Professional 2D CAD Software for Africa

AfroDraft is a browser-based 2D computer-aided design (CAD) application built specifically for African engineers, architects, students, and technical professionals. Unlike traditional CAD software that requires expensive licenses, powerful hardware, and lengthy installations, AfroDraft runs directly in your web browser with zero setup.

Why AfroDraft Exists

Across Africa, engineering and architecture students are held back by the cost of professional CAD software. A single AutoCAD license costs more than many students earn in a month. AfroDraft eliminates this barrier entirely. Every feature — from basic line drawing to full DXF export — is free and always will be.

Professional-Grade Features

With over 155 commands, AfroDraft covers the complete 2D drafting workflow. Create precise geometry with lines, circles, arcs, polylines, splines, and polygons. Modify your designs with move, copy, rotate, scale, trim, extend, offset, fillet, and chamfer tools. Add professional annotations including linear, aligned, angular, and radial dimensions, text labels, leaders, and cross-hatching.

The precision system includes 15 snap types (endpoint, midpoint, center, intersection, perpendicular, tangent, and more), ortho mode for straight-line constraints, and polar tracking for angular precision. A full layer management system lets you organise complex drawings with custom colors, linetypes, and lineweights.

Export and Compatibility

AfroDraft exports to DXF format, the industry standard for exchanging 2D drawings between CAD applications. Open your AfroDraft drawings in AutoCAD, BricsCAD, FreeCAD, LibreCAD, or any other DXF-compatible software. Additional export options include SVG for web graphics, PNG for images, and PDF for print-ready documents from the layout system.

Paper Space and Layouts

AfroDraft includes a paper space layout system for creating print-ready sheets. Set up viewports at different scales, add title blocks, and compose professional drawing sheets — just like you would in AutoCAD's layout tabs.

Four Professional Themes

Choose between Light, Dark, Blueprint, and High Contrast themes. The Blueprint theme recreates the classic blue-and-white aesthetic of traditional engineering drawings, while Dark mode reduces eye strain during long drafting sessions.

Built for Low-Bandwidth Environments

AfroDraft is optimised for performance on lower-spec devices and slower internet connections common across Africa. Once loaded, the application runs entirely client-side — no server round-trips for drawing operations, no cloud dependency for saving work. Your drawings stay on your device.

Whether you're a first-year engineering student in Lagos, an architect in Nairobi, or a structural engineer in Cape Town, AfroDraft gives you the tools you need to create precise, professional 2D technical drawings — completely free.
