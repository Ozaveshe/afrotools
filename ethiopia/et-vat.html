Ethiopia Ministry of Finance: VAT Proclamation 1341/2024 with schedules

Article 8 sets 0% for Schedule 1 zero-rated supplies and 15% for other taxable supplies. Article 9 requires acceptable documentary proof. The proclamation took effect on approval in August 2024.

Planning estimate only. Confirm classification, evidence, registration, invoice, return and payment requirements with the Ministry of Revenue or a qualified adviser.

Calculation methodology

The calculator adds or extracts the Article 8 standard rate. Zero-rating is available only after the user confirms an exact Schedule 1 match and acceptable Article 9 documentary evidence. Exempt supplies, registration, withholding, filing and transaction classification are not inferred.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Schedule 1 zero-rating requires an exact supply match and acceptable documentary proof.
  • Schedule 2 exemptions are not modeled as zero-rated transactions.
  • Confirm classification, evidence, registration, invoicing, filing and payment duties with the Ministry of Revenue or a qualified adviser.

Test-case examples

Input: Net amount: 1,000 at the displayed standard VAT rate.
Expected: VAT equals 1,000 multiplied by the displayed rate; total equals net amount plus VAT.
Why: Confirms the core VAT-exclusive calculation path and makes the rate dependency auditable.

Input: VAT-inclusive amount with the displayed standard VAT rate.
Expected: Net amount equals total divided by 1 plus the rate; VAT equals total minus net amount.
Why: Confirms reverse VAT handling for invoices that already include tax.
