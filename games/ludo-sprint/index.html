Ready to roll

Roll a six to enter the board. First token to finish wins.

Rules

  • Roll 6 to leave home. After that, every roll moves your token forward.
  • If you land on the computer, its token goes home. Same rule applies to you.
  • First to reach finish wins. Progress and wins stay in this browser only.

Roadmap

Online PvP comes later, after account limits, matchmaking, anti-cheat, abuse controls, and disconnect handling are real.

No gambling, betting, cash rewards, or real-money stakes are part of this game.
