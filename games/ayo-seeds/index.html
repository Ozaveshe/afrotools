A fast seed-sowing game against the computer. Pick a pit, sow the seeds, capture two or three on the computer side, and finish with the bigger harvest.

You are the bottom row. This is a fast learning ruleset inspired by Ayo/Oware-style games, not a claim to cover every regional rule.

When one side is empty, remaining seeds are collected and the higher score wins.

Rules

  • Pick a non-empty pit on your row, then seeds are sown one by one around the board.
  • If your last seed lands on the computer row and that pit has 2 or 3 seeds, you capture them.
  • Computer plays automatically with a simple offline strategy.
