Our Mission: Build the Tools Africa Needs

AfroTools exists because Africa deserves world-class digital tools built for its own regulations, currencies, and daily realities. Most online calculators are designed for the US or Europe and ignore African tax codes, grading systems, and financial structures entirely. We are changing that by building a comprehensive, free toolkit that works for every African country.

Every tool we ship is informed by real user needs. Community suggestions are the single most important input to our roadmap. When multiple people request the same tool, it moves to the top of our build queue.

How Suggestions Get Prioritised

  1. 1Demand: The more people who request the same tool, the higher it ranks. Duplicate requests are our strongest signal.
  2. 2Impact: Tools that serve the most users across the most countries get priority over niche single-country requests.
  3. 3Feasibility: Tools with publicly available data (tax brackets, exchange rates, grading scales) are faster to build and ship.
  4. 4Urgency: If you mark your request as urgent and explain why, we factor that into our timeline.
