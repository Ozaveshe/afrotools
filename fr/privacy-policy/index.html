Section 01

What We Collect

AfroTools collects only what is necessary to operate the platform. We follow a strict minimal data policy — we never collect data we don't need.

  • Email address — only when you voluntarily subscribe to updates or request a PDF report. We never require an account to use any tool.
  • Usage data — anonymised page views via Google Analytics (no personal identifiers linked to browsing behaviour).
  • Calculator inputs — salary and tax inputs are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not store or transmit your financial data to our servers.
  • Contact form submissions — when you use the contact form, your name, email, and message are stored securely by Netlify Forms for the purpose of replying to you.

Section 02

How We Use Your Email

If you submit your email address, we use it only for the specific purpose you agreed to:

  • Sending notifications when new tools launch for your country
  • Alerting you when tax law changes affect a calculator you've used
  • Delivering PDF reports you've requested to be sent by email

We do not sell, rent, or share your email with any third parties. Ever.

ℹ️

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send. Unsubscribes are processed immediately — no 30-day delays.

🍪
Section 03

Cookies

AfroTools uses no advertising cookies and no third-party tracking cookies that identify individual users. We use only:

  • Analytics cookies — via Google Analytics to collect anonymous page view data (country, device type, page visited). No personal identifiers are linked.
  • Functional cookies — small browser storage values used to remember your calculator préférénces within a single session. These are not transmitted to any server.
🔒
Section 04

Your Calculator Data

This is our strongest privacy commitment: all calculations happen locally in your browser using JavaScript.

Your salary figures, tax inputs, health data, and results are never sent to our servers. When you download a PDF report, it is generated entirely in your browser and saved directly to your device — no data passes through our servers.

🛡️

Closing your browser tab deletes all your calculation inputs permanently. We have no record of them.
