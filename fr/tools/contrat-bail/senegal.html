Senegal Tenancy Agreement — Legal Requirements

Tenancy agreements in Senegal are governed by Loi No. 2014-03 portant bail à usage d'habitation au Sénégal. The maximum security deposit is 2 months rent (caution). Landlord notice period: 3 months written notice via huissier for residential. Tenant notice period: 3 months written notice. Disputes are resolved through the Tribunal d'Instance / Commission de Conciliation.

Tenant protection: Strong tenant protection; eviction requires court order; rent tribunal available. Rent increases: Rent controlled in Dakar and regional capitals; increase cannot exceed 5–10% per year. Stamp duty: Yes — enregistrement fee; lease must be registered within 1 month.
