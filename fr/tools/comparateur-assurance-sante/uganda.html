Uganda motor third-party (MTP) covers COMESA zones at UGX 400,000-600,000/year. MTP limits are UGX 1M per person and UGX 10M aggregate. The market has 144 licensed players. Major insurers: Jubilee Allianz, Alliance Africa, APA, Sanlam. The market is regulated by IRA (Assurance Regulatory Authority of Uganda), with insurance penetration at 0.74% of GDP.
