Guinea-Bissau Employment Contract — What the Law Requires

Guinea-Bissau employment contracts are governed by Lei Geral do Trabalho da Guiné-Bissau. All employers are required to provide written terms of employment. Key statutory requirements include: minimum wage of CFA19,030/month, maximum 45 working hours per week, 30 days annual leave, and 9 weeks maternity leave (100% (social security)).

Employers in Guinea-Bissau must make mandatory deductions for INSS (4% employee, 14% employer). The notice period for termination is 1-3 months. Employment disputes are resolved through the Labour Court. Severance pay: Per labour code.
