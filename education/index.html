Free Education Tools for Africa

AfroTools provides free education calculators designed for African students and educators. Our WAEC/NECO Grade Calculator helps Nigerian and Ghanaian secondary school students calculate their aggregate scores, check JAMB and O'Level eligibility for university admission, and convert grades to GPA on Nigerian (5-point) and international (4-point) scales.

The WAEC (West African Examinations Council) and NECO (National Examinations Council) grades use a points system from A1 (1 point, Excellent) to F9 (9 points, Fail). An aggregate of 5 subjects of 20 or less is generally required for university admission in Nigeria. Our tool checks eligibility against actual cut-off marks for universities including University of Lagos, University of Ibadan, Obafemi Awolowo University, Ahmadu Bello University, and others.

For Ghanaian students, our Ghana WASSCE calculator uses the same A1-F9 grading system but calculates based on best 6 subjects, as required by University of Ghana (UG), Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), and University of Cape Coast (UCC). All education tools are free, require no registration, and work on mobile devices.
