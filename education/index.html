Free Education Tools for Africa — 25+ Calculators

AfroTools provides 25+ free education calculators designed specifically for African students, parents and educators. Our grading tools cover WAEC/NECO (Nigeria, Ghana), JAMB aggregate, KCSE (Kenya), South African Matric APS scores and GPA calculators for both the 5.0 and 4.0 scales used across Nigerian and Kenyan universities.

Our financial tools help students plan the real costs of education: school fees comparisons across 54 African countries (public vs private, primary to university), study abroad cost estimates for UK, Canada, Australia, USA and Germany, boarding school costs, student loan repayment schedules for NELFUND (Nigeria), HELB (Kenya), NSFAS (South Africa) and the Ghana Students Loan Trust Fund, and education savings projections.

Country-specific tools include the HELB Repayment Calculator (Kenya), NYSC Allowance Calculator (Nigeria), Ghana National Service Scheme calculator and a Teacher Salary Scale Lookup covering government pay scales for 9 African countries. The Scholarship Eligibility Checker matches your profile against Mastercard Foundation, Chevening, DAAD, Fulbright, Commonwealth and 15+ other scholarships.

Study tools include an Exam Countdown Timer, Flashcard Maker, Citation Generator (APA/MLA/Harvard), Plagiarism Self-Check, Course Load Planner and Exam Timetable Generator. Career tools cover Tutoring Rate benchmarks, Professional Certification ROI (ACCA, PMP, AWS, ICAN) and an African Coding Bootcamp Comparator. All tools are free, require no registration, and work on mobile devices.
