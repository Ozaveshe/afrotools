Ghana Car Import Cost Guide

The Ghana Car Import Cost Calculator separates official taxes from third-party charges, which is important when comparing Tema, Takoradi, and dealer alternatives.

That matters because the number people quote in conversation is usually not the number they finally pay. In Ghana, the customs value, overage effects, statutory levies, and port-side charges can all shift the real landed cost.

What users miss

  • Depreciated manufacturer or first purchase price can drive the customs value.
  • VAT, NHIL, GETFund, AU, ECOWAS, EXIM, examination, and processing fees can all matter.
  • Age overage penalty should be visible before a user commits.
  • Right-hand steering can require special dispensation.
  • Safe bond rent and trade plates belong in the real budget.

Official sources to check before paying

  • GRA: Vehicle Importation and Clearing
  • GRA e-services and ICUMS entry points

Use the estimate for planning and verify the current GRA and ICUMS assessment before clearing.
