Nigeria Car Import Cost Guide

Use the Nigeria Car Landed Cost Calculator when you need a budget that goes beyond duty. Start with purchase price, FOB, CIF, or make/model/year, then add Apapa or Tin Can port costs, clearing, storage risk, inland delivery, registration, and FX sensitivity.

What users miss

  • Terminal handling and release documents.
  • Clearing agent and inspection costs.
  • Demurrage after free storage days.
  • Vehicle policy pack changes around FOB-based charges and levies.
  • Compliance warning for passenger vehicles above 12 years.

The estimate is not an official customs assessment. Save the quote, export a PDF, and confirm the final bill with a licensed clearing agent.
