Uganda Car Import Tax Guide

The Uganda Car Import Tax and Landed Cost Calculator is built around URA valuation-guide friendly data. If the exact official row is missing, it labels the output as an estimate.

What users miss

  • Official valuation can differ from declared invoice price.
  • Mombasa and Dar corridors have different freight and inland delivery assumptions.
  • Environmental levy and other taxes can change the budget for older cars.
  • FX movement matters between quote and clearance.
  • Registration and local transport are separate from customs.

Use the quote as a planning view and confirm current URA assessment values before paying.
