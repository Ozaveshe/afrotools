What Is a WhatsApp Click-to-Chat Link?

A WhatsApp click-to-chat link is a special URL that uses the wa.me domain followed by your phone number in international format. When someone clicks the link on their phone or computer, WhatsApp opens automatically with a new conversation directed to your number. The customer does not need to have your number saved in their contacts.

The basic format looks like this:

https://wa.me/2348012345678

The number must include the country code without any plus signs, spaces, dashes, or brackets. For a Nigerian number like 0801-234-5678, the international format is 2348012345678. For a Kenyan number like 0712-345-678, it becomes 254712345678. For a South African number like 071-234-5678, use 27712345678.

How to Add a Pre-Filled Message

One of the most powerful features of WhatsApp links is the ability to include a pre-typed message. When the customer clicks the link, the message appears in the text field so they only need to tap send. This reduces friction dramatically and also helps you identify which marketing channel generated the enquiry.

To add a pre-filled message, append the text parameter to your link:

https://wa.me/2348012345678?text=Hi%20I%20want%20to%20order%20jollof%20rice

Spaces and special characters need to be URL-encoded. The %20 represents a space. Our WhatsApp Link Generator handles this encoding automatically so you can type your message in plain English and get a properly formatted link.

Pre-Filled Message Ideas for African Businesses

Different business types can benefit from different pre-filled messages. Here are practical examples:

  • E-commerce / Instagram shops: "Hi, I saw your post and I'd like to place an order"
  • Real estate agents: "Hello, I'm interested in the property listing on your website"
  • Restaurants and catering: "Hi, I'd like to see your menu and place an order for delivery"
  • Freelancers and agencies: "Hello, I'd like to get a quote for your services"
  • Schools and training centres: "Hi, I'd like information about your programmes and fees"

Using specific messages for different channels (website, Instagram bio, Facebook page) lets you track where enquiries originate, even without formal analytics.

Add a floating WhatsApp button to your website. This is now standard practice for businesses across Lagos, Nairobi, and Johannesburg. Email Signatures

Add a line to your email signature like "Chat with us on WhatsApp" linked to your wa.me URL. This is especially effective for B2B businesses where email conversations often need a quicker follow-up channel.

WhatsApp Business App vs Regular WhatsApp

While click-to-chat links work with any WhatsApp account, businesses should strongly consider using the free WhatsApp Business app. It provides several features that make managing customer conversations much easier:

  • Business profile: Display your address, business hours, website, and a description
  • Quick replies: Save and reuse frequent responses with keyboard shortcuts
  • Labels: Organise conversations by category (new customer, pending payment, completed order)
  • Catalogue: Showcase products with images, prices, and descriptions inside WhatsApp
  • Away messages: Auto-reply when you are not available
  • Greeting messages: Automatically welcome first-time customers

The WhatsApp Business app is free and available on Android and iOS. It uses a separate phone number from your personal WhatsApp, which is important for maintaining work-life boundaries.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

When setting up your WhatsApp link, watch out for these frequent errors that prevent the link from working:

  • Including the plus sign: Use 2348012345678 not +2348012345678
  • Including spaces or dashes: The number must be digits only with no formatting characters
  • Using the local number format: Use the full international format including country code
  • Forgetting URL encoding: Spaces in your pre-filled message must be encoded as %20 or +
  • Linking to a number without WhatsApp: Make sure the number you use has WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business installed and active

If your business has separate numbers for sales, support, and orders, create a different WhatsApp link for each. Use distinct pre-filled messages so staff can immediately see which department the customer intended to reach.

Many African businesses rely on Instagram as their primary storefront. Instead of choosing between your website link and your WhatsApp link in your bio, create a link-in-bio page that includes both, along with your other important links. Frequently Asked Questions

Is a WhatsApp click-to-chat link free to create?

Yes. WhatsApp click-to-chat links use the official wa.me domain and cost nothing to generate. You can create one manually or use a free tool like our WhatsApp Link Generator. There are no monthly fees or subscriptions required.

Do customers need to save my number to use a wa.me link?

No. That is the main advantage of click-to-chat links. When someone clicks the link, WhatsApp opens a conversation with your number directly, without the customer needing to save your contact first.

Can I add a pre-filled message to my WhatsApp link?

Yes. Append a text parameter to your wa.me link. The message will appear pre-typed in the chat box so the customer only needs to tap send. The format is wa.me/number?text=Your%20message.

Does the WhatsApp link work on both mobile and desktop?

Yes. On mobile devices, the link opens the WhatsApp app directly. On desktop computers, it opens WhatsApp Web or the desktop app if installed. The link works across Android, iOS, Windows, and Mac.

Can I track how many people click my WhatsApp link?

WhatsApp itself does not provide click analytics. However, you can use a URL shortener like Bitly or a UTM-tagged redirect to track clicks before they reach WhatsApp. This gives you data on how many people initiated a conversation through each marketing channel.
