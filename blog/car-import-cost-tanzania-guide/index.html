Tanzania Car Import Cost Guide

The Tanzania Car Import Cost Calculator separates the customs/tax stage from the first-registration stage, which keeps the Dar es Salaam import budget clearer.

What users miss

  • Used passenger vehicles can trigger age-based excise for 8 to 10 years and over 10 years.
  • Registration tax, licence fee by cubic capacity, and first-registration levies are not the same as customs charges.
  • Storage, clearing, and inland delivery beyond Dar es Salaam can change the on-road total.
  • FX movement between quote and clearance needs a buffer.
  • Motorcycle-specific branches can be added through the same rule engine later.

Use the estimate for planning and verify the latest TRA classification before paying.
