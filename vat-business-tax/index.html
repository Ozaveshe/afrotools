VAT & Business Tax Tools for African Entrepreneurs

Running a business in Africa means checking country rules, filing periods, invoice treatment, withholding rules, import duties, zero-rated supplies, exemptions, and source dates before issuing final documents.

The VAT & Business Tax hub gets you from a business question to the right workflow quickly: price the sale, prepare the invoice, check VAT/WHT assumptions, estimate margin, or collect source notes before filing.

For SMEs and finance teams: Use calculators for VAT-inclusive and VAT-exclusive amounts, profit margin, markup, break-even, invoices, and receipts. Keep customer data, invoice lines, uploaded files, and raw tax documents out of the hub; enter details only inside the tool that needs them.
