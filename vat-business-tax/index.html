    FAQ

    VAT & business tax questions

    Common questions about how the calculators work and how current the rates are.

    Which countries and VAT rates does this hub cover?
    The VAT calculator covers all 54 African markets. Standard VAT and GST rates range from 0% to 20%. Somalia, Eritrea, and Libya have no operational VAT system, so they correctly show 0%. Liberia and Sierra Leone use GST rather than VAT.
    How do I add VAT versus extract (reverse) VAT?
    Add VAT multiplies a net (VAT-exclusive) amount by the rate and adds it to the total. Extract VAT works backwards from a VAT-inclusive price: net = total divided by (1 + rate), and the VAT is the difference. For example, at 16% VAT, adding to 1,000 gives 1,160; extracting from 1,160 gives net 1,000 and VAT 160.
    What are the VAT rates in Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, and Ghana?
    Nigeria charges 7.5%, Kenya 16%, and South Africa 15%. Ghana levies 15% VAT plus a 2.5% NHIL and 2.5% GETFund, which the calculator shows as a 20% effective charge. Always confirm the current rate with the national revenue authority before invoicing.
    What is a VAT registration threshold?
    It is the annual taxable turnover above which a business must register for VAT and start charging it - for example around KES 5 million in Kenya or 25 million naira in Nigeria. The hub shows thresholds for context only; confirm your obligation with the tax authority or an adviser, as thresholds change with each finance act.
    Are these VAT rates official and up to date?
    The rates are planning-grade and were last reviewed against national revenue-authority guidance on 3 May 2026. VAT rates, thresholds, and exemptions change with annual budgets, so recheck the current figure with the relevant authority before you file a return or issue a final invoice.
    Does this hub store my invoice or customer data?
    No. The business route planner saves route metadata only - no invoice lines, customer records, or tax documents. Enter transaction details only inside the specific calculator that needs them.
