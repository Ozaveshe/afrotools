Calculation methodology

The calculator applies 15% GST in SLE. A zero-rated result requires exact current First Schedule evidence; an exempt result requires exact current Second Schedule evidence. Compulsory registration is screened at NLe 500,000 over the past or expected next 12 months, or one third over the past 4 months. Voluntary registration remains at the Commissioner-General's discretion.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Finance Acts can amend the First and Second Schedules, so exact current transaction evidence is required.
  • No automatic voluntary-registration approval, filing, deduction, refund or invoice-compliance decision is modeled.
  • Confirm filing, registration, and remittance duties with the official authority or a qualified adviser before submission.

Test-case examples

Input: SLE 1,000 net at the standard GST rate.
Expected: GST is SLE 150 and the gross amount is SLE 1,150.
Why: Confirms the 15% GST-exclusive calculation in the current currency.

Input: Past-four-month taxable supplies of NLe 166,666.66 and then NLe 166,666.67.
Expected: The first remains below compulsory registration; the second meets the exact NLe 500,000 divided-by-three threshold.
Why: Protects the statutory one-third boundary without silently rounding it down.
