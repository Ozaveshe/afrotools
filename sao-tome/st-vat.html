Calculation methodology

The local engine adds or extracts the enacted 15% standard rate. It enables 7.5% only after explicit confirmation of an exact Annex I basic-basket line and retained invoice evidence. It does not infer exemptions, exports, registration, deduction, filing or invoice compliance. The official IVA manual's illustrative 2025 example using 16% is disclosed but is not modeled because no primary enactment changing the rate was found as checked 2026-07-23.

Known limitations

  • Informational estimate only. It is not professional tax, legal, payroll, or filing advice.
  • Only the 15% standard rate and evidence-gated 7.5% Annex I treatment are modeled.
  • The official manual's illustrative 16% example is not treated as enacted law.
  • Exemptions, exports, registration, deductions, filing and invoice compliance are not modeled.
  • Confirm filing, registration, and remittance duties with the official authority or a qualified adviser before submission.
